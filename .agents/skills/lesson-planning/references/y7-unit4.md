### Grammar Notes

- 两 vs 二: use 两点 for 2:00, NOT 二点
- 差 means "to" in time expressions: 差X分Y点 = X minutes to Y o'clock
- 零 is required before single-digit minutes: 六点零五分, NOT 六点五分

### Key Sentence Patterns

**Questions:**
- 你怎么上学？
- 你爸爸怎么上班？

**Answers:**
- 我坐校车上学。
- 我走路上学。
- 妈妈开车上班。
- 我每天坐公共汽车上学。

**Negation:**
- 我妈妈不工作。

### Grammar Notes

- 坐 + vehicle = travel by that vehicle: 坐飞机, 坐地铁, 坐校车
- 走路 = walk; no 坐 needed
- 开车 = drive a car
- 每 + time word = every: 每天, 每年, 每个星期
- 怎么 asks about method or manner

## Workbook Exercises (轻松学中文 1 Workbook)

Workbook 1, Unit 4 (book pp.98–133) supplies the written practice/homework for Lessons 10–12, plus a unit revision and unit test. Each lesson has ~20 exercises. Most textbook activities are oral; use the workbook to assign homework and vary practice with writing-focused tasks.

### Recurring exercise types (all three lessons)

- **Radical / stroke practice**: copy the lesson's 6 radicals; write new-word characters; stroke-count tasks; find characters for a given radical, write the radical for a given character, write a radical's meaning
- **Pinyin & meaning**: write pinyin for words/phrases (recycles prior-unit vocab — 工作, 商人, 独生女, 秘书, 律师, 电话号码); write characters from pinyin; write character + pinyin from an English meaning
- **Phrase building**: fill in a missing character/word to form a phrase; add a verb to form a phrase
- **Word-search grids**: circle target phrases, then write them out
- **Production**: complete dialogues from a given B-line or picture prompt; sentence rearrangement; reading-comprehension passage + questions; E→C translation

### Per-lesson highlights

**L10 Time (pp.98–107, 22 ex.)** — Clock work dominates: draw hands for given times; read times off analogue / animal-shaped clocks (incl. 差 forms, e.g. 差一刻六点); E→C translation (quarter past ten / five to six / now / grade nine / only daughter); reading comp (Wang Shuwen nationality/family). Radicals 土又刀米彡衤.

**L11 Daily Routine (pp.108–117, 20 ex.)** — Time conversion: digital → Chinese with AM/PM context (14:45 → 下午…); write dates in Chinese; odd-one-out / circle words unrelated to routine; match routine pictures to phrases (起床/吃早饭/上学/上课/放学/睡觉) and put clock hands on routine sentences; reading comp (王大中's day) + open production ("introduce your own daily routine"). Radicals 穴矢页扌艹走.

**L12 Transport (pp.118–127, 20 ex.)** — Add a verb to phrases (__床/__学/__车/__课); sentence rearrangement (妈妈每天走路上班); antonym pairs (上班/下班, 上车/下车, 每天/每年); match vehicle pictures to words (火车/电车/公共汽车/出租车/地铁/校车); spot-the-stroke-mistake correction (律/秘/睡/两); sort phrases by category (vehicles/routine/people/time); reading comp (王小天, dad drives to work). - **Revision (pp.128–129)**: consolidates clock times (incl. 差), time-of-day words, routine verbs, question words 几点/怎么, vehicles, all three radical groups, plus 10 model Q&A pairs across time/routine/transport.

### Common Student Errors

- 二点 instead of 两点 (两 is required before 点)
- Forgetting 零 before single-digit minutes: 三点零五分, NOT 三点五分
- Word order: 坐 + vehicle + activity, NOT activity + 坐 + vehicle
- Confusing 上午 and 早上: both can mean "morning" but cover different time ranges (早上 5:00–8:00; 上午 before noon)
- Confusing 上/下 pairs: 上班/下班, 上课/下课, 上学/放学, 上车/下车
- Mixing time-period words: 早上 vs 上午; 下午 vs 晚上
