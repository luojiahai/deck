## Grammar Progression Map

```
L1-L2: 喜欢 + V          ──→  Foundation: expressing preferences
L3-L4: 正在 + V          ──→  Progressive aspect: actions in progress
L5:    A、B 都...        ──→  Inclusive adverb: "both/all"
L6:    一边 A 一边 B      ──→  Simultaneous actions
L7:    Integrated review  ──→  All patterns in authentic dialogue
L8:    Consolidation      ──→  Summative application
```

### Grammar Detail

**1. 正在 + Verb (Progressive Aspect) — L3-L4**
- Structure: Subject + 正在 + Verb (+ Object)
- Meaning: Action is happening right now
- Contrast with: Subject + Verb (habitual/general)
- Question: Subject + 正在 + 做什么?
- Examples:
  - 他正在唱歌。 Tā zhèngzài chàng gē. (He is singing.)
  - 他们正在上课。 Tāmen zhèngzài shàng kè. (They are in class.)
  - 妈妈正在听音乐。 Māma zhèngzài tīng yīnyuè. (Mum is listening to music.)

**2. 都 (Both/All) — L5**
- Structure: Item A、Item B + 都 + Verb/Adj
- Meaning: Both A and B (or all items in a list)
- Uses enumeration comma (、) not 和
- Examples:
  - 英语、汉语，我都会说。 Yīngyǔ, Hànyǔ, wǒ dōu huì shuō.
  - 弹钢琴、弹吉他，他都会。 Tán gāngqín, tán jítā, tā dōu huì.
  - 唱歌、听音乐，我都喜欢。 Chàng gē, tīng yīnyuè, wǒ dōu xǐhuān.

**3. 一边...一边... (Simultaneous Actions) — L6**
- Structure: Subject + 一边 + Action A + 一边 + Action B
- Meaning: Doing A and B at the same time
- Both actions must be simultaneous (not sequential)
- Often combined with 有时候 for "sometimes"
- Examples:
  - 他有时候一边听音乐一边看书。 Tā yǒu shíhou yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān kàn shū.
  - 我一边唱歌一边弹钢琴。 Wǒ yībiān chàng gē yībiān tán gāngqín.

## Lesson 1: 你的爱好是什么? — Introducing Hobbies

### Lesson Overview
- **Topic**: Introduction to hobbies and music vocabulary
- **Duration**: 50 minutes
- **Curriculum Links**: VCCCC068 (Socialising), VCCCC071 (Translating), VCCCC074 (Systems of language)
- **Learning Intention**: We are learning to talk about our hobbies in Chinese
- **Success Criteria**:
  - I can say 5 hobby words in Chinese with correct tones
  - I can ask "What is your hobby?" in Chinese
  - I can answer "My hobby is..." in Chinese
  - I can write the characters 爱, 音, 乐 with correct stroke order
- **Prior Knowledge**: Students can read pinyin, know basic self-introduction (我叫..., 我是...)

### Key Vocabulary (7 items)
| 汉字 | Pinyin | English |
|------|--------|---------|
| 爱好 | àihào | hobby |
| 音乐 | yīnyuè | music |
| 唱歌 | chàng gē | to sing |
| 听音乐 | tīng yīnyuè | to listen to music |
| 弹钢琴 | tán gāngqín | to play piano |
| 读书 | dúshū | to read (study) |
| 看书 | kàn shū | to read (books) |

### Language Structures
- 你的爱好是什么? — Nǐ de àihào shì shénme? (What is your hobby?)
- 我的爱好是... — Wǒ de àihào shì... (My hobby is...)
- 你喜欢做什么? — Nǐ xǐhuān zuò shénme? (What do you like to do?)
- 我喜欢... — Wǒ xǐhuān... (I like...)

### Lesson Sequence

**Warm-up (5 min)**
Picture-guessing: Teacher shows images of people singing, playing piano, reading. Students call out in English. Teacher asks: "How do we say these in Chinese? By the end of today, you'll know."

**Core Teaching (20 min)**
1. Introduce vocabulary via flashcards: 音乐, 唱歌, 听音乐, 弹钢琴, 读书, 看书. Each word shown with image, characters, pinyin, English. Teacher models pronunciation. Students repeat chorally then individually.
2. Teach 爱好: 你的爱好是什么? 我的爱好是唱歌。Drill the question-answer pattern.
3. Teach 喜欢 + activity: 你喜欢做什么? 我喜欢听音乐。
4. Contrast 读书 (study/read aloud) vs 看书 (read silently).

**Guided Practice (15 min)**
- Pair work: Students ask each other 你的爱好是什么? and answer using vocabulary cards as prompts
- Teacher circulates, checks pronunciation, corrects tones
- Extension: Chain drill — student A answers, then asks student B

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
Exit ticket: Each student writes on a mini whiteboard: 我的爱好是______。 (fill in one Chinese word from today). Teacher scans before dismissal.

**Resources**: Flashcards (physical or on slides), mini whiteboards, markers
**Homework**: Practise writing 爱, 音, 乐 (5 times each), review vocabulary with Quizlet

## Lesson 2: 你喜欢什么音乐? — Music Preferences & Activities

### Lesson Overview
- **Topic**: Types of music, expressing preferences, listing activities
- **Duration**: 50 minutes
- **Curriculum Links**: VCCCC068 (Socialising), VCCCC071 (Translating), VCCCC074 (Systems of language)
- **Learning Intention**: We are learning to describe what kinds of music we like
- **Success Criteria**:
  - I can name 2 types of music in Chinese
  - I can ask and answer "What music do you like?"
  - I can use 和 to list two activities I like
  - I can write the characters 唱 and 歌 with correct stroke order

### Key Vocabulary (4 new items + review)
| 汉字 | Pinyin | English | Note |
|------|--------|---------|------|
| 弹吉他 | tán jítā | to play guitar | from p.72 context |
| 流行音乐 | liúxíng yīnyuè | pop music | extension |
| 古典音乐 | gǔdiǎn yīnyuè | classical music | extension |
| 歌 | gē | song | component of 唱歌 |

### Language Structures
- 你喜欢什么音乐? — Nǐ xǐhuān shénme yīnyuè? (What music do you like?)
- 我喜欢流行音乐。— Wǒ xǐhuān liúxíng yīnyuè. (I like pop music.)
- 我喜欢唱歌和弹钢琴。— Wǒ xǐhuān chàng gē hé tán gāngqín. (I like singing and playing piano.)

### Lesson Sequence

**Warm-up (5 min)**
Quick-fire recall: Teacher flashes L1 vocab images. Students write the Chinese (pinyin OK) on mini whiteboards. Self-check against answers on screen. Goal: activate prior knowledge.

**Core Teaching (18 min)**
1. Introduce 弹吉他 (from p.72), 流行音乐, 古典音乐. Audio clips: play 10-second excerpts of pop and classical music. Students identify which is which.
2. Teach 你喜欢什么音乐? pattern. Model with 流行音乐 and 古典音乐.
3. Introduce 和 for listing two activities: 我喜欢唱歌和弹钢琴。
4. Preview tomorrow's concept: show a picture of someone singing RIGHT NOW vs. generally. "Next lesson we'll learn how to say 'He IS singing right now' — but today, we talk about what we like generally."

**Guided Practice (17 min)**
- Listening bingo: 4×4 bingo grid with activities. Teacher calls out sentences (e.g., 我喜欢唱歌和弹吉他). Students mark matching squares.
- Pair survey: Students ask 3 classmates 你喜欢什么音乐? and record answers in a table. Report back: "Tom 喜欢流行音乐。"

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
Exit ticket: Write on mini whiteboard: 我喜欢______和______。(fill with two activities in Chinese)

## Lesson 3: 他正在唱歌 — 正在 Progressive Aspect

### Lesson Overview
- **Topic**: The progressive aspect — describing actions happening right now
- **Duration**: 50 minutes
- **Curriculum Links**: VCCCC069 (Informing), VCCCC074 (Systems of language)
- **Learning Intention**: We are learning to say what someone is doing right now in Chinese
- **Success Criteria**:
  - I can explain what 正在 means in English
  - I can form a sentence with 正在 + verb correctly
  - I can tell the difference between "I sing" and "I am singing" in Chinese
  - I can write the characters 正 and 在 with correct stroke order

### Key Vocabulary
| 汉字 | Pinyin | English | Note |
|------|--------|---------|------|
| 正在 | zhèngzài | (progressive aspect marker) | Grammar focus |
| 做 | zuò | to do | 做什么 = do what |
| 什么 | shénme | what | Review |

### Language Structures
- Subject + 正在 + Verb (+ Object) — action in progress RIGHT NOW
- Subject + Verb (+ Object) — habitual/general action
- Contrast pairs:
  - 他唱歌。 Tā chàng gē. (He sings. — general)
  - 他正在唱歌。 Tā zhèngzài chàng gē. (He is singing. — right now)

### Lesson Sequence

**Warm-up (5 min)**
"Right now vs. Usually": Teacher acts out two scenarios — (1) miming singing while standing still = "I sing", (2) actually singing = "I am singing". Students describe the difference in English. Bridge: "In Chinese, we use 正在 to show something is happening RIGHT NOW."

**Core Teaching (20 min)**
1. Explicit teaching: 正在 + Verb. Write on board: 我唱歌 vs 我正在唱歌。Draw timeline: past → NOW (正在) → future.
2. Textbook p.70 content: Show the picture prompt — 他正在唱歌。Students repeat.
3. Additional examples with images:
   - 妈妈正在听音乐。 Māma zhèngzài tīng yīnyuè.
   - 弟弟正在弹钢琴。 Dìdi zhèngzài tán gāngqín.
   - 老师正在看书。 Lǎoshī zhèngzài kàn shū.
4. Key rule: 正在 always goes before the verb. Never after. Never at the end. Show common error: *他唱歌正在* (WRONG).

**Guided Practice (15 min)**
- "正在 or not?" — Teacher shows image + sentence. Students hold up GREEN card if 正在 is correct, RED card if not. (Include distractor sentences where 正在 doesn't make sense, e.g., "I like music" — *我正在喜欢音乐* is wrong.)
- Textbook character stroke counting game (p.70): Two teams. Teacher shows a character. Teams race to count strokes and write the number on mini whiteboards. Correct answer = 1 point. Characters: 唱 (11), 歌 (14), 弹 (11), 钢 (9), 琴 (12), 正 (5), 在 (6).

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
Exit ticket: "Look around the classroom. Write one sentence: ______正在______。" Students observe and write what someone is doing right now.

## Lesson 4: 他们正在做什么? — 正在 Dialogue Practice

## Lesson 5: 我都会! — 都 for "Both/All"

### Lesson Overview
- **Topic**: Using 都 to express "both" and "all" (textbook p.72 top)
- **Duration**: 50 minutes
- **Curriculum Links**: VCCCC068 (Socialising), VCCCC070 (Creating), VCCCC074 (Systems of language)
- **Learning Intention**: We are learning to use 都 to say "both" or "all" in Chinese
- **Success Criteria**:
  - I can use 、(enumeration comma) to list items in Chinese
  - I can form sentences with A、B 都 + Verb
  - I can write 3 sentences using 都 about my own skills
  - I can write the characters 都 and 会 with correct stroke order

### Key Vocabulary
| 汉字 | Pinyin | English |
|------|--------|---------|
| 都 | dōu | both; all |
| 会 | huì | can (learned skill) |

### Language Structures (from p.72)
- A、B 都 + Verb: 英语、汉语，我都会说。 Yīngyǔ, Hànyǔ, wǒ dōu huì shuō.
- 弹钢琴、弹吉他，他都会。 Tán gāngqín, tán jítā, tā dōu huì.
- 唱歌、听音乐，我都喜欢。 Chàng gē, tīng yīnyuè, wǒ dōu xǐhuān.
- Key rule: Use 、(enumeration comma), NOT 和, before 都.

### Lesson Sequence

**Warm-up (5 min)**
Character quiz: Write 8 characters from L1-L4 from pinyin prompts on mini whiteboards. Self-check. (爱, 音, 乐, 唱, 歌, 正, 在, 画)

**Core Teaching (15 min)**
1. Textbook p.72 top content: Show the model sentences. 英语、汉语，我都会说。弹钢琴、弹吉他，他都会。
2. Teach 、vs 和: 、is for listing items in a series (especially before 都), 和 connects two items. Show: "我喜欢唱歌和弹钢琴" vs "唱歌、弹钢琴，我都喜欢" — different structures for different purposes.
3. 会 vs 喜欢: 会 = can (learned skill), 喜欢 = like. Compare: 我会弹钢琴 (I can play piano) vs 我喜欢弹钢琴 (I like playing piano).

**Guided Practice (18 min)**
- Sentence building: Students receive word cards (activities + names + 都 + 会/喜欢). Arrange into correct sentences. Write on mini whiteboards. Teacher checks.
- Skill survey: Students interview 3 classmates — "你会什么? (What can you do?)" Record answers. Write sentences using 都: "Tom 唱歌、弹钢琴，他都会。"

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
Exit ticket: Write 3 sentences about YOURSELF using 都. Template: "______、______，我都会/都喜欢。"

## Lesson 6: 一边听音乐一边看书 — Simultaneous Actions

### Lesson Overview
- **Topic**: Expressing simultaneous actions with 一边...一边... (textbook p.72 bottom)
- **Duration**: 50 minutes
- **Curriculum Links**: VCCCC069 (Informing), VCCCC070 (Creating), VCCCC074 (Systems of language)
- **Learning Intention**: We are learning to say that someone is doing two things at the same time in Chinese
- **Success Criteria**:
  - I can explain what 一边...一边... means in English
  - I can form a sentence with 一边 A 一边 B correctly
  - I can combine 有时候 + 一边...一边... in one sentence
  - I can write the characters 边 and 看 with correct stroke order

### Key Vocabulary
| 汉字 | Pinyin | English |
|------|--------|---------|
| 一边...一边... | yībiān... yībiān... | (simultaneous action marker) |
| 有时候 | yǒu shíhou | sometimes |
| 好几个小时 | hǎo jǐ ge xiǎoshí | several hours |

### Language Structures (from p.72)
- Subject + 一边 + Action A + 一边 + Action B
- 他有时候一边听音乐一边看书。 Tā yǒu shíhou yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān kàn shū.
- 我一边唱歌一边弹钢琴。 Wǒ yībiān chàng gē yībiān tán gāngqín.
- Extension with 好几个小时: 他一边听音乐一边看书，看了好几个小时。

### Lesson Sequence

**Warm-up (5 min)**
"Can you do two things at once?" — Show 4 scenarios. Students vote: possible or impossible?
1. Listen to music + read a book (possible → 一边...一边...)
2. Sleep + run (impossible!)
3. Sing + play piano (possible)
4. Eat + swim (impossible/dangerous!)
Bridge: "Today we learn how to say 'doing A while doing B' in Chinese."

**Core Teaching (18 min)**
1. Introduce 一边...一边... with the textbook example: 他有时候一边听音乐一边看书。
2. Break down structure: 一边 [action 1] 一边 [action 2]. Both actions happen AT THE SAME TIME.
3. Combine with 有时候 for "sometimes": 有时候 + 一边...一边...
4. Brief mention of 好几个小时: "several hours" — appears in the textbook context. 他一边听音乐一边看书，看了好几个小时。(He read while listening to music for several hours.)
5. Key rule: Both actions must be possible simultaneously. You CANNOT say *我一边睡觉一边跑步*.

**Guided Practice (17 min)**
- Drawing + caption activity (from p.72): Each student draws a picture of someone doing two things at once. Below the drawing, write a caption using 一边...一边...
- Gallery walk: Students display drawings. Class walks around, reads captions, votes on "Best 一边...一边... Art" with sticky dots.

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
Exit ticket: "What do YOU sometimes do at the same time? Write one sentence: 我有时候一边______一边______。"

## Lesson 8: 中国乐器与复习 — Chinese Instruments, Culture & Unit Review

### Lesson Overview
- **Topic**: Chinese musical instruments, cultural comparison, and unit summative assessment
- **Duration**: 50 minutes
- **Curriculum Links**: VCCCC068 (Socialising), VCCCC070 (Creating), VCCCC072 (Reflecting), VCCCC074 (Systems of language)
- **Learning Intention**: We are learning about Chinese musical instruments and showing what we've learned in this unit
- **Success Criteria**:
  - I can name 4 Chinese musical instruments in Chinese
  - I can compare Chinese and Western instruments in simple Chinese
  - I can correctly use 正在, 都, and 一边...一边... in sentences
  - I can demonstrate my learning in the unit test

### Key Vocabulary (Cultural Extension)
| 汉字 | Pinyin | English | Instrument Type |
|------|--------|---------|-----------------|
| 古筝 | gǔzhēng | guzheng | Plucked zither (21 strings) |
| 二胡 | èrhú | erhu | Two-stringed fiddle |
| 琵琶 | pípá | pipa | Pear-shaped lute |
| 笛子 | dízi | dizi | Bamboo flute |

### Language Structures (review + cultural comparison)
- 古筝有很多弦。 Gǔzhēng yǒu hěn duō xián. (The guzheng has many strings.)
- 二胡有两根弦。 Erhú yǒu liǎng gēn xián. (The erhu has two strings.)
- 我喜欢古筝，也喜欢吉他。两种乐器我都会。 (I like guzheng and guitar. I can play both instruments.)
- This is a 古筝。它看起来像... (It looks like...)

### Lesson Sequence

**Warm-up (5 min)**
Sound quiz: Play 10-second audio clips of 4 instruments (古筝, 二胡, 琵琶, 笛子). Students guess: "Which one is which?" — matching game with instrument names on the board. Quick reveal with images.

**Core Teaching (15 min)**
1. Introduce the 4 instruments with images and audio. Each: name, pinyin, basic description in simple Chinese.
2. Cultural comparison: "Which Western instruments are similar?" (古筝 ≈ harp/zither, 二胡 ≈ violin, 琵琶 ≈ lute/guitar, 笛子 ≈ flute). Students discuss briefly in pairs.
3. Intercultural reflection: "Why might different cultures develop different instruments? What does music tell us about a culture?" Quick think-pair-share.
4. Grammar quick-review: One rapid-fire example each of 正在, 都, 一边...一边... using instruments as context.

**Summative Assessment (25 min)**
Four-part unit test (see Assessment Map above):
- **Section A — Listening** (5 min, 5 marks): Teacher reads 5 sentences. Students match each to the correct picture on the answer sheet.
- **Section B — Reading** (7 min, 6 marks): Read a short paragraph about someone's hobbies and answer 3 comprehension questions in English.
- **Section C — Writing** (8 min, 10 marks): Write 5 sentences about hobbies. Must use 正在, 都, and 一边...一边... at least once each.
- **Section D — Characters** (5 min, 5 marks): Write characters from pinyin prompts.

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
Test collection. Preview next unit. Celebration: "You've learned so much in these 8 lessons! Next unit: 爱好(二) — more hobbies!"
