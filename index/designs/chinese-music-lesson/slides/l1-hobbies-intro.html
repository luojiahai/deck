Lesson 1

对话 · Dialogue

duì huà
A
你有什么爱好？
Nǐ yǒu shénme àihào?
What hobbies do you have?
B
我喜欢唱歌和跳舞。
Wǒ xǐhuān chànggē hé tiàowǔ.
I like singing and dancing.
A
你喜欢听音乐吗？
Nǐ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè ma?
Do you like listening to music?
B
喜欢！我喜欢听音乐。
Xǐhuān! Wǒ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè.
Yes! I like listening to music.
Teacher: Read together (whole class), then in two groups (A/B), then pairs. Swap roles.

Lesson 1

句型 · Pattern

jù xíng
New word: 会 (huì) — can / know how to
你会________吗？
Nǐ huì ________ ma?
Can you ________? / Do you know how to ________?
✅ Yes
会！我会弹钢琴。
Huì! Wǒ huì tán gāngqín.
Yes! I can play the piano.
❌ No
不会。我不会弹钢琴。
Bú huì. Wǒ bú huì tán gāngqín.
No. I canʼt play the piano.
唱歌 跳舞 弹钢琴 弹吉他
Teacher: 会 is different from 喜欢 — 会 = ability (can you?), 喜欢 = preference (do you like?). Drill both: 「你喜欢...吗?」vs「你会...吗?」
