有时候 + 一边...一边...

Yǒu shíhou — Sometimes
有时候 = sometimes
yǒu shíhou
Subject + 有时候 + 一边 A 一边 B
📚🎧
我有时候一边听音乐一边做功课。
Wǒ yǒu shíhou yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān zuò gōngkè.
有 (yǒu = have) + 时候 (shíhou = time) → "have times when" = sometimes

Extension: 好几个小时

Kuòzhǎn · Extension (not required, but useful!)
✦ Extension Vocabulary
好几个小时
hǎo jǐ ge xiǎoshí = several hours
⏱️
他一边听音乐一边看书，看了好几个小时。
Tā yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān kàn shū, kàn le hǎo jǐ ge xiǎoshí.
好 here = "quite" or "a good" (colloquial), not "good"
好几 + measure word = "quite a few" / "several"

写字: 边 (biān)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
边
Radical 辶 (chuò) — walk / movement
Strokes 5 strokes
Pinyin biān
English side; while (in 一边)
Components 力 (lì) + 辶 (chuò)
边 is simplified from 邊 (biān).
一边 yībiān = on one side / while
旁边 pángbiān = beside / next to
✋ Air-trace the character with your finger!

写字: 看 (kàn)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
看
Radical 目 (mù) — eye
Strokes 9 strokes
Pinyin kàn
English to look at; to read
Components 手 (shǒu, modified) + 目 (mù)
Mnemonic: "Hand above eyes — looking into the distance."
看书 kàn shū = to read (silently)
看电视 kàn diànshì = to watch TV
✋ Air-trace the character with your finger!
