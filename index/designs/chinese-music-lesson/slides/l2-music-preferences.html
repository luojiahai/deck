Key Pattern — Duration of an Action

Dòngzuò de shíjiān chángdù · How long an action lasts
Subject + Verb + Duration + 的 + Object
他每天打一个小时的电话。
Tā měitiān dǎ yī gè xiǎoshí de diànhuà.
He makes phone calls for one hour every day.
打 + 一个小时 + 的 + 电话
verb   duration   的   object

💡 的 connects the duration to the object — "one hour OF phone calls"

Duration Examples

Lìjù · Example sentences
1 我每天写三十分钟的汉字。 30 分钟 Wǒ měitiān xiě sānshí fēnzhōng de Hànzì. I write characters for 30 min every day.
2 她每天弹一个半小时的吉他。 1.5 小时 Tā měitiān tán yī gè bàn xiǎoshí de jítā. She plays guitar for 1.5 hours every day.
3 我每天睡八个小时的觉。 8 小时 Wǒ měitiān shuì bā gè xiǎoshí de jiào. I sleep for 8 hours every day.
4 他每天看四十五分钟的书。 45 分钟 Tā měitiān kàn sìshíwǔ fēnzhōng de shū. He reads for 45 minutes every day.

💡 Notice: 的 + object comes AFTER the duration — "30 minutes OF writing characters"

一边...一边...

Yībiān...yībiān... · Doing two things at the same time
Subject + 一边 + Action A + 一边 + Action B
一边听音乐一边写字
yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān xiězì
listen to music while writing characters
一边吃饭一边看书
yībiān chīfàn yībiān kàn shū
eat while reading a book
一边唱歌一边弹吉他
yībiān chàng gē yībiān tán jítā
sing while playing guitar

💡 一边 = "one side" — you're doing action A on one side and action B on the other!

Combined — Duration + 一边...一边...

Hé qǐlái · Putting it all together
1 我每天一边听音乐一边写一个小时的汉字。 Wǒ měitiān yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān xiě yī gè xiǎoshí de Hànzì. Every day I write characters for 1 hour while listening to music.
2 他每天一边吃饭一边看十五分钟的书。 Tā měitiān yībiān chīfàn yībiān kàn shíwǔ fēnzhōng de shū. Every day he reads for 15 minutes while eating.
3 她每天一边唱歌一边弹一个半小时的吉他。 Tā měitiān yībiān chàng gē yībiān tán yī gè bàn xiǎoshí de jítā. Every day she plays guitar for 1.5 hours while singing.

💡 一边...一边... goes BEFORE the verb phrase · Duration + 的 + Object stays at the end

写字: 写 (xiě)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
写
Radical 宀 (mián) — roof
Strokes 5 strokes
Pinyin xiě
English to write
写字 xiězì = to write characters
写汉字 xiě Hànzì = to write Chinese characters

写字: 睡 (shuì)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
睡
Radical 目 (mù) — eye
Strokes 13 strokes
Pinyin shuì
English to sleep
睡觉 shuìjiào = to sleep
目 (eye) + 垂 (to hang down) = sleepy eyes!
