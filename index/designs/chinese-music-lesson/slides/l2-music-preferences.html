Grammar

和 for Listing

和 = and (for nouns and activities)
Use 和 to connect two things you like — NOT for connecting sentences
1 唱歌和弹钢琴 chàng gē hé tán gāngqín singing and playing piano
2 流行音乐和古典音乐 liúxíng yīnyuè hé gǔdiǎn yīnyuè pop music and classical music
3 听音乐和看书 tīng yīnyuè hé kàn shū listening to music and reading
⚠️ 和 connects nouns/verb phrases only — NOT for connecting sentences!
❌ 我喜欢唱歌和我喜欢弹钢琴 → ✅ 我喜欢唱歌和弹钢琴

Character Writing

写汉字 · 唱

唱
11 strokes
✋ Air-trace here
口 (mouth) + 昌 (chāng) — makes sense for singing!
Pinyin chàng
English to sing
Radical 口 (kǒu) — mouth
Strokes 11
Mnemonic
口 (mouth) on the left + 昌 (prosperous) on the right = singing makes your mouth prosperous!
Stroke Order
竖、横折、横、竖、横折、横、横、竖、横折、横、横

Character Writing

写汉字 · 歌

歌
14 strokes
✋ Air-trace here
哥 (elder brother) + 欠 (owe) = "Elder brother owes a song"
Pinyin gē
English song
Radical 欠 (qiàn) — owe/lack
Strokes 14
Mnemonic
哥 (gē, elder brother) + 欠 (qiàn, owe) = 歌 (gē, song)
"Elder brother owes a song!" — He promised to sing but hasn't yet!
Stroke Order
横、竖、横折、横、竖、横、竖、横折、横、竖、横、撇、横撇、捺
