Tāmen zhèngzài xué huà shuǐcǎihuàr.
They are learning to paint watercolour.
儿化音 érhuàyīn: The 儿 at the end (国画儿, 水彩画儿) is a Beijing-style retroflex pronunciation. In southern China, people often say 国画 and 水彩画 without the 儿.

Dialogue Pattern · 对话句型
A: 他们/她们正在做什么？
Tāmen zhèngzài zuò shénme?
B: 他们/她们正在 [activity]。
Tāmen zhèngzài [activity].
Substitution Options · 替换词

Character Writing · 汉字书写
画
Pinyinhuà
Englishto paint; painting; picture
Strokes8 strokes
Radical田 (tián — field)
Stroke Order
一丨𠃍一 丨一∟一
Components一 + 田 + 凵
Mnemonic: "A field (田) inside a frame — like a painting in a frame."
Trace in the air with your finger. Say the strokes out loud. Then write ×5 in your notebook.

Character Writing · 汉字书写
国
Pinyinguó
Englishcountry; nation
Strokes8 strokes
Radical囗 (wéi — enclosure)
Stroke Order
丨𠃍一一 丨一丶一
Components囗 + 玉 (jade)
Mnemonic: "Jade (玉) inside a border (囗) = country/kingdom."
Compound words: 中国 Zhōngguó (China) 国画儿 guóhuàr (Chinese painting)
Air-write with counting. Connect to words you know: 中国, 国画儿. Write ×5 in your notebook.
