语法复习 · Grammar Recap

yǔ fǎ fù xí
Pattern 1
你有什么爱好？→ 我喜欢 ______。
Asking & answering about hobbies
Pattern 2
我喜欢 ______，也喜欢 ______，还喜欢 ______。
Extending answers with 也 and 还
Pattern 3
我一边 ______ 一边 ______。
Two actions at the same time
Quick oral drill: Ask 5 students random questions using these 3 patterns. Keep it fast!

课文 · Reading

kè wén

📖 我叫田云 · My name is Tian Yun

我叫田云。我有很多爱好。

Wǒ jiào Tián Yún. Wǒ yǒu hěn duō àihào.

我喜欢一边弹钢琴，一边唱歌。

Wǒ xǐhuān yìbiān tán gāngqín, yìbiān chànggē.

我还喜欢听音乐和读书。

Wǒ hái xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè hé dúshū.

我每天读一个小时的书。

Wǒ měitiān dú yí ge xiǎoshí de shū.

Read together twice. Then students read individually. Highlight: 一边...一边..., 还, 每天, 一个小时.

理解问题 · Comprehension

lǐ jiě wèn tí
1. 田云有什么爱好？ (What are Tian Yunʼs hobbies?)
2. 她一边做什么一边做什么？ (What does she do at the same time?)
3. 她每天读几个小时的书？ (How many hours does she read?)
Students answer in pairs first, then share with class. Aim for full Chinese sentence answers!

中国乐器 · Chinese Instruments

Zhōngguó yuèqì
🪕 古筝 gǔ zhēng Chinese zither 21 strings · plucked · 2500+ years old
🎻 二胡 èr hú Chinese fiddle 2 strings · bowed · very expressive sound
🎵 琵琶 pí pá Chinese lute 4 strings · plucked · "pear-shaped" body
🎶 笛子 dí zi Chinese flute Bamboo · horizontal · bright, clear tone
Play a short clip of each instrument if available (YouTube: 古筝, 二胡, 琵琶, 笛子). Students guess which is which.

Group Dialogue · 小组对话

xiǎo zǔ duì huà

🎯 Create & perform a dialogue. Include:

你有什么爱好？ 我喜欢 + 也/还 一边...一边... 每天...个小时

Work in groups of 2-3. Write 6-8 lines. Perform for the class!

⏱ Prep time 8 min
🎭 Perform 2 min each
Encourage creativity! They can pretend to be musicians, talk about Chinese instruments, be funny.
