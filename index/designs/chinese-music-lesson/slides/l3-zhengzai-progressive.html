Learning Intention

今天我们要学什么？

jīn tiān wǒ men yào xué shén me?

To understand and use 正在 to ask and answer about actions happening right now.

I can explain what 正在 means and form sentences correctly
I can tell the difference between "I sing" and "I am singing" in Chinese
I can ask and answer 正在做什么？ fluently
I can choose 他们 or 她们 correctly in writing
I can write 正 在 画 国 with correct stroke order

⚠️ Common Mistake

正在放在哪里？

zhèngzài fàng zài nǎlǐ?
❌ 他唱歌正在。
WRONG! 正在 is after the verb ❌
✅ 他正在唱歌。
RIGHT! 正在 is before the verb ✅
⚠️ 正在 ALWAYS before the verb!
This is the #1 mistake — remember: 正在 comes FIRST!

Quick Check

正在 ✓ or ✗?

judge: correct use of 正在?
1 他正在唱歌。 ✅ Correct
2 我正在喜欢音乐。 ❌ Wrong — 喜欢 can't be progressive
3 妈妈正在听音乐。 ✅ Correct
4 他正在是学生。 ❌ Wrong — 是 can't be progressive
5 弟弟正在看书。 ✅ Correct
🤔 Remember: 喜欢 and 是 describe states, not actions — can't use with 正在!

答案 · Answer Key

Quick Check Answers

duì bú duì? · Right or wrong?
1 他正在唱歌。 ✅ Correct — action happening now
2 我正在喜欢音乐。 ❌ Wrong — 喜欢 is a state, not an action
3 妈妈正在听音乐。 ✅ Correct — action happening now
4 他正在是学生。 ❌ Wrong — 是 is a state, not an action
5 弟弟正在看书。 ✅ Correct — action happening now
⚠️ Key rule: 正在 only works with ACTIONS, not states (喜欢, 是, 知道...)

🖌️ Students with watercolours (Image: 水彩画儿 lesson)

They are learning to paint watercolour.
儿化音 érhuàyīn: The 儿 at the end (国画儿, 水彩画儿) is a Beijing-style retroflex pronunciation. In southern China, people often say 国画 and 水彩画 without the 儿.

你做我猜! 🎭
Nǐ zuò wǒ cāi! · You act, I guess!
How to play:
  1. One student comes to the front and picks an action from the word bank
  2. Act it out — NO talking! Use gestures only 🙅‍♂️
  3. Class asks together: 他/她正在做什么？
  4. First student with a full 正在 sentence wins a point!
  5. Winner becomes the next actor
Sentence frame:
Q: 他/她正在做什么？
→
A: 他/她正在____！
⏱ 30 seconds per round · Keep score between teams!
