27–36 min · You Do · Writing
活动二 · 位置词造句
YOU DO · Write → Peer-Check
1
Teacher models one example live: 我们学校的图书馆在体育馆的旁边。
2
Individual writing (5 min): 5 sentences, one per location word, each following X 在 Y 的 + location word exactly.
3
Peer-check (2 min): swap books — neighbour circles any word-order errors in pencil, without fixing them.
4
Students self-correct any circled sentences.
Scaffold
Gap-fill frame per sentence, location word already given for each of the 5 lines
Extension
Write unaided (no gap-fill) + add one 中间 sentence with two named reference points (X 在 Y 和 Z 的中间)
Teacher Model
我们学校的图书馆在体育馆的旁边。
Sentence Frame (Scaffold)
我们学校的在的。
