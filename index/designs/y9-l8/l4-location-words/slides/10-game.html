How to Play — 6–8 Rounds

1
Divide the class into 3–4 teams
2
Project one new error sentence at a time (not reused from Activity 1)
3
Next team's student says the corrected sentence within 10 seconds. Correct = 1 point; wrong/slow = next team may steal
4
Play 6–8 rounds, keep score on the board or projector

Scaffold

Teacher may repeat the sentence once if a student mishears it before the 10-second timer starts

Extension

Winning students must add the reference-point ID for a bonus point:
"参照物是'游泳池'。"
