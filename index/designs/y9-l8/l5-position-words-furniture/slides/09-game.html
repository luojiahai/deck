How to Play — 5–6 Rounds

1
Project one scrambled sentence at a time
2
Pairs reorder the phrase in exercise books, as fast as possible
3
First pair to hold up the correct sentence wins a point
4
Teacher reveals correct order to confirm. Mix furniture-scene and floor-plan sentences.

Scaffold

Rounds 1–2: keep the position word underlined in the scramble as a hint of where it must land relative to 的

Extension

After winning a round, the pair must generate one additional original sentence using the same position word before the next round starts
Setup: Projector only — 5–6 scrambled-sentence slides prepared in advance
