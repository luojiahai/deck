20–27 min · We Do (Based on Act. 7, p.76)
活动一 · 教室里的东西
WE DO · Model → Turn-and-Talk → Share
1
Project labelled classroom scene (desk, chair, bookshelf, blackboard, window, door, pencil case, bag). Teacher models 3 sentences, pointing to each item.
2
Turn-and-talk (2 min): Partner A describes 2 items using position words, Partner B describes 2 different items; swap and check for repeats.
3
Cold call 3 students to share one sentence each with the class.
