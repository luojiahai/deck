YOU DO · Model → Write → Peer-Check
1
Teacher models live, thinking aloud: 教室在楼上，办公室在楼下——用'楼上'和'楼下'。图书馆在餐厅的对面——用'对面'。
2
Individual writing (5 min): paragraph (5–6 sentences) using at least 4 different position words (mixing L4 + today's set).
3
Peer-check (2 min): swap books; neighbour checks ≥4 different position words used and circles word-order errors.
