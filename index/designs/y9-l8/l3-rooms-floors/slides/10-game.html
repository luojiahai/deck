36–43 min · Game
快问快答楼层 · Floor Number Flash

How to Play — 8–10 Rounds

1
Divide the class into two teams (left half vs right half)
2
Teacher calls out a room and states its floor ONLY ONCE in a full sentence
3
Immediately asks a rapid follow-up question — both teams race to answer chorally; first correct floor number in Chinese wins the point
4
Play 8–10 rounds, mixing in both Text 2 and dictation-set rooms

What It Practises

Fast listening recall of floor levels; reinforces the 在……层 pattern under time pressure.

Scaffold

Teacher may repeat the room name (but not the floor number) once if a team asks for clarification.

Extension

After winning a round, one team member must restate the full sentence with the location phrase (旁边/隔壁/中间), if applicable, for a bonus point.
