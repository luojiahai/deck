活动二 · 听写楼层图 Listen-and-Label
YOU DO · Listen & Label
1
Copy the 3-floor building outline quickly (1 min) — Floor 1 / Floor 2 / Floor 3, each with 2 empty boxes
2
Teacher reads 6 sentences, once each, at natural pace — no repeats until the full set is done
3
Write the correct room name in the correct floor box as you listen
4
Teacher re-reads the full set once more at normal pace — check and fill any gaps, then self-mark against the revealed floor plan
OTR 2
All students write independently during the dictation
Scaffold
6 room names projected as word bank — students only need to catch the floor number
Extension
Add one spatial detail after listening — e.g. predict/infer which rooms might be next door to each other — and share your reasoning with a partner
