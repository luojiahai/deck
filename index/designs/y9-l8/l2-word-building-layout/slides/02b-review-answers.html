答案 · Correct the Teacher
1
我们学校有五个教学楼。
✅ 五幢教学楼！
个 → 幢  |  幢 counts whole buildings/blocks — 教学楼 is a whole building.
2
学校有一幢图书馆。
✅ 一个图书馆！
幢 → 个  |  个 is the default measure word for most rooms/facilities.
3
我们学校有一个篮球场和一场足球场。
✅ 一个足球场！
场 → 个  |  "场" is already inside the word — it doesn't take its own measure word.
