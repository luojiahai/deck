27–35 min · You Do · Writing
活动二 · 看地图介绍学校
YOU DO · Describe → Turn & Talk → Write
1
Teacher models 3 items from the map: 我们学校有一个图书馆，还有一个礼堂。
2
Turn-and-talk (2 min): Partner A describes 3 facilities, Partner B describes 3 different ones; swap & check no repeats.
3
Individual writing (3 min): short paragraph (4–5 sentences) in exercise books, correct measure words throughout.
4
Cold call 2 students to read their paragraph aloud.
Sentence Frame (Scaffold)
我们学校有幢教学楼、个、个和个。
我们学校还有一个。
Scaffold
Sentence frame above kept projected throughout the task
Extension
Describe all ten facilities, no repeated sentence structure — alternate 我们学校有…… / 我们学校还有……
