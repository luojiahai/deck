How to Play

1
Divide class into 3–4 teams. Project one base word at a time.
2
First student in each team's line races to be first to call out the completed compound word with the correct building-block character.
3
First correct answer scores a point; teacher reveals the correct compound to confirm.
4
Rotate through all 12 base words, keeping score on the whiteboard corner or projector.

Scaffold

If a team is stuck, teacher may reveal the first character of the building block as a hint.

Extension

Once a team wins a round, one member must use the new compound word in a full sentence before the point is confirmed.
