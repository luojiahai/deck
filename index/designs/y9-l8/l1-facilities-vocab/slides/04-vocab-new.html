💡
馆 (guǎn, 3rd tone) appears in both 图书馆 and 体育馆 — drill this character alone first, then in both compounds.
Pattern: X场 (chǎng) = an open field/court for X — 篮球场, 足球场, 操场, 停车场 all follow this compound, so 场 does not need its own measure word.
字形小知识 · Component breakdown: 介(mediate)+绍(carry on)=介绍 "introduce"  ·  图(picture)+书(book)+馆(hall)=图书馆 "library"  ·  礼(ceremony)+堂(hall)=礼堂 "auditorium"  ·  操(exercise)+场(field)=操场 "sports field"  ·  游泳(swim)+池(pool)=游泳池 "swimming pool"
