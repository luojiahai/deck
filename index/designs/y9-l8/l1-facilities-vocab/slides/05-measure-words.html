14–17 min · I Do · Grammar Focus
量词 · Measure Words 个 & 幢
个
gè
default for most rooms/facilities:
一个图书馆, 一个礼堂
幢
zhuàng
whole buildings/blocks:
五幢教学楼

Which measure word?

数整栋楼用"幢"，其他的大部分用"个"。 Count a whole building block with 幢; count most other rooms/facilities with 个.

✗ 一个教学楼 → ✓ 一幢教学楼
⚠️
场-words don't take a separate measure word
篮球场/足球场/操场/停车场 already include 场 — always 一个篮球场, never "一场篮球场".
