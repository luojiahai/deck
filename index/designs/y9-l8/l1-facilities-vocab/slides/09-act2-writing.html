27–37 min · You Do · Writing-Focused
活动二 · 我们学校有……
YOU DO · Write & Peer-Check
1
Teacher models on projector — writes 3 sentences live, thinking aloud: "教学楼——整栋楼——幢。图书馆——个。"
2
Individual writing (6 min): paragraph with at least six facilities, correct measure words, 和 before the last item.
3
Peer-check (2 min): swap books with a neighbour, circle any 个/幢 errors in pencil.
4
Cold call 2–3 students to read their paragraph aloud (2 min).
OTR 2: All students write independently; the peer-check step requires every student to read and mark a partner's work.
Sentence Frame (kept projected throughout)
我来介绍一下我们学校。我们学校有幢教学楼、个、个和个。
Scaffold
Sentence frame + word bank projected alongside throughout the task
Extension
Write at least 8 facilities (full list) without the word bank, plus an opinion sentence: "我们学校有一个游泳池，我觉得游泳池很有用。"
