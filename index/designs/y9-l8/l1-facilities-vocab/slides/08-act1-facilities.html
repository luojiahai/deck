20–27 min · We Do (Act. 1, p.71)
活动一 · 看图说设施
WE DO · Picture Flash
1
Teacher projects each picture for 3 seconds — class chorally names the facility.
2
After naming, the 汉字 fades in to confirm.
3
After all 8: display all at once. Cold call individual students to point and name.
4
Final round: teacher says the English name, class chorally says the Chinese — no visual support.
OTR 1: After the random-pointing round, teacher rapid-fires English names (library, gymnasium, canteen, car park, swimming pool…) and all students respond chorally each time.
Scaffold
Keep pinyin visible on the first pass through all 8; remove for the second and third passes
Extension
After naming each facility, add measure word + mini-sentence: "图书馆——我们学校有一个图书馆。"
