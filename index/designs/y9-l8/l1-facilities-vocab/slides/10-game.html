How to Play

1
Setup (projector only): display 8 facility nouns one at a time — no measure word shown
2
Two teams (left half vs right half). Teacher calls the next student in rotation, alternating teams, to say the noun with its correct measure word: "一个图书馆！"
3
Correct = 1 point. If wrong, the other team may steal by answering correctly.
4
Keep a running score on the projector or whiteboard corner.

Scaffold

If a student hesitates, teacher may offer the first sound of the measure word ("g……") as a prompt before moving to the next student.

Extension

Winning answers for fluent speakers must be given as a full sentence, not just "measure word + noun":
我们学校有一个图书馆。
