How to Play — Same Teams as Act. 1

1
Describe (3 min): teams describe the projected city map together using position words — 3–4 sentences
2
Redesign (4 min): each team redesigns the city on a blank grid in exercise books — move buildings anywhere — and writes 3–4 sentences describing the new layout
3
Present (2 min): one member from 2–3 teams presents their redesigned city in Chinese; class listens and can ask one question each

Scaffold

Sentence frame: ______在______的______。
图书馆学校医院 公园商店火车站

Extension

Use at least five different position words across the description and redesign, and justify one design choice:
"我们把公园放在学校旁边，因为……"
