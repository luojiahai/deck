Part 2 · 短文描述 (5 min)
Write a short paragraph (4–5 sentences) describing your own school. Must include:
at least one measure word (个/幢)
one 层=楼 reference
one X在Y的位置词 sentence
我们学校有幢教学楼。
教室在层。
图书馆在的。
Extension: Write 6+ sentences without the frame, using at least one ⭐ test-variant word (校长室, 游泳馆, 体育场 or 运动场) correctly.
