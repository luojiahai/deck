L6 · ASKING THE TIME Practice · Walk and Talk
25–33 min · Activity 2
Walk and Talk · 走一走，说一说
Each student gets a "watch card" with a time. Walk around the room and have real conversations!
Round 1

Basic: Ask + Record

1
Walk to a classmate
2
Ask: 现在几点？
3
They answer using their watch card
4
Write down their time + name
5
Switch roles
6
Move to a new partner
📝 Record sheet: Name | Digital Time | Chinese Expression
Round 2

Advanced: Write BOTH + Follow-up

1
New partner — ask 现在几点？
2
Write their answer in digits AND Chinese
Then ask one follow-up:
→ 你要做什么？ (What will you do?)
→ 你忙吗？ (Are you busy?)
→ 你有表吗？ (Do you have a watch?)
