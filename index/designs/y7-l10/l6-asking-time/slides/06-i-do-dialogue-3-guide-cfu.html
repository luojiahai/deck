L6 · ASKING THE TIME I Do · Extended Dialogue + Guide + CFU
Dialogue 3 · Extended (Polite + Reason)
A
请问，现在几点？
Qǐng wèn — Excuse me, what time is it?
B
差十分四点。
Ten to four. (3:50)
A
谢谢你！我要去踢足球。
Thank you! I need to go play football.
B
不客气。玩得开心！
You're welcome. Have fun!
