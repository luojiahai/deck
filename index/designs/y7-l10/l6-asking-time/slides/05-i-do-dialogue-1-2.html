Model Dialogues · 示范对话
Dialogue 1 · Basic Q&A
A
现在几点？
Xiànzài jǐ diǎn? · What time is it?
B
两点半。
Liǎng diǎn bàn. · Half past two.
A
谢谢！
Xièxie! · Thanks!
B
不客气。
Bù kèqì. · You're welcome.
Dialogue 2 · Verification (repeat to confirm)
A
现在几点？
What time is it?
B
五点四十五分。
5:45.
A
五点四十五分？
5:45? (repeating to confirm)
B
对，五点四十五分。
Yes, 5:45.
💡 Repeating the time back shows you understood and confirms accuracy.
💡 Notice: reply is just the time — no need to repeat 现在几点.
