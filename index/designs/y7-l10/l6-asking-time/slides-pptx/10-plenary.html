Review & Reflect · 回顾与反思

Revisit Success Criteria

I can ask the time using 现在几点 in a real conversation.

I can answer with the most natural Chinese time expression.

I can have a real conversation about time with classmates.

Self-Assessment: Conversation Skills

3

Level 3

I can ask AND answer about time with a follow-up question, using 请问 naturally.

2

Level 2

I can ask 现在几点？ and answer the time. I sometimes use natural patterns.

1

Level 1

I can recognise 现在几点？ but I'm not yet confident answering or asking.
