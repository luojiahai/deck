刂 (knife radical) + 亥 (phonetic)
Originally: to carve / notch → a marked division of time
The character 刻 means "a quarter" of an hour (15 minutes).
In Chinese, we say 一刻 for "one quarter" (:15 past) and 三刻 for "three quarters" (:45 past).
