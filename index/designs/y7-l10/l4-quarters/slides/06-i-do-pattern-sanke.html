⚠️
三刻 = 3 quarters PAST the hour, NOT "to" the next hour!
Always count FORWARD: 4:45 = 四点三刻 (three quarters past 4 o'clock)
Also correct: 四点四十五分 — but 三刻 is more natural in spoken Chinese
