When answering, choose the shortest and most natural pattern!

链式对话 · Chain Dialogue
We Do — whole class, 7 minutes
1
教师开始 · Teacher starts
Teacher turns to Student 1 and asks: 「现在几点？」
Student 1 answers, then asks Student 2 — continue around the class!
2
规则 · Rules
No repeating a time already said. No repeating a pattern twice in a row.
If previous used 半, you must use 分/刻/差/点!
3
挑战 · Challenge
Answer within 5 seconds — or you are out! (playful skip)
★ Extension: fluent students must use 差 in their answer
Scaffold: 现在几点？ → (time) → 下一个！ — pattern summary visible on board throughout
活动 · Activity 1
走一走，问一问 · Walk and Talk
You Do — stand up, walk around, ask your classmates!
How it works
1 Each student gets a watch card with a time (digits + Chinese)
2 Stand up, walk around, ask classmates: 现在几点？
3 Round 1 (3 min): Ask and record the digits on paper
4 Round 2 (7 min): Write BOTH digits AND Chinese! Add a follow-up: 「谢谢！」or「真的吗？」or「好的！」
★ Extension: fluent students act as time verifiers — check others' answers!
