快速问答 · Quick Questions (textbook p84)
1 你叫什么名字？ Nǐ jiào shénme míngzì? What's your name? → write your Chinese name
2 你是哪国人？ Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén? Which country are you from? → 美国人 / 英国人 / 澳大利亚人
3 你家有几口人？ Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén? How many people in your family? → write a number
4 你的生日是几月几号？ Nǐ de shēngrì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào? When is your birthday? → write the date in digits
5 你住在哪儿？ Nǐ zhù zài nǎr? Where do you live? → write the city
6 你今年上几年级？ Nǐ jīnnián shàng jǐ niánjí? What year level are you in? → 七年级
7 你家的电话号码是多少？ Nǐ jiā de diànhuà hàomǎ shì duōshao? What's your home phone number? → write any fake number — practise digits

句型 · Key Pattern
How to say o'clock times in Chinese
数字 + 点 = o'clock
一点
yī diǎn · 1:00
三点
sān diǎn · 3:00
八点
bā diǎn · 8:00
十二点
shí'èr diǎn · 12:00
句型 · Pattern
检查理解
Finger voting — 1 finger = yes ✓ / 2 fingers = no ✗
1 「一点」is 1 o'clock — yes or no?
2 「二点」is correct — yes or no?
3 三点的拼音是 sān diǎn — yes or no?
4 Teacher says「九点」— write the DIGIT on paper, hold it up!
检测 · Check
检查理解 · 答案
Let's check — did you get them right?
1 「一点」is 1 o'clock ✓ Yes — 一点 = 1:00
2 「二点」is correct? ✗ No — should be 两点!
3 三点的拼音是 sān diǎn ✓ Yes — sān diǎn ✓
4 Teacher says「九点」— write the digit → 9（九）

合唱闪卡 · Choral Clock Flash
We Do — whole class together
1 Projector shows a clock face — everyone says the time together in Chinese
2 Start slow, then speed up — 12 o'clock times, shuffled
3 2:00 will appear twice — pause and emphasise「两点」each time!
★ Fluent students: add 早上 (morning) or 下午 (afternoon) before the time
Scaffold: 数字 + 点 — keep this formula visible throughout

活动 · Activity 2
传声筒 · Telephone Game
4 teams — whisper, run, write!
1 Class in 4 rows（teams）
2 Teacher whispers a time in Chinese to the first student in each row
3 Each student whispers it to the next — down the row
4 Last student runs to the board and writes the time in Chinese characters
★ First correct team wins a point! After each round, everyone says the answer together.
Rounds

Round 2 两点 ← 陷阱!
