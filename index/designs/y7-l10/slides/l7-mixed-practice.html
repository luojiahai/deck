学习目标 · Learning Goals
Today: the Time Olympics — 4 rounds, compete against yourself!
Learning Intention
We are learning to use all time expressions fluently and accurately under time pressure.
Success Criteria — I can…
  • Hear Chinese time and write the correct digits （听中文时间写数字）
  • See a clock and say the most natural Chinese expression instantly （看钟表秒说中文）
  • Switch between 点, 分, 半, 刻, and 差 without hesitation （灵活切换所有时间表达）

第四轮 · Champion's Choice
冠军之选 · 答案揭晓
The more natural, spoken-style choice — self-mark: 10 points each!
1A — 三点Shorter is better
2A — 七点半半 is more spoken than 三十分
3A — 六点三刻三刻 is shorter and more spoken
4B — 差十分三点差 version sounds more natural
5A — 九点一刻一刻 is the standard spoken form
6B — 差五分十二点差 version is much shorter!
7A — 一点零五分差五十五分 is too long
8A — 四点三刻三刻 is the shortest option
9A — 差十分九点差 is smoother for 8:50
10B — 十二点半半 is much more spoken
Key principle: shorter → more spoken. 半/刻/差 are used because they're faster than saying full minutes!
