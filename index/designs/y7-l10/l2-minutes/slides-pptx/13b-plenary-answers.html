EXIT TICKET

Write 8:07 in Chinese.

ANSWER

八点零七分

bā diǎn líng qī fēn

八 (8) + 点 (o'clock) + 零 (zero) + 七 (7) + 分 (minutes) — Remember: use 零 when minutes < 10!
