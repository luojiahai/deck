土

tǔ · "earth" radical

Found in: 地, 场, 坐, 在

寸

cùn · "inch" radical

Found in: 对, 时, 封, 寺

Characters with 土

地 — dì, ground / earth

坐 — zuò, to sit

在 — zài, at / in / on

Characters with 寸

时 — shí, time (as in 时间)

对 — duì, correct / towards

封 — fēng, to seal (envelope)
