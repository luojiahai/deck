New Vocabulary · 新词汇

分

fēn

minute(s)

4 strokes · 部首：刀 (dāo)

零

líng

zero

13 strokes · 部首：雨 (yǔ)

分 (fēn) = minute. Just like o'clock uses 点, minutes use 分 after the number. 零 (líng) = zero. Used as a placeholder when minutes are 1–9, like saying "oh-five" in English for 3:05.
