🚪 EXIT TICKET
Write 8:07 in Chinese.
Answer
八点零七分
bā diǎn líng qī fēn
八 (8) + 点 (o'clock) + 零 (zero) + 七 (7) + 分 (minutes)
Remember: use 零 when minutes are less than 10!
