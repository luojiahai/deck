13 strokes · 部首：雨 (yǔ)
分 (fēn) = minute. Just like o'clock uses 点, minutes use 分 after the number.
零 (líng) = zero. Used as a placeholder when minutes are 1–9, like saying "oh-five" in English for 3:05.
