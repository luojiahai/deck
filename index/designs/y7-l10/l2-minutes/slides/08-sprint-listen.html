Sprint 1: Lightning Listen · 闪电听力
Teacher says 8 times in Chinese. You write the DIGITS. Speed + accuracy!
HOW TO PLAY
1
Teacher says a time in Chinese (e.g. "三点十分"). No repeating!
2
Write the digits only on your paper (e.g. 3:10).
3
After all 8, swap papers with a partner for marking.
4
Teacher reveals answers on the next slide. Count your score out of 8!
