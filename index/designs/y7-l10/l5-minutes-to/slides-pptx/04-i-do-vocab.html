New Vocabulary · 新词汇

差

chà

to fall short of; to lack

9 strokes · radical: 工 (gōng)

现在

xiànzài

now; at present

10 strokes · radical: 王 (xiàn)

表

biǎo

watch; clock; to show

8 strokes · radical: 衤 (yī)

The character 差 means "to fall short" or "not enough." In telling time, it means we are short of the next hour — exactly like "minutes to" in English.
