Radical Spotlight · 部首聚焦

彡

shān

"hair" or "bristle" radical

Appears in characters related to decoration, pattern, or hair — e.g. 形 (form), 影 (shadow), 彩 (colour).

衤

yī

"clothing" radical

Left-side variant of 衣 (yī, clothing). Appears in 表 (watch), 衫 (shirt), 裤 (trousers), 裙 (skirt).

表

表 = 衤 (clothing radical)

The character for "watch" is built on the clothing radical — originally, a watch was something you wore!
