6:50 — Which is more natural? 1 finger = 六点五十分, 2 fingers = 差十分七点.

差 means "fall short of" — 1 finger = yes, 2 fingers = no.

→ 差五分八点

4

Trap! 2:50 using 差 — write it on paper.

→ 差十分三点 (NOT 差十分两点!)
