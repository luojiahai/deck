REVISIT SUCCESS CRITERIA

I can say half-past times using 半

I can tell the difference between X点半 and X点三十分

I can see a clock and pick the right pattern — 点, 分, or 半

Self-Assessment

On your paper, draw a clock showing an easy time (one you feel confident with) and a clock showing a hard time (one you still find tricky). Write the Chinese next to each.

Exit Ticket

Write 2:30 in Chinese.

Double trap: don't forget 两 (not 二) + 半

1 finger = Achieved

2 fingers = Getting there

Fist = Need help
