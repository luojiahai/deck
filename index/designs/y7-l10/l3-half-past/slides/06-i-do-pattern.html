💡
X点半 = X点三十分 — both correct!
半 is shorter and more common in everyday speech. 三十分 is more formal / written.
⚠️
2:30 = 两点半 NOT 二点半!
Same rule as o'clock: before 点, use 两 (liǎng) instead of 二 (èr)
