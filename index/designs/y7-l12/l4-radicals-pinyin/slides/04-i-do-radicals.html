Scaffolded — One Radical at a Time
For each radical: show it alone → show it highlighted inside the example character → say what image/meaning it evokes → students copy-write the radical 3× on whiteboards → move to the next. Do not introduce all 6 at once.
Stroke Order
Demonstrate stroke order on the projector for the two trickier radicals — 竹 and 钅 — before students copy.
铁
铁 (tiě, "iron") uses the 钅(metal) radical!
Recall 地铁 (dìtiě, "subway") from Lesson 2 — same 铁 character.
