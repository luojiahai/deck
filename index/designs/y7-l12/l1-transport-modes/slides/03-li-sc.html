Learning Intention
We are learning to name common modes of transport in Chinese and describe how family members get to school or work.

I can name at least 6 modes of transport in Chinese.
说出交通工具的名称
2
I can write sentences using 坐 + vehicle, and 走路 / 开车.

3
I can describe how someone in my family commutes.
