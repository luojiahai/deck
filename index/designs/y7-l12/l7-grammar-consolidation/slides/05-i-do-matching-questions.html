Matching & Forming Questions
Ex11 · Match the Halves
The question word tells you what KIND of answer to look for.
怎么→ method — HOW someone travels
几点→ time — WHAT TIME something happens
谁→ who — WHO is involved
Model 2 example pairs — think aloud: "This question ends in 怎么, so I need an answer about a method of transport…"
Ex13 · Form the Question
Given an answer, produce the matching question.
