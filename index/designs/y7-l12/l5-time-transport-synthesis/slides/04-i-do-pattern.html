10–20 min · I Do · Key Sentence Pattern
时间 + 交通工具 · The Full Commute Sentence
Subject + (每天) + Time + 坐/走路/开车 + Activity
ExampleEnglish
我爸爸七点半开车上班。My dad drives to work at 7:30.
我妈妈每天八点坐地铁上班。My mum takes the subway to work at 8 every day.
我哥哥七点一刻坐校车上学。My brother takes the school bus to school at quarter past 7.
🎉
