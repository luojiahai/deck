What's the difference between 小说 and 杂志?
小说 = novel — a long work of fiction you read cover to cover.
杂志 = magazine — a periodical with articles, pictures, and features.
2
Spot 都 in Text 2 — what does the sentence mean?
油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画。
"Oil painting, watercolour — I like painting both."
都 (dōu) bundles a list together: "all / both of these"
3
What does 正在 tell us about an action?
正在 marks an action happening right now, at this moment.
我正在学画国画儿。 = "I am currently learning to paint Chinese painting."
