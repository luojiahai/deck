How to Play

1️⃣
Students have a 3×3 card with 9 English duration phrases in random squares.
2️⃣
Teacher calls out duration phrases in Chinese (e.g. 两个小时). Students find & mark the English equivalent.
3️⃣
Three in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) = BINGO! Call out and read back the phrases.
🔄
Play 2–3 rounds. Sample card shown → each student's card has phrases in different positions.
📣
Teacher calls in Chinese → students mark English
e.g. 两个小时 → students mark "2 hours"
Scaffold
Duration list stays on projector so weaker students can reference it
Extension
Students take turns calling phrases in Chinese (student caller role)
Setup / OTR
Print or draw 3×3 blank grid; fill squares with any 9 of the 12 English phrases in any order
