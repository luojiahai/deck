L3 · DURATION · 时间长度 Learning Intention & Success Criteria
Learning Intention · 学习目标
We are learning to say how long we do activities using the duration structure in Chinese.
Success Criteria · 成功标准 — I can…
1
Say time duration phrases in Chinese
e.g. 五分钟 · 一刻钟 · 两个小时 · 四个半小时
2
Use the duration structure: 每天 + verb + duration + 的 + noun
e.g. 他每天弹一个小时的钢琴。
3
Describe how long someone does their hobby from a picture
Look at an image → produce a full sentence with duration
