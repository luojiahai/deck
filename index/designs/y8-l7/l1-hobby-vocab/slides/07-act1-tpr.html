L1 · HOBBY VOCAB Activity 1 · TPR · 20–28 min
Activity 1
TPR · Say It and Do It
⏱ 8 min
We Do

Instructions

  1. Teacher calls out a hobby verb — e.g. 弹钢琴, 唱歌, 玩电脑游戏
  2. Students mime the action. Build speed.
  3. Reverse: Teacher mimes → students call out the Chinese word
  4. Extension: students take turns as the caller
