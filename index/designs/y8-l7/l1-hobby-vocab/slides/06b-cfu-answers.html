What does 弹 mean?
弹 = play a string or keyboard instrument
Used for piano, guitar, lute — not for bowed strings like violin
2
How to say "I like listening to music"
我喜欢听音乐。
wǒ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè
3
Same and different: 弹钢琴 vs 弹吉他
Same verb 弹 — different instruments: 钢琴 (piano) vs 吉他 (guitar)
One verb, two different objects. This pattern works for any plucked/keyboard instrument.
