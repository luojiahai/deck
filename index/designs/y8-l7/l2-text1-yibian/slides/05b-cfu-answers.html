答对了吗？
Let's go through the answers together.
1
Is this correct?
我一边弹钢琴，我一边唱歌。
✗
Incorrect — subject repeated!
✓ 我一边弹钢琴，一边唱歌。
In 一边……一边……, the subject appears only once at the start. Do not repeat 我 before the second 一边.
2
What is wrong?
一边我听音乐，一边读书。
✗
一边 comes after the subject, not before!
✓ 我一边听音乐，一边读书。
Word order: Subject → 一边 → Verb 1，一边 → Verb 2. The subject must come first.
3
Simultaneous actions?
我喜欢一边吃饭一边看电视。
✓
👍 Yes — correct!
Eating AND watching TV at the same time — one person, two simultaneous actions. This is a perfect 一边……一边…… sentence!
