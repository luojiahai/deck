Text 1 · 课文 (with pinyin)
我叫田云。我有很多爱好。
Wǒ jiào Tián Yún. Wǒ yǒu hěn duō àihào. 我喜欢一边弹钢琴，一边唱歌。
Wǒ xǐhuān yībiān tán gāngqín, yībiān chànggē. 我还喜欢听音乐和读书。
Wǒ hái xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè hé dúshū. 我每天读一个小时的书。
Wǒ měitiān dú yī ge xiǎoshí de shū.
Instructions
1
Read together twice as a class (choral)
2
Partner A reads aloud — Partner B listens and corrects
3
Swap roles — B reads, A corrects
4
Focus on tone and rhythm — 一边 has 4th then 1st tone
Pair Roles
Partner A
Reads aloud fluently
Partner B
Listens, corrects pronunciation
Swap!
Then swap roles
Scaffold
Pinyin visible — students can use it as a pronunciation guide
Extension
Cover pinyin, read characters only — try from memory
OTR
Every student reads aloud — no silent observers in pair work
