L4 · PINYIN ao/ou + LISTENING Phonics · ao vs ou
声母辨音 · Distinguishing ao and ou
Exaggerate mouth shapes — students mirror.
Vowel Sound 1
ao
Open wide — jaw drops low
like "ow" in cow
Examples
好 hǎo
高 gāo
爱好 àihào
Vowel Sound 2
ou
Lips round and close together
like "oh" in go
Examples
走 zǒu
头 tóu
九 jiǔ
Teacher says — students mirror the mouth shape. Switch between ao and ou several times.
