L7 · 都 · BOTH / ALL Critical Word Order
Where does 都 go?
都 comes BEFORE the verb — never after!
❌
WRONG
小说、杂志，我喜欢都。
都 placed AFTER the verb 喜欢 — this is incorrect Chinese!
✓
CORRECT
小说、杂志，我都喜欢。
都 placed BEFORE the verb 喜欢 — Subject + 都 + Verb ✓
Items ，Subject  都  Verb  —  都 always sits between the subject and the verb
