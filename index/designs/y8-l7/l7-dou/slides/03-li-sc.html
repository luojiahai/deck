L7 · 都 · BOTH / ALL Learning Intention & Success Criteria
We Are Learning To…
Express "both" and "all" preferences using the pattern ……，都……
By the end of this lesson, I can…
1
Place 都 correctly — after the subject, before the verb
油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画
2
List two or more items and use 都 to say I like / do them all
小说、杂志，他都喜欢看。  弹钢琴、唱歌、听音乐，她都喜欢。
3
Combine 都 and 正在 in a partner dialogue
我正在弹钢琴。吉他、钢琴，我都喜欢弹。
