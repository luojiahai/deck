1
Where does 都 go — before or after the verb?
✓ Answer  Before the verb.  Subject + 都 + Verb
2
Write: "Guitar and piano — I like playing both."
✓ Answer  吉他、钢琴，我都喜欢弹。
3
What is wrong?  弹钢琴、唱歌，我喜欢都。
都 is in the WRONG place — it comes AFTER 喜欢, but it must come BEFORE the verb.
✓ Corrected  弹钢琴、唱歌，我都喜欢。
