L7 · 都 · BOTH / ALL Key Pattern · ……，都……
The 都 Rule
Item 1、Item 2 ……
Items listed FIRST
，
Subject
who likes
都
both / all
Verb
action
⚠ List the items FIRST — then Subject + 都 + Verb
Examples
1
油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画。
Oil paintings and watercolours — I like painting both.
2
小说、杂志，他都喜欢看。
Novels and magazines — he likes reading both.
3
弹钢琴、唱歌、听音乐，她都喜欢。
Piano, singing, music — she likes them all.
