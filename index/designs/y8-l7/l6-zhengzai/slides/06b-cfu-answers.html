CFU Answers · 正确答案
Review and discuss each one before moving on
1
Which means "he plays piano every day"?
✓
A · 他喜欢弹钢琴
喜欢 = habitual/general preference — not tied to right now.
B (他正在弹钢琴) means he is playing piano at this exact moment.
2
"She is reading a novel right now."
✓
她正在看小说。
正在 + verb (看) + object (小说) — subject + 正在 + verb + object.
3
What's wrong? 他正在弹了钢琴
✓
Remove 了 — 正在 already signals present action
了 marks completed actions (past/change of state). It clashes with 正在 (now/ongoing). Correct: 他正在弹钢琴。
