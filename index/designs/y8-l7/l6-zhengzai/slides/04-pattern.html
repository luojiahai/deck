8–20 min · I Do
Key Pattern · 正在 + verb
Use 正在 before any verb to say the action is happening right now
Subject
主语 subject
+
正在
right now · 现在进行
+
Verb
动词 action
+
Object
宾语 (optional)
→
"[Subject] is [verb]-ing right now"
他正在弹钢琴。
He is playing piano right now.
她正在唱歌。
She is singing right now.
你正在听什么音乐？
What music are you listening to right now?
你们正在学中文。是不是？meta · right now!
You are learning Chinese. Aren't you? (Yes — right now!)
Q form
你正在做什么？
→
