L6 · 正在 · PRESENT CONTINUOUS I Do · 喜欢 vs 正在
Key Contrast · 区别
Habitual vs Right Now
Same verb — completely different meaning depending on which marker you use
喜欢 · Habitual
喜欢
General truth · always true · habit · preference
不受时间限制 — not tied to this moment
他喜欢弹钢琴。
He likes playing piano. (in general, always)
VS
正在 · Right Now
正在
Action in progress · happening at this exact moment
现在进行 — happening as we speak
他正在弹钢琴。
He is playing piano right now. (at this moment)
