Activity 2 · You Do · Pairs
Mime & Guess · 猜猜我在做什么
A mimes silently — B asks — A answers — swap roles after 3 mimes
8 min
A mimes silently (no words!) B asks using the question frame A answers yes / no Swap after 3 mimes each 2 pairs perform for class
Dialogue Flow · 对话流程
A
Partner A — mimes
🙊 silent action mime
Act out the verb with no speaking
B
Partner B — asks
你正在______吗？
或者：你在做什么？
Are you [verb]-ing? / What are you doing?
A
Partner A — answers
是的，我正在______。
或 不是，我正在______。
Yes, I'm …  /  No, I'm actually …
↔ Swap roles after 3 mimes each!
Question Frame · 问句框架
Ask (Option A)
你正在______吗？
Are you [verb]-ing right now?
Ask (Option B)
你在做什么？
What are you doing?
Answer — Yes
是的，我正在______。
Yes, I'm [verb]-ing.
Answer — No
不是，我正在______。
No, I'm actually [verb]-ing.
Scaffold Question frame stays on board throughout. Verb bank from Act 1 still visible.
Extension Use 一边……一边…… in at least one mime: 我正在一边弹钢琴一边唱歌。
OTR Every student mimes and speaks — teacher circulates, taps students to perform.
