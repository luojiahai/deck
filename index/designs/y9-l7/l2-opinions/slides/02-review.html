L2 · 我觉得 Review · Subjects + 门 (0–8 min)
0–8 min · Warm-Up Review
回顾 · School Subjects 科目

Activity Flow

  1. Teacher flashes 10 subject characters — class reads chorally
  2. Cold call: 5 students name a subject from a picture
  3. Sentence drill: 我今年学___门课。 — 3–4 students answer in full
  4. One student lists 3 subjects with 门: 一共三门课
MODEL SENTENCE
我学英语、数学和汉语，一共三门课。
