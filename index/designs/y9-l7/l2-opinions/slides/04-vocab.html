L2 · 我觉得 I Do · New Vocabulary · 8–14 min
8–14 min · I Do · Introduce
New Vocabulary · 新词汇
觉得
juéde
feel; think (that)
Opinion verb — always follows subject: 我觉得……
有用
yǒuyòng
useful
有 (have) + 用 (use) → lit. "has use"
难
nán
difficult; hard
Pairs with verbs → 难学, 难写
容易
róngyì
easy
Pairs with verbs → 容易学, 容易写
写
xiě
write
Action verb for characters
✍️
Remember 学 (xué · learn/study) from L1 — both 写 and 学 combine with 难 and 容易 to form compounds!
