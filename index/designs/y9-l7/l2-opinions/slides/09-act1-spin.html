20–28 min · We Do
科目转盘 · Subject Spin
Activity Flow · 活动流程
  • 1
    Teacher projects subject picture one by one (13 rounds)
  • 2
    Whole class generates a sentence chorally using the frames
  • 3
    Cold call 2 individuals per subject for OTR
  • 4
    Extension: add 但是 for contrast
TEACHER MODEL

Extension 延伸： 我觉得物理有用，但是物理很难学。
