Exit Ticket · 出门票

出门票 · Before You Leave
用"觉得"写两句话：
一句说有用，
一句说难学或容易学。
Write 2 sentences: one saying something is useful, one saying something is difficult/easy to learn.
Volunteer share: 2 students read their exit sentences aloud — class gives thumbs up/down on correctness
