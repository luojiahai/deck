Example Sentence 例句 Structure Note 结构 Pattern Tag
我觉得汉语很有用。
Subject + 觉得 + topic + 很 + adjective
 Basic Opinion
我觉得数学很难学。
Compound adjective fills the 很 + adj slot
 Compound: 难 + verb
汉字不容易写。
不 before 容易 → negation without 觉得
 Negation: 不容易
我觉得英语不难学。
不 before 难 → not difficult; compound broken
 Negation: 不难
Position Rule: 觉得 comes immediately after the subject — the opinion clause follows directly.  ·  不 always goes directly before 容易 or 难, never at the end.
