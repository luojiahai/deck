大概 / 差不多 + 要 + Verb + Durationpattern: answer

你每天功课多吗？要做多长时间？
Do you have a lot of homework every day? How long does it take?
🧑
不太多，大概要做两个小时。
Not too much — it takes about two hours.
你每天睡觉要多长时间？
大概要睡八个小时。
打电话要多长时间？
差不多要打半个小时。
💡 大概 and 差不多 both mean "approximately" — interchangeable. 大概 is slightly more common in speech.
