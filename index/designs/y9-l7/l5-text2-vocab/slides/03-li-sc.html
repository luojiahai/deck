Lesson Objectives
学习目标 · Learning Intention
We Are Learning To
Ask and explain WHY we like or dislike a school subject using 为什么 and 因为.
Success Criteria — By the end of this lesson I can…
1
Recognise and write the new Text 2 vocabulary: 科目、为什么、意思、有意思、没意思、教
2
Answer a 为什么 question about a school subject using 因为我觉得……很有意思 / 没意思
3
Write my own 为什么 question about a school subject
