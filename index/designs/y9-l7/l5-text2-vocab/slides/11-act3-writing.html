L5 · 为什么？因为！ You Do · Act.8: Written 为什么 Q&A (33–38 min)
33–38 min · You Do · Act. 8
书写练习 · Written 为什么 Q&A
Sentence Frame · 句型框架
为什么你喜欢/不喜欢 科目？
因为我觉得 科目 很 　。
Part A
3 min
Teacher displays 6 subject-based question prompts
Students write a complete 因为 answer sentence for each question prompt in their exercise books.
Part B
2 min
Students write their own questions
Write 2 of their own 为什么 questions about any school subject.
After Part A: cold call 2–3 students to read written answer aloud. If errors → write on board, class corrects together.
Accept one-clause answers; extension students use 因为……所以……
Scaffold · 支架
Sentence frame stays on projector. Accept one-clause 因为 answers.
Extension · 延伸
Part B: write 4 questions. Part A answers use 因为……所以…… AND 对……感兴趣.
Sample Q&A Pairs · 示例问答
Q 问句
你为什么喜欢历史？
A 回答
因为我觉得历史很有意思。
Q 问句
你为什么不喜欢物理？
A 回答
因为我觉得物理很难学，没意思。
