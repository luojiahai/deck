Procedure · 步骤
1
Teacher pronounces each word ×2 → class chorals ×2
2
Students write 3-column entry in exercise books: 汉字 | 拼音 | 英文
3
After writing, teacher points to hanzi without pinyin → cold call: "怎么读？"
4
After all 6 words: rapid quiz → choral response for each word
Pinyin visible while writing → hidden for quiz only
Cover pinyin column with hand or piece of paper during the rapid quiz round
Scaffold · 支架
Pinyin stays on projector while students write; removed only when pointing for cold-call recall.
