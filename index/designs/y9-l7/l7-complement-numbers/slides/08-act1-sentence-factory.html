Activity Subject Complement

Word Bank · Complements
很好  /  很快  /  很慢  /  不太好  /  非常好
Activity Steps
1
Teacher models Row 1: 他滑冰滑得很好。 Students write. (1 min)
2
Students write independently for Rows 2–3. (3 min)
3
Turn-and-talk: 你经常滑冰吗？你滑冰滑得好吗？ (2 min)
4
Cold call 3 students to report partner's answer. (2 min)
5
Students write 2 more free-choice sentences. (3 min)
Scaffold
Sentence frame stays visible on board. Use word bank for complements.
Extension
Add frequency clause: 她不经常跑步，但是跑步跑得很快. Add reason with 因为.
