L7 · 动词 + 得 Learning Intention & Success Criteria · 8–10 min
8–10 min · LI & SC
学习目标 & 成功标准
Learning
Intention
We are learning to describe how well people do things using 动词 + 得 + 补语, and to express approximate quantities using consecutive digits.
Success Criteria · 成功标准 — I can…
1
我能用"动词 + 得 + 补语"造句 I can make a verb + 得 + complement sentence about an activity  (e.g. 他滑冰滑得很好，她跑步跑得很快)
2
我能翻译含相邻数字的约数句子 I can translate sentences with approximate consecutive-number patterns  (e.g. 两三次、五六个、一两百)
3
我能用"经常"和"常常"表示频率 I can use 经常 and 常常 to describe frequency
