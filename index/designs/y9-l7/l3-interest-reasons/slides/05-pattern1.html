16–20 min · I Do
句型一 · Pattern 1: 对……感兴趣
Structure 主语 + 对 + 话题 + 很 + 感兴趣
我对历史很感兴趣。
I am very interested in history.
她对音乐很感兴趣。
She is very interested in music.
我对数学不太感兴趣。
I'm not very interested in maths. (negation: 不太)
❌ Common Error
我在历史很感兴趣。
在 = in / at a place
History is not a location — 在 is for places, not topics.
✓ Correct Form
我对历史很感兴趣。
对 = towards / regarding
对 marks the topic of interest. Always use 对, not 在.
