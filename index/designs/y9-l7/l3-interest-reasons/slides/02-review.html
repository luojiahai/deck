Teacher Models

Sequence

  1. Project 6 subjects — choral response (2 rounds): teacher points + gestures → class responds with full opinion sentence
  2. Cold call ×4 — individual students produce a full 觉得 sentence
  3. Turn-and-talk (30 sec) — 告诉旁边同学：你觉得哪个科目最难？
