Learning Intention
We are learning to name school subjects in Chinese and count them correctly using the measure word 门.
1
I can name at least ten school subjects in Chinese with correct pronunciation 能说出至少十个科目名称，发音正确
2
I can use the measure word 门 correctly to count subjects — not 个 能正确使用量词"门"而不用"个"
3
I can say which subjects I study this year in a complete sentence 能用完整句子说：我今年学__门课：……
