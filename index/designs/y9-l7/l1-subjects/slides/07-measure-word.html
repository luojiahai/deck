量词 · Measure Word 门
门
mén
measure word for subjects / courses

In Chinese, numbers need a measure word before the noun

Just like English says "two slices of bread" or "three sheets of paper" — Chinese uses 门 when counting academic subjects or courses.

✗ 一个课 → ✓ 一门课
⚠️
Most common error: using 个 (gè) instead of 门
个 is the generic measure word — it does NOT work for subjects. Use 门.
💡
门 also means "door / gate" — imagine school subjects as doors of knowledge!
