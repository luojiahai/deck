How to Play — 5–6 Rounds

1
Display all 15 subjects (汉字 only)
2
Screen goes blank — students write as many as they remember
3
Grid returns with ONE hidden — teams race to name the missing subject
4
Correct answer = 1 point. Repeat!

Scaffold

Rounds 1–2: pinyin visible in the hidden box only (as a hint)

Extension

Fluent students must use the missing subject in a full sentence before the point is awarded:
我今年学生物。
Prioritise new subjects (物理化学生物地理美术戏剧法语日语) in later rounds
