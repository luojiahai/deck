新科目 · New Subjects (Text 1, p.61)
物理
wùlǐ
physics
物 things + 理 reason → physics: the "reason behind things"
生物
shēngwù
biology
生 life + 物 things → biology: the "study of living things"
化学
huàxué
chemistry
化 change + 学 study → chemistry: "study of change"
地理
dìlǐ
geography
地 earth + 理 reason → geography: the "reason of the earth"
Memory tip: 物理 and 地理 both end in 理 (lǐ) — they're sciences that explain why things are the way they are.   |   生物 and 化学 both deal with transformations: life growing, substances changing.
