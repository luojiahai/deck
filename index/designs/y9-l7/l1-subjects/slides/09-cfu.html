Gesture Check
一门课 vs 一个课
Teacher holds up two signs — one says 门, one says 个.

Students point right hand at the correct measure word when teacher says a number + 课 phrase.
2
Cold Call
Rapid Translation
Teacher says 汉字 → student says English. Random order:

地理 = geography?   生物 = biology?   戏剧 = drama?

Also reverse: teacher says English → students write 汉字 on mini-whiteboard
3
Choral Error Correction
Spot the Mistake!
Teacher projects:
我今年学十个课 Class corrects together — loudly!
十门课！
Teacher note: Aim for 100% participation in checks 1 and 3. For cold call, pre-identify 4–5 students who need a stretch — give them the harder pairs like 化学/物理.
