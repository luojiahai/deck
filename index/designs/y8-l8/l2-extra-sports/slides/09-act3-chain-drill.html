Activity 3
Chain Drill · Naming 10 Sports
⏱ 7 min
You Do

Instructions

  1. Stand in a circle or row.
  2. First student says one sport in Chinese.
  3. Next student repeats it and adds a new one — the chain continues.
  4. Continue until someone can't recall the sequence or a new sport.
Goal: aim for 10 total sports — today's 8 + 网球 + 打网球 from Lesson 1

Scaffold
Sport flashcards laid out on the table as a visual prompt for anyone who blanks.
Extension
Advanced students must also add 跟朋友一起 before each sport, not just the bare word.
OTR
Every student in the chain must produce at least one sport aloud.
