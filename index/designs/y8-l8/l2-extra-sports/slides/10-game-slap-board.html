How to Play

1️⃣
Two students at the board (or a table with cards spread out).
2️⃣
Teacher calls out a sport in Chinese.
3️⃣
Both students slap the matching card/word.
🏆
Winner stays at the board for the next round — new challenger steps up.
📣
Teacher calls in Chinese → students slap the card
e.g. 滑雪 → both players slap the 滑雪 card
Setup
Sport cards printed and spread on a table, or written on the whiteboard in a grid.
Progression
Winner stays for the next round — new challenger comes up to try to beat them.
Call & Slap
Teacher calls a sport in Chinese — fastest correct slap wins the point.
