Pair Grammar Drill · 跟(和)……一起
⏱ 9 min
We Do → You Do

Instructions

  1. In pairs, complete 4 sentence starters using 跟(和)……一起.
  2. Then write 4 fully original sentences of your own — different sports, different people.
  3. Use the word banks on the right for reference.
Sentence Starter · 句型框架
我喜欢______一起打篮球。
Worked Example
我喜欢跟妹妹一起打篮球。

People · 人

朋友 哥哥 姐姐 同学 家人

Today's 8 Sports · 运动

打排球 打篮球 打乒乓球 打羽毛球 打高尔夫球 踢足球 滑冰 滑雪
Scaffold
Word bank of people (朋友, 哥哥, 姐姐, 同学, 家人) and today's 8 sports projected as reference.
Extension
Add a reason or frequency word from memory (经常, 每个周末) if you recall it — bonus, not required yet.
OTR
Turn and talk — each partner reads one sentence aloud before sharing with the class.
