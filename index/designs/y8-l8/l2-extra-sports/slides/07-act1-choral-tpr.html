L2 · MORE SPORTS Activity 1 · We Do
Activity 1
Choral + TPR Picture Drill
⏱ 7 min
We Do

Instructions

  1. Teacher projects each of the 8 sport pictures in random order.
  2. Class chorally calls out the Chinese word.
  3. Then mimes the action — swing a racket, kick, skate.

Word Bank · 词库

打排球play volleyball
打篮球play basketball
打乒乓球play table tennis
打羽毛球play badminton
打高尔夫球play golf
踢足球play football / soccer
滑冰ice skate
滑雪ski
Scaffold
Pinyin stays visible under each picture for the first pass.
Extension
On the second pass, hide pinyin — students rely on the picture and character only.
OTR
Whole-class choral response + mime on every picture.
