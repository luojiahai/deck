⏱ 4 min
You Do

Instructions

  1. Teacher says a duration phrase in English — e.g. "five days", "three months"
  2. First student translates it aloud into Chinese
  3. That student then says a new duration phrase in English for the next student
  4. Continue round the room — every student must respond
Extension: combine your duration with a sport — 他每天跑三个小时步。
