Where does the duration word go?
动词 Verb
+
时长 Duration
+
宾语 Object
跑步 and 游泳 are two-part words (verb + object) — the duration slides in between the two parts. No 的 needed.
他每天跑一个小时步。
He runs for an hour every day.
她每天游半个小时泳。
She swims for half an hour every day.
他每天打一个小时篮球。
He plays basketball for an hour every day. (same pattern)
✗他每天跑步一个小时。
✗他每天跑一个小时的步。
"跑步 and 游泳 are actually two-part words — verb + object glued together. When I add a duration, it slides in between the two parts, and unlike some other duration sentences you've seen, these usually drop 的."
