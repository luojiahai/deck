Quick translate: "I don't really like basketball."
我不太喜欢打篮球。
I don't really like basketball.
2
Does 但是 mean "and"?
No — 但是 means "but"
但是 expresses contrast, not addition.
3
Error correction: 我喜欢不太运动。
Word order — should be 我不太喜欢运动。
不太 goes right before 喜欢, not after the verb it's connected to.
