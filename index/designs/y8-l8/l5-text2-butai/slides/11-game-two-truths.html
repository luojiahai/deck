L5 · TV · MOVIES · 不太喜欢 Game · Two Truths, One Lie · 38–43 min
Game
Two Truths, One Lie · 两真一假
⏱ 5 min
Groups of 3–4

How to Play

  1. In small groups of 3–4
  2. Each student says three sentences about their own likes/dislikes using 喜欢 / 不太喜欢 — two true, one false
  3. The group guesses which is the lie by asking follow-up questions in Chinese where possible

What It Might Sound Like

1我不太喜欢运动，但是我喜欢游泳。
2我很喜欢运动。我还喜欢看电视、看电影。
3我差不多每个周末都看电影。
The group discusses which one is false — asking follow-up questions in Chinese where possible.
Setup
None needed — purely oral
Group size
Groups of 3–4
OTR
Every student in each group produces three sentences and asks/answers follow-up questions
