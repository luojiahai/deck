How to say what sport you like — and which verb to use
我喜欢
+
运动
→
"I like to [sport]"
我喜欢跑步。
I like running / jogging.
我喜欢游泳。
I like swimming.
我喜欢跟朋友一起打网球。
I like playing tennis together with my friend.
我喜欢跟我的猫一起打网球！silly example
I like playing tennis with my cat!
Verb Rule · 动词规则
打 + racket sport
打网球 dǎ wǎngqiú — "hit tennis"
No verb — already a verb
跑步 · 游泳 complete action-words on their own
