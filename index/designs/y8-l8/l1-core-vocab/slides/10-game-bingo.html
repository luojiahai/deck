How to Play

1️⃣
Each student fills a 3×3 grid with today's 7 words — their choice of squares, some repeat.
2️⃣
Teacher calls out English meanings or pinyin, one at a time. Find & cross off the matching 汉字 square.
3️⃣
Three in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) — call out 宾狗 (Bingo)!
📣
Teacher calls English/pinyin → students cross off 汉字
e.g. "swim" / yóuyǒng → students cross off 游泳
Scaffold
Today's 7-word list stays visible on the board so students can double-check before crossing off
Extension
Students take turns as the caller, choosing English or pinyin prompts for the class
Setup
Blank grids (photocopied) + markers/pens — students fill in any 7 of today's words themselves
