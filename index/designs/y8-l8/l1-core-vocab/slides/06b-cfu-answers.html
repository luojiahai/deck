What's the verb for 网球?
网球 needs 打 in front — 打网球
dǎ wǎngqiú — "hit tennis". 打 is used for racket/ball sports.

How to say "swim" in Chinese

yóuyǒng
3
Error check: 我打跑步。 What's wrong?
跑步 never takes a verb in front — should be 我跑步。
跑步 and 游泳 are already complete action-words — no 打 needed.
