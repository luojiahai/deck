Learning Intention
We are learning to correctly place frequency adverbs (经常/常常/差不多) before the verb, and use 但是 to contrast experiences.
1
I can say 经常 and 常常 and place them correctly before the verb, not after
2
I can rewrite a 常常 sentence as a 差不多每天都 sentence
3
I can make a 但是 sentence about places I have and haven't been — 去过……但是没去过……
