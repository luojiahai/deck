Activity 2
但是 Experience Sentences
⏱ 6 min
You Do

Instructions

  1. You'll receive 3 experience checklists of places visited (✓) and not visited (✗)
  2. Write a sentence for each checklist using 去过……但是没去过……
  3. Use the worked example as your model

Worked Example · 例句

我去过英国和美国，但是没去过日本。
I've been to the UK and the US, but haven't been to Japan.
Scaffold
The worked textbook example stays projected as a model to adapt
Extension
Students write one checklist and sentence about their own real travel experience
OTR
Cold call 4–5 students to read one sentence aloud
