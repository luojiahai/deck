Key Sentence Patterns · 关键句型
Where adverbs go — and how to contrast experiences with 但是

Frequency Adverb Placement

Subject + 经常/常常/差不多 + Verb + Object
我经常运动。I often exercise.
我常常看电影。I often watch movies.
我差不多每天都游泳。I swim almost every day.
The adverb sits directly before the verb — never at the end of the sentence.

但是 Experience Sentences

我去过____，但是没去过____。
我去过英国和美国，但是没去过日本。 I've been to the UK and the US, but haven't been to Japan.
去过 = "have been to" (met earlier) — today's focus is joining two experiences with 但是.
