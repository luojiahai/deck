Key Sentence Pattern · 关键句型

Duration Q&A · 提问时长

Q你每天/每个星期 + verb + 几个小时 + object？
A我每天/每个星期 + verb + duration + object。
你每天跑几个小时步？我每天跑一个小时步。
How many hours do you jog every day? → I jog for 1 hour every day.
你每个星期打几个小时网球？我每个星期打三个小时网球。
How many hours a week do you play tennis? → I play tennis for 3 hours a week.

正在 Recap · 现在进行时

Subject + 正在 + Verb (+ Object)→ "is [verb]-ing right now"
他正在打篮球。
He is playing basketball right now.
她正在跑步。
She is jogging right now.
你正在打乒乓球吗？不，我正在写作业！silly · pause for reactions
Are you playing ping-pong right now? No, I'm doing homework!
