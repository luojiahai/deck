Instructions

  1. Teacher shows each people-doing-sport picture one at a time — Act.1 (p.71) + Act.10 (p.76) pictures combined into one set
  2. Students first write a 正在 sentence describing the picture on their whiteboard
  3. Once every picture has been written, teacher calls out phrases at random
  4. Students act out (TPR) the matching picture's action
