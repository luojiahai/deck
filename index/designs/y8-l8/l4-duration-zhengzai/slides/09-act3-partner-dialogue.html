Activity 3
Written Partner Dialogue
⏱ 7 min
You Do

Instructions

  1. In pairs, write a 3–4 exchange dialogue asking and answering about sport duration for two different sports
  2. Swap papers with your partner to check for errors
  3. Read your dialogue aloud together

Model Dialogue · Reference Structure

A
你每天跑几个小时步？
B
我每天跑一个小时步。
A
你每个星期打几个小时网球？
B
我每个星期打三个小时网球。
Scaffold
A model dialogue stays projected as a reference structure to adapt.
Extension
Use one 打-sport, one 踢/no-verb sport, and vary the time frame (每天 vs 每个星期) across your dialogue.
OTR
Partner peer-check counts as a response for every student; 2–3 pairs read their dialogue to the class.
