示范翻译 · Worked Translation Example
Watch how the teacher thinks through word order before writing the Chinese.
Translate"I sleep at 9:30 pm."
1
"Time first or last? In English, the time comes after the verb — 'I sleep at 9:30pm.'"
2
"In Chinese, time comes before the verb — time-of-day, then clock time, then the verb."
3
"So: 我 (I) + 晚上 (evening) + 九点半 (9:30) + 睡觉 (sleep)."
我 + 晚上 + 九点半 + 睡觉
Final Answer
我晚上九点半睡觉。
