L8 · 复习 I Do · Cumulative Dialogue · 10–20 min
10–20 min · I Do · Ex11, p.85
综合对话示范 · Cumulative Dialogue Model
One full dialogue combining greetings, routine, activities, and bedtime — nearly every pattern from the unit.
Part 1 · Greeting & Morning · 问候与起床
A
早上好！你叫什么名字？
Zǎoshang hǎo! Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi? · Good morning! What's your name?
B
我叫王小明。
Wǒ jiào Wáng Xiǎomíng. · My name is Wang Xiaoming.
A
你早上几点起床？
Nǐ zǎoshang jǐ diǎn qǐchuáng? · What time do you get up?
B
我早上六点半起床，六点三刻吃早饭。
... liù diǎn bàn qǐchuáng, liù diǎn sān kè chī zǎofàn. · I get up at 6:30, breakfast at 6:45.
Part 2 · School, Afternoon & Night · 上学、放学与睡觉
A
你几点去上学？
Nǐ jǐ diǎn qù shàngxué? · What time do you go to school?
B
我七点一刻去上学，坐校车。
... qī diǎn yī kè qù shàngxué, zuò xiàochē. · I go at 7:15, by school bus.
A
你们下午几点放学？
Nǐmen xiàwǔ jǐ diǎn fàngxué? · What time do classes finish?
B
我们下午三点半放学。放学以后，我打篮球。
... sān diǎn bàn fàngxué. Fàngxué yǐhòu, wǒ dǎ lánqiú. · Finish at 3:30. After, I play basketball.
A
你晚上几点睡觉？
Nǐ wǎnshang jǐ diǎn shuìjiào? · What time do you go to bed?
B
我晚上九点半睡觉。
Wǒ wǎnshang jiǔ diǎn bàn shuìjiào. · I go to bed at 9:30pm.
