L7 · 采访 I Do · Modelling: Notes → Report · 10–20 min
10–20 min · I Do · Teacher Model
示范 · Notes → Report Sentence
Teacher asks a volunteer all 8 questions, then models turning the notes into a report.
Step 1 · Ask & Note
Teacher asks the volunteer all 8 questions and writes the answers on the board in note form — keywords only, not full sentences.
7点 起床
7点半 早饭
……
→
Step 2 · Notes → Sentence
The notes become one flowing report sentence:
他/她早上七点起床，七点半吃早饭……
🔁
Person switch: the interview uses 你 (you) — the written report uses 他 (he) or 她 (she).
