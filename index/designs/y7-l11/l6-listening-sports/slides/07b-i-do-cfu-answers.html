🔑 Answer Key
1
「我下午四点半___。」
✅ Any sports activity fits! e.g. 打篮球 / 看书 / 做作业 / 打网球 — as long as it's a verb + activity.
2
「我晚上七点游泳。」
✅ Not wrong — just picture-check it! Unusual timing, but pools can be indoor, so it's grammatically fine.
3
Example sentences teacher might say:
我下午四点打篮球 → 👍 makes sense
我晚上十二点打网球 → 👎 odd — very late, courts are closed
