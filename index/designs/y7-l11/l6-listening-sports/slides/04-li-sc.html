Learning Intention
We are learning to listen for a.m./p.m. context and combine our daily-routine sentences with activities we already know.
1
I can listen and correctly tick whether a time is a.m. or p.m.
听音判断上午还是下午/晚上
2
I can make a sentence combining a time + an activity from earlier units.
用时间+活动造句，例：我下午四点打篮球
3
I can spot an error in a combined routine-and-activity sentence.
找出句子里的错误
