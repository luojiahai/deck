10–20 min · I Do · Key Sentence Patterns
Statements · 陈述句
我/我们 + 时间 + 动词。
PatternExample
我早上X点起床。I get up at X in the morning.
我X点一刻去上学。I go to school at quarter past X.
我们X点上课。We attend class at X.
我们下午X点半放学。Classes finish at half past X in the afternoon.
我晚上X点半睡觉。I go to bed at half past X in the evening.
