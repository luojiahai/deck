10–20 min · I Do · Key Sentence Patterns
Questions · 疑问句
PatternExample
你早上几点起床？What time do you get up in the morning?
你几点吃早饭？What time do you eat breakfast?
你们下午几点放学？What time do you (pl.) finish school in the afternoon?
你几点睡觉？What time do you go to bed?
💬 Model 2–3 Q&A pairs live with the class
Think-Aloud · 转换思路
Replace the exact time with 几点, and move 你 into the 我 spot.
我早上六点半起床 → 你早上几点起床？
👥
他/她 — describing someone else
他早上七点起床。 Only the pronoun changes — the rest of the sentence stays the same!
