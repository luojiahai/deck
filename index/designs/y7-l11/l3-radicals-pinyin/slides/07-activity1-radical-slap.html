Radical Slap · 拍部首
Two sets of the 6 radical cards, spread on the table. Two students race to slap the correct one!

🙋 2 students race at the front table 👂 Teacher calls a meaning OR shows the example character ✋ First to slap the correct radical wins! 🔄 Winner stays up — rotate through the whole class in pairs
Scaffold
Radical table stays projected as a reference throughout
Extension
