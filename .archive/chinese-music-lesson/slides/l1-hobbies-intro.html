写字: 爱 (ài)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
爱
Radical 爫 (zhuǎ)
Strokes 10 strokes
Pinyin ài
English love; hobby (in 爱好)
爱好 àihào = hobby
我爱音乐 Wǒ ài yīnyuè = I love music
✋ Air-trace the character with your finger!

写字: 音 (yīn)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
音
Radical 音 (yīn) — sound
Strokes 9 strokes
Pinyin yīn
English sound
音乐 yīnyuè = music
声音 shēngyīn = sound / voice
✋ Air-trace the character with your finger!

写字: 乐 (yuè)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
乐
Radical 丿 (piě)
Strokes 5 strokes
Pinyin yuè / lè
English music (yuè); happy (lè)
音乐 yīnyuè = music (yuè)
快乐 kuàilè = happy (lè)
✋ Air-trace the character with your finger!

写字: 好 (hǎo)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
好
Radical 女 (nǚ)
Strokes 6 strokes
Pinyin hǎo
English good; (in 爱好: hobby)
爱好 àihào = hobby
你好 nǐ hǎo = hello
✋ Air-trace the character with your finger!

写字: 唱 (chàng)

Xiě zì · Character Writing
唱

Strokes 11 strokes
Pinyin chàng
English to sing
