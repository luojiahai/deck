Model Dialogue

课文对话 · Textbook Dialogue

A
你每天看几个小时的书?
Nǐ měitiān kàn jǐge xiǎoshí de shū?
How many hours a day do you read?
B
我每天看两个小时的书。
Wǒ měitiān kàn liǎngge xiǎoshí de shū.
I read books for two hours every day.
A
你住在哪儿?
Nǐ zhù zài nǎr?
Where do you live?
B
我住在墨尔本。
Wǒ zhù zài Mò'ěrběn.
I live in Melbourne.
A
你每天做什么?
Nǐ měitiān zuò shénme?
What do you do every day?
B
我每天读书、听音乐、唱歌。
Wǒ měitiān dúshū, tīng yīnyuè, chàng gē.
I read, listen to music, and sing every day.

Extension

扩展问题 · Extended Questions

正在 pattern
你现在正在做什么?
Nǐ xiànzài zhèngzài zuò shénme?
What are you doing RIGHT NOW?
都 pattern
唱歌、弹钢琴，你都会吗?
Chàng gē, tán gāngqín, nǐ dōu huì ma?
Can you do both — sing and play piano?
一边...一边... pattern
你有时候一边听音乐一边看书吗?
Nǐ yǒushíhou yībiān tīng yīnyuè yībiān kàn shū ma?
Do you sometimes listen to music while reading?
Add these to your interview! Use at least 5 questions.

Writing

写一段话 · Write a Paragraph

Choose ONE person you interviewed. Write 4-6 sentences about them.
You MUST include:
at least one 正在 sentence at least one 都 sentence at least one 一边...一边... sentence
Example 我的朋友叫小明。他住在墨尔本。他的爱好是唱歌和弹钢琴，这两样他都会。他现在正在看书。他有时候一边听音乐一边看书。

Grammar Check

范文分析 · Example Analysis

✓ 正在
✓ 都
✓ 一边...一边...
Grammar checklist — make sure all three appear in your paragraph!
我的朋友叫小明。他住在墨尔本。他的爱好是唱歌和弹钢琴，这两样他都会。他现在正在看书。他有时候一边听音乐一边看书。
都 pattern 他都会 — he can do both (sing AND play piano)
正在 pattern 他正在看书 — he is reading RIGHT NOW
一边 pattern 一边听音乐一边看书 — listening to music AND reading at the same time
