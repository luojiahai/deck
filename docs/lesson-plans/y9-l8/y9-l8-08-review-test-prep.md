# Review + Test 3.2 Prep — School Facilities 学校设施

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 8 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to consolidate all Lesson 8 vocabulary and sentence patterns, and to prepare accurate written responses for Test 3.2.

## Success Criteria

- 我能翻译Lesson 8所有测验词汇（中文↔英文）(I can translate all Test 3.2 vocabulary in both directions)
- 我能正确写出18个名词的量词 (I can correctly write the measure word for all 18 Test 3.2 practice nouns)
- 我能用"X在Y的……"句型写出完整、正确语序的句子 (I can write complete, correctly-ordered sentences using the location-word pattern)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (display vocabulary grid, patterns, quiz questions, sentence frames, exit ticket)
- Students: exercise books and pens
- Teacher: scoreboard drawn on whiteboard corner for team quiz

---

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Rapid recall of core Lesson 8 vocabulary and the two key sentence patterns.

**How:** Project 10 quick-fire prompts one at a time. For vocabulary prompts (items 1–7), display the Chinese word and cold-call one student, then confirm with a choral repeat. For sentence-pattern prompts (items 8–10), display the sentence starter and cold-call a student to complete it; class echoes the full sentence chorally.

Quick-fire sequence:
1. 图书馆 → *library*
2. 实验室 → *laboratory*
3. 隔壁 → *next door*
4. 楼上 → *upstairs*
5. 停车场 → *car park*
6. 校医室 → *school clinic*
7. 中间 → *between; centre*
8. Starter: 我们学校有五______教学楼。→ *幢* (choral echo of full sentence)
9. Starter: 汉语教室在法语教室的…… → *旁边*（elicit completion, choral echo）
10. Starter: 校长办公室在教师办公室和校医室的…… → *中间*（cold call + choral echo）

OTR: cold call (individual) + choral echo (whole class) on every item.

**Connection:** 今天是第八节课。我们复习所有的内容，然后练习考试会考的问题。这节课结束后你们就准备好考测验3.2了。

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Display the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria on the projector. Teacher reads each line in Chinese; class echoes chorally. Point out that the three SC map directly onto what Test 3.2 will assess. Leave SC visible on a corner of the board for the remainder of the lesson.

**Delivery (10–14 min, 4 min):** Display the full grid with the English column initially hidden. Students write the English meaning for each word in their exercise books. After 2 minutes, reveal the English column row by row — students self-check. Teacher calls attention to the four ⭐ words and writes them on the board corner.

#### Key Sentence Pattern Summary

Display both patterns with modelled examples. Students copy into exercise books.

| # | Pattern | Modelled example |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 个/幢 — measure words for facilities | 一个图书馆。五幢教学楼。 |
| 2 | 层 = 楼 (floor levels) | 三层 = 三楼。 |
| 3 | X 在 Y 的 + 位置词 | 图书馆在体育馆的对面。 |
| 4 | 楼上/楼下 (location) vs 上楼/下楼 (movement) | 教室在楼上。/ 我们现在要上楼。 |

**Delivery (14–16 min, 2 min):** Flash each row; teacher reads aloud, class repeats chorally. Point out the Pattern 3 common error: position word must never come before 在.

#### CFU Checkpoints
- After vocabulary grid: "Hold up your exercise book — show me your translation for 校长室 and 游泳馆." (Hold-up-book; scan for errors.)
- After patterns: "Give me ONE sentence using X在Y的旁边." — cold call two students, class echoes the better one chorally.
- Error check: Project 操场旁边在体育馆。 "找出错误！" — cold call one student to fix it.

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 量词书写练习（引导）— We Do (8 min) | 20–28 min

**What:** Guided walkthrough of the Test 3.2 measure-word writing format, using 6 of the 18 practice nouns.

**How:**
- Project the six items one at a time: 一__狗, 一__鱼, 一__桌子, 一__车, 一__帽子, 一__教学楼.
- Teacher models the first two aloud, thinking through the category each noun belongs to: "'狗'是动物——用'只'。" (一只狗) "'桌子'是家具——用'张'。" (一张桌子)
- Turn-and-talk (2 min): partners work out the remaining four together (一条鱼, 一辆车, 一顶帽子, 一幢教学楼).
- Cold call four students, one item each; class echoes the correct answer chorally.

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** Turn-and-talk requires every student to attempt all four remaining items.

- **Scaffold:** Project a word bank of the six correct measure words (只, 张, 条, 辆, 顶, 幢) in scrambled order alongside the nouns.
- **Extension:** Students must justify their choice for one item aloud, explaining the noun category (e.g. "'车'是交通工具，交通工具用'辆'。").

---

#### Activity 2: 计时练习 Timed Practice Test — You Do (10 min) | 28–38 min

**What:** Independent, timed practice mirroring the Test 3.2 format — no talking, silent individual work.

**How:**
1. **Measure-word writing (5 min):** Project the remaining 12 nouns from the Test 3.2 list (一__马, 一__牛, 一__椅子, 一__花, 一__手套, 一__钢琴, 一__汉语课, 一__课, 一__鞋, 一__西服, 一__老师, 一__衣服). Students write the correct measure word for each in their exercise books, working independently against a visible 5-minute countdown.
2. **Short written description (5 min):** Students write a short paragraph (4–5 sentences) describing their own school, using at least one measure word (个/幢), one 层=楼 reference, and one X在Y的位置词 sentence.
3. Teacher reveals the measure-word answers on the projector; students self-mark part 1. Part 2 is collected for teacher review (not marked in class).

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** All students complete both timed sections independently.

- **Scaffold:** For part 2, keep a sentence frame projected: 我们学校有__幢教学楼。教室在__层。图书馆在__的__。
- **Extension:** For part 2, students must write 6+ sentences without the frame and use at least one ⭐ test-variant word (校长室, 游泳馆, 体育场, or 运动场) correctly.

---

#### Game: 复习问答接力 Review Relay (5 min) | 38–43 min

**What:** A short, high-pace team relay reviewing vocabulary and the location-word pattern before the final written check.

**Setup:** Divide the class into 4 teams (reuse groupings from earlier lessons if convenient). Teacher draws/reuses the scoreboard on the whiteboard corner. Teams raise hands to buzz in; teacher cold-calls if no hands after 5 seconds.

**Round 1 — 词汇闪卡 (3 questions, ~1 min):** 校医室 → *school clinic*; 游泳馆 → *swimming pool (test variant)*; 楼上 → *upstairs*.

**Round 2 — 改错句 (2 questions, ~2 min):** 图书馆旁边在礼堂。→ *图书馆在礼堂的旁边。*; 停车场中间在教学楼和操场。→ *停车场在教学楼和操场的中间。*

**Round 3 — 量词问答 (3 questions, ~2 min):** 一__帽子 → *顶*; 一__铅笔 → *支*; 一__教学楼 → *幢*.

- **Scaffold:** Allow teams 5 seconds to confer before answering on Round 2.
- **Extension:** Bonus point in Round 2 if the team also names which word was mis-ordered ("位置词'旁边'放错位置了").

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Display the three SC on projector. Teacher reads each one in Chinese; students rate themselves silently: 👍 ready / ✋ getting there / 👎 need more practice. Quick show of hands for each rating — teacher notes which SC has the most 👎 responses (signals what to address before Test 3.2).

- **Self-assessment:** Students write a single Chinese sentence at the bottom of today's page: 我最需要复习______，因为______。Teacher cold-calls 1–2 students to share.

- **Exit Ticket:** Display on projector:
  > 用中文写两三句话：介绍你们学校的一个设施，说说它在哪儿。
  >
  > (Write 2–3 sentences in Chinese: introduce one facility at our school and say where it is.)

  Students write in exercise books. Teacher circulates and reads over shoulders — look for correct measure words and correct X在Y的位置词 order. This mirrors the exact format Test 3.2 will expect.
