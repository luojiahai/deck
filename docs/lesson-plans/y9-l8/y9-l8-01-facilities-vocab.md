# Year 9 — 学校设施 (Seq. 1 of 8): Introducing Our School + Measure Words 个/幢

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 1 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to name school facilities in Chinese and introduce our school using 我们学校有……

## Success Criteria

- I can name at least eight school facilities in Chinese (能说出至少八个学校设施的名称)
- I can use the correct measure word for buildings and facilities — 个 for most facilities, 幢 for whole buildings (能正确使用量词"个"和"幢")
- I can introduce my school in a complete sentence using 我们学校有…… (能用"我们学校有……"介绍我们的学校)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** The measure word 门 (from Lesson 7, for counting subjects) and the four key opinion patterns from the Lesson 7 review lesson, as a bridge into today's new measure words.

**How:**

*Part A — Numbers warm-up (2 min):* Project numerals 1–20 in a scrambled grid. Cold call students at random to read a numeral aloud in Chinese; class echoes chorally. This primes students for counting facilities later in the lesson (五幢教学楼, 两个篮球场, etc.).

*Part B — 门 recall + pattern flash (6 min):* Project: 我今年学十__课。 Cold call one student to fill the blank ("门!"). Class echoes: "十门课！" Then rapid-fire flash four Lesson 7 sentence starters (对……感兴趣 / 觉得 / 因为……所以…… / verb+得), cold call one student per starter, class echoes the completed sentence.

**Connection:** "科目用'门'数——一门课，两门课。但是今天我们不谈科目了，我们要谈学校本身。学校的建筑物用什么量词呢？今天你们会学到两个新的量词。"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud in Chinese; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave these visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson. Teacher: "到今天结束，你们应该都能向别人介绍我们的学校。"

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Display each row on the projector: 汉字 → pinyin → English. Teacher says the word twice; class repeats twice. After every four words, do one quick choral sweep of all words so far.

**New — Text 1 (p. 70–71):**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 介绍 | jièshào | introduce |
| 教学楼 | jiàoxué lóu | teaching building |
| 图书馆 | túshūguǎn | library |
| 礼堂 | lǐtáng | auditorium; hall |
| 操场 | cāochǎng | playground; sports field |
| 体育馆 | tǐyùguǎn | gymnasium |
| 篮球场 | lánqiúchǎng | basketball court |
| 足球场 | zúqiúchǎng | football pitch |
| 餐厅 | cāntīng | dining hall; canteen |
| 游泳池 | yóuyǒngchí | swimming pool |
| 停车场 | tíngchēchǎng | car park |

*Pronunciation tip:* 馆 (guǎn, 3rd tone) appears in both 图书馆 and 体育馆 — drill this character in isolation first, then in both compounds. Point out the pattern: X场 (chǎng) = an open field/court for X — 篮球场, 足球场, 操场, 停车场 all follow this compound pattern, so 场 does not need its own measure word.

*Component breakdown (quick mention, not deep teaching):* 介 (mediate) + 绍 (carry on) = 介绍 "introduce"; 图 (picture) + 书 (book) + 馆 (hall) = 图书馆 "library"; 礼 (ceremony) + 堂 (hall) = 礼堂 "auditorium"; 操 (exercise) + 场 (field) = 操场 "sports field"; 游泳 (swim) + 池 (pool) = 游泳池 "swimming pool". Point these out briefly while presenting the vocabulary table above — one sentence each is enough.

#### Key Sentence Pattern

**Structure:** 我来介绍一下我们学校。我们学校有…… (list facilities with measure words).

**Modelled examples (project each one, teacher reads aloud, class repeats):**

1. 我来介绍一下我们学校。我们学校有五幢教学楼、一个图书馆、一个礼堂、一个操场。
2. 我们学校还有一个餐厅、一个游泳池和一个停车场。
3. (Silly example — wait for reaction:) 我们学校有一百个足球场！

**Measure words — taught explicitly:**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| 个 | gè | default for most rooms/facilities: 一个图书馆, 一个礼堂 |
| 幢 | zhuàng | whole buildings/blocks: 五幢教学楼 |

Write on projector: ❌ 一**个**教学楼 → ✅ 一**幢**教学楼。"数整栋楼用'幢'，其他的大部分用'个'。" Flag: 篮球场/足球场/操场/停车场 do not take a separate measure word — 场 is already part of the compound, so it is always 一个篮球场, not "一场篮球场".

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Gesture)** Project "五**幢**教学楼" and "五**个**教学楼" side by side. "哪个对？右手指对的那个。" Every student points.
- **(Cold call)** "Alex，'library' 用中文怎么说？" → "Priya，'swimming pool' 呢？" → "Sam，'car park'？"
- **(Choral — error correction)** Project: 我们学校有一**个**教学楼。 "这个句子有错误。哪里错了？大家一起说！" → Class calls out "一**幢**教学楼！"

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 看图说设施 — We Do (7 min) | 20–27 min

**(Based on Act. 1, p. 71)**

**What:** Display the 8 facility pictures from Act. 1 on the projector, one at a time. Students name the facility in Chinese, then build a full sentence.

**How:**
- Teacher projects each picture for 3 seconds: class chorally names the facility.
- After naming, the 汉字 fades in to confirm.
- After all 8: display all at once. Cold call individual students to point and name.
- Final round: teacher says the English name, class chorally says the Chinese — no visual support.

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** After the random-pointing round, teacher rapid-fires English names (library, gymnasium, canteen, car park, swimming pool…) and all students respond chorally each time.

- **Scaffold:** Keep pinyin visible during the first pass through all 8 pictures; remove pinyin for the second and third passes.
- **Extension:** After naming each facility, fluent speakers must immediately add the measure word and a mini-sentence: "图书馆——我们学校有一个图书馆。"

---

#### Activity 2: 我们学校有…… — You Do, Writing-Focused (10 min) | 27–37 min

**What:** Students write a short paragraph introducing their own school using the 我来介绍一下我们学校。我们学校有…… pattern, listing at least six facilities with correct measure words.

**How:**
- Teacher models on the projector: writes 3 sentences live, thinking aloud about which measure word to use for each facility ("教学楼——整栋楼——幢。图书馆——个。").
- Individual writing (6 min): students write their own paragraph in exercise books — at least six facilities, correct measure words, using 和 before the last item in each list.
- Peer-check (2 min): students swap exercise books with a neighbour and check each other's measure words, circling any 个/幢 errors in pencil.
- Cold call 2–3 students to read their paragraph aloud (2 min).

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** All students write independently; the peer-check step requires every student to read and mark a partner's work.

- **Scaffold:** Keep this sentence frame projected throughout:
  - 我来介绍一下我们学校。我们学校有__幢教学楼、__个__、__个__和__个__。
  - Word bank projected alongside: 图书馆, 礼堂, 操场, 体育馆, 篮球场, 足球场, 餐厅, 游泳池, 停车场.
- **Extension:** Students must write at least eight facilities (the full list) without the word bank, and add one personal opinion sentence using a Lesson 7 pattern: "我们学校有一个游泳池，我觉得游泳池很有用。"

---

#### Game: 量词大挑战 — Projector Relay (6 min) | 37–43 min

**How to play:**

1. **Setup (projector only):** Project a list of 8 facility nouns one at a time (no measure word shown).
2. Divide the class into two teams (left half vs right half). For each noun, teacher calls on the next student in the rotation from alternating teams to say the noun with its correct measure word out loud: "一个图书馆！"
3. Correct answer = 1 point for the team. If wrong, the other team may steal by answering correctly.
4. Keep a running score on the projector or whiteboard corner.

**What language it practises:** Rapid retrieval of measure word + noun pairing (个 vs 幢); reinforces the compound-word rule for 场 words.

**Setup needed:** Projector only — one slide listing the 8 nouns in random order; teacher can also add 教学楼 twice to test 幢 specifically.

- **Scaffold:** If a student hesitates, teacher may offer the first sound of the measure word ("g……") as a prompt before moving to the next student.
- **Extension:** Winning answers for fluent speakers must be given as a full sentence, not just "measure word + noun": "我们学校有一个图书馆。"

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria again. Teacher reads the first; a student volunteer reads the second and third. "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还不行 — for each criterion in turn. Note any students showing 1 finger on criterion 2 (个 vs 幢) for the next lesson's review.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Students write ONE complete sentence:
  > 我们学校有__幢教学楼和一个______。
  They must use the correct measure word for both blanks. Teacher circulates and reads over shoulders; tap any student who has used 个 for 教学楼 and gesture them to self-correct.

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要用今天学的设施词汇玩组词游戏，还要看学校地图，练习介绍学校的布局。"
