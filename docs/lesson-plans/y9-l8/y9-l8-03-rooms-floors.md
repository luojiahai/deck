# Year 9 — 学校设施 (Seq. 3 of 8): Rooms + Floor Levels (Text 2)

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 3 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to name school rooms in Chinese and understand people asking and telling which floor a room is on.

## Success Criteria

- I can name at least eight school rooms in Chinese (能说出至少八个教室/房间的名称)
- I understand that 层 and 楼 both mean "floor" and can be used interchangeably (理解"层"和"楼"都表示"楼层"，可以互换使用)
- I can listen to Text 2 and answer comprehension questions about where rooms are located (能听懂课文二，回答关于教室位置的问题)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- CD player — Track 31 (Text 2, p. 75)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** The word-building blocks from Lesson 2 (馆/场/池/楼/室), with a focus on 室 as the bridge into today's room vocabulary.

**How:**

*Part A — Word-building flash (4 min):* Project the five building-block characters (馆/场/池/楼/室) one at a time. Class chorally gives one facility word using each. For 室, cold call: "上节课我们猜了几个'室'的词，还记得吗？" — elicit 办公室, 音乐室, 实验室, 校医室 if remembered; if not, teacher reminds briefly.

*Part B — Measure word quick fire (4 min):* Project three facility nouns (图书馆, 教学楼, 足球场); class chorally gives the measure word + noun for each (一个图书馆, 五幢教学楼, 一个足球场).

**Connection:** "上节课我们组了'室'这个词——办公室、音乐室、实验室。今天我们正式学这些房间的词，还要学怎么问和说：这个房间在几楼？"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson.

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Display each row on the projector: 汉字 → pinyin → English. Teacher says the word twice; class repeats twice. After every four words, do one quick choral sweep.

**New — Text 2 (p. 76):**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 教室 | jiàoshì | classroom |
| 三层 = 三楼 | sān céng = sān lóu | third floor |
| 旁边 | pángbiān | beside; next to |
| 实验室 | shíyànshì | laboratory |
| 隔壁 | gébì | next door |
| 厕所 | cèsuǒ | toilet |
| 男厕所 / 女厕所 | nán cèsuǒ / nǚ cèsuǒ | men's / women's toilet |
| 左边 | zuǒbiān | left side |
| 右边 | yòubiān | right side |
| 校长 | xiàozhǎng | principal |
| 办公室 | bàngōngshì | office |
| 教师 | jiàoshī | teacher (formal) |
| 校医室 | xiàoyīshì | school clinic |
| 中间 | zhōngjiān | between; centre |

*Teaching note:* Today's focus is recognising these words and understanding 层=楼 for floor levels. The location words (旁边, 隔壁, 左边/右边, 中间) appear in this list and in Text 2 — introduce them receptively today (recognise meaning when heard/read); next lesson will drill producing them correctly in full sentences.

#### Key Sentence Pattern

**Structure:** [Room] 在 [floor]。/ [Room] 在几层？

**Modelled examples (project each one, teacher reads aloud, class repeats):**

1. 汉语教室在三层。
2. 物理实验室在哪儿？— 在二层。
3. (Silly example — wait for reaction:) 校长办公室在游泳池里面！

*Explain 层=楼:* "三层 和 三楼 意思一样，都是'third floor'。课文里两个都会看到。" Project side by side: 三层 = 三楼。

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Choral)** "'三层'和'三楼'——意思一样还是不一样？" → class calls "一样！"
- **(Cold call)** "Priya, 'laboratory' 用中文怎么说？" → "Sam, 'toilet' 呢？"
- **(Gesture)** Project 教室在几层？ and 教室在哪层？ "这两个问题意思一样吗？" (Both ask the same thing — accept either.)

#### Text / Dialogue

**Text 2 (p. 75, CD Track 31) — displayed on projector with pinyin:**

> 新生1：汉语教室在几层？
> 小明：在三层，在法语教室的旁边。
> 新生2：物理实验室在哪儿？
> 小明：在一号教学楼的二层，在音乐室的隔壁。
> 新生1：厕所在哪儿？
> 小明：在一楼。男厕所在左边，女厕所在右边。
> 新生2：校长办公室在哪儿？
> 小明：在五号教学楼一层，在教师办公室和校医室的中间。

Teacher reads the dialogue aloud once at normal pace (roles voiced distinctly); class follows along with pinyin visible. Play CD Track 31. Then ask 3 comprehension questions (English answers accepted today — Chinese re-statement invited from fluent speakers):

1. 汉语教室在几层？在哪个教室的旁边？(third floor; next to the French classroom)
2. 物理实验室在哪儿？(second floor of Building 1, next door to the music room)
3. 校长办公室在哪儿？(first floor of Building 5, between the teachers' office and the school clinic)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 课文二精听 — We Do (7 min) | 20–27 min

**What:** Focused listening comprehension of Text 2, moving from supported to unsupported listening.

**How:**
- Play CD Track 31 again with the pinyin text visible. Students follow silently.
- Play a third time with characters only (pinyin hidden). Students listen for the four locations.
- Project the three comprehension questions from I Do again (no answers shown). Turn-and-talk (2 min): partners compare answers.
- Cold call three students, one question each, to answer in a full Chinese sentence: "汉语教室在三层，在法语教室的旁边。"

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** Turn-and-talk requires every student to state at least one answer aloud to their partner.

- **Scaffold:** Keep the dialogue text (characters only) visible during turn-and-talk for students who need to re-read.
- **Extension:** Students must answer all three questions using the full 在……的旁边/隔壁/中间 phrasing exactly as in the text, without looking.

---

#### Activity 2: 听写楼层图 Listen-and-Label — You Do, Writing (9 min) | 27–36 min

**What:** Teacher-led dictation. Students label a projected floor-plan diagram with room names and floor numbers as the teacher reads original sentences aloud (not from the CD).

**How:**
- Project a simple 3-floor building outline (Floor 1 / Floor 2 / Floor 3, each with 2 empty boxes) in students' exercise books (they copy the outline quickly, 1 min) or draw directly if books are already ruled.
- Teacher reads each sentence once, at natural pace, no repeats until the full set is done:
  1. 音乐室在二层。
  2. 校医室在一层。
  3. 汉语教室在三层。
  4. 物理实验室在二层。
  5. 校长办公室在一层。
  6. 法语教室在三层。
- Students write the correct room name in the correct floor box as they listen.
- After all 6, teacher re-reads the full set once more at normal pace for students to check and fill any gaps.
- Reveal the correct floor-plan on the projector; students self-mark.

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** All students write independently during the dictation.

- **Scaffold:** Before the dictation begins, project the 6 room names as a word bank so students only need to catch the floor number, not spell the room name from memory.
- **Extension:** Students must also add one spatial detail after listening — e.g., predicting/inferring "音乐室和物理实验室都在二层，可能在隔壁" — and share their reasoning with a partner.

---

#### Game: 快问快答楼层 Floor Number Flash (7 min) | 36–43 min

**How to play:**

1. Divide class into two teams (left half vs right half).
2. Teacher calls out a room from today's vocabulary and states its floor ONLY ONCE in a full sentence (drawing from the dictation set or Text 2): e.g. "物理实验室在二层。"
3. Immediately after, teacher asks a rapid follow-up: "物理实验室在几层？" Both teams race to answer chorally as fast as possible — the first team to call out the correct floor number in Chinese wins the point.
4. Play 8–10 rounds, mixing in both Text 2 and dictation-set rooms.

**What language it practises:** Fast listening recall of floor levels; reinforces 在……层 pattern under time pressure.

**Setup needed:** Projector only, for the running scoreboard (or whiteboard corner).

- **Scaffold:** Teacher may repeat the room name (but not the floor number) once if a team asks for clarification.
- **Extension:** After winning a round, one team member must restate the full sentence with the location phrase (旁边/隔壁/中间) if applicable, for a bonus point.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the first; student volunteers read the second and third. "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还不行 — for each criterion. Note any students showing 1 finger on criterion 3 (listening comprehension) for extra support.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Students write ONE sentence answering: 你们班的教室在几层？(Which floor is our classroom on?) — using the real floor of their actual classroom.

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要专门练习怎么说位置——旁边、隔壁、中间、左边、右边——用完整的句子介绍教室在哪儿。"
