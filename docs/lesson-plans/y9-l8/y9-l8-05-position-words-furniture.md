# Year 9 — 学校设施 (Seq. 5 of 8): Extended Position Words + Furniture

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 5 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to describe furniture positions and building layout using an extended set of position words: 上面、下面、前面、后面、对面、楼上、楼下.

## Success Criteria

- I can name all seven new position words and use them correctly (能正确使用七个新的位置词)
- I can describe where an object or room is using X 在 Y 的 + position word (能用"X在Y的……"描述物品或房间的位置)
- I can describe a building floor plan using both old and new position words (能用新旧位置词描述教学楼的布局)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** The five location words and the X 在 Y 的 + location word structure from Lesson 4.

**How:**

*Part A — Quick fire (4 min):* Project 5 location words (旁边, 隔壁, 中间, 左边, 右边) one at a time. Class chorally gives the English meaning.

*Part B — Error correction (4 min):* Project two word-order-error sentences from the L4 style; cold call two students to correct them chorally-echoed by the class:
1. 图书馆旁边在礼堂。→ 图书馆在礼堂的旁边。
2. 操场左边在体育馆。→ 操场在体育馆的左边。

**Connection:** "上节课我们学了五个位置词。今天我们再学七个新的——用来说东西在桌子'上面'、'下面'，或者教室在楼'上面'还是'下面'。语序规则还是一样：X在Y的……"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson.

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Display each row on the projector: 汉字 → pinyin → English. Teacher says the word twice; class repeats twice.

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 上面 | shàngmian | above; on top of |
| 下面 | xiàmian | below; underneath |
| 前面 | qiánmian | in front |
| 后面 | hòumian | behind |
| 对面 | duìmiàn | opposite |
| 楼上 | lóushàng | upstairs |
| 楼下 | lóuxià | downstairs |

*Variant note:* "课本里 Act. 7 也用了'左面'和'右面'——意思跟上节课学的'左边'、'右边'完全一样，只是换了一个字。" Project side by side: 左边 = 左面; 右边 = 右面.

#### Key Sentence Pattern (Recycled + Extended)

**Structure:** X 在 Y 的 + [position word]。 (same rule as Lesson 4 — reinforce, do not re-teach from scratch)

**Modelled examples (furniture scene, project a simple classroom picture with labelled objects — desk, chair, bookshelf, blackboard):**

1. 书在桌子上面。
2. 椅子在桌子下面。
3. 黑板在老师的前面。
4. (Silly example — wait for reaction:) 老师的椅子在黑板的上面！

**Building-layout examples:**

5. 图书馆在体育馆的对面。
6. 教室在楼上，餐厅在楼下。

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Gesture)** Project 书**在**桌子上面 and 书**上面在**桌子. "哪个对？" Every student points — reinforces the word-order rule still applies.
- **(Cold call)** "Sam, 'upstairs' 用中文怎么说？" → "Priya, 'opposite' 呢？"
- **(Choral)** Teacher points to the projected classroom picture: "黑板在哪儿？" → class calls out "黑板在老师的前面！"

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 教室里的东西 — We Do (7 min) | 20–27 min

**(Based on Act. 7, p. 76)**

**What:** Guided description of furniture positions using the new vocabulary, moving from teacher-modelled to student-produced.

**How:**
- Project a labelled classroom scene (desk, chair, bookshelf, blackboard, window, door, pencil case, bag).
- Teacher models 3 sentences describing the scene, pointing to each item: "铅笔盒在桌子上面。书包在椅子下面。窗户在黑板的对面。"
- Turn-and-talk (2 min): Partner A describes 2 items using position words, Partner B describes 2 different items; swap and check for repeats.
- Cold call 3 students to share one sentence each with the class.

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** Turn-and-talk requires every student to produce at least two sentences aloud.

- **Scaffold:** Keep all seven position words plus the word bank of classroom objects (桌子, 椅子, 黑板, 书包, 铅笔盒, 窗户, 门) projected throughout.
- **Extension:** Students must describe at least 4 items without repeating a position word, and add one sentence about the classroom's location relative to another room: "我们的教室在图书馆的楼上。"

---

#### Activity 2: 教学楼平面图 — You Do, Writing (9 min) | 27–36 min

**(Based on Act. 9, p. 77)**

**What:** Students write a short paragraph describing a school building's floor plan using both the Lesson 4 and today's position words.

**How:**
- Project a simple 2-storey floor plan: upstairs has 教室 and 音乐室; downstairs has 办公室 and 图书馆; the 图书馆 is opposite the 餐厅ext (an adjoining external building shown on the map).
- Teacher models one sentence live, thinking aloud about which position word fits: "教室在楼上，办公室在楼下——用'楼上'和'楼下'。图书馆在餐厅的对面——用'对面'。"
- Individual writing (5 min): students write a paragraph (5–6 sentences) describing the floor plan, using at least four different position words (mixing Lesson 4 and today's set).
- Peer-check (2 min): students swap books; the neighbour checks that at least four different position words were used and circles any word-order errors.

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** All students write independently; peer-check requires every student to read and mark a partner's paragraph.

- **Scaffold:** Provide a partially-filled sentence frame: 教室在______。办公室在______。图书馆在餐厅的______。
- **Extension:** Students must describe the floor plan without a sentence frame, using at least six of the twelve total position words taught across Lessons 4–5, and add a comparison sentence: "我觉得图书馆的位置比餐厅方便，因为……"

---

#### Game: 位置词拼句 Sentence Jumble (7 min) | 36–43 min

**How to play:**

1. Project a scrambled sentence (words/phrases out of order), one at a time, e.g.: 上面 ｜ 书 ｜ 桌子 ｜ 在 → 书在桌子上面。
2. Students work in pairs to reorder the phrase in their exercise books as fast as possible.
3. First pair to hold up the correct sentence wins a point for that round; teacher reveals the correct order to confirm.
4. Play 5–6 rounds, using both furniture-scene and floor-plan sentences.

**What language it practises:** Internalising the fixed X 在 Y 的 + position word order by physically reconstructing it.

**Setup needed:** Projector only — prepare 5–6 scrambled-sentence slides in advance.

- **Scaffold:** For the first 1–2 rounds, keep the correct position word underlined in the scramble as a hint of where it must land relative to 的.
- **Extension:** After winning a round, the pair must generate one additional original sentence using the same position word before the next round starts.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the first; student volunteers read the second and third. "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还不行 — for each criterion. Note any students showing 1 finger on criterion 1 (new vocabulary) for extra support next lesson.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Students write ONE sentence describing where something in the real classroom is right now, using one of today's new position words: 我的______在______的______。

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要做两个听力练习，还要复习量词，玩一个记汉字的游戏。"
