# Year 9 — 学校设施 (Seq. 7 of 8): Radical Competition + City Map Design

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 7 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to recall radicals under competition pressure and use position words to describe and redesign a city map.

## Success Criteria

- I can write at least six radicals correctly from memory (能默写至少六个部首)
- I can describe a city map using position words in complete sentences (能用位置词描述城市地图)
- I can recognise the eight stationery preview words and know that 游泳馆 is a test-only variant of 游泳池 (能认读八个文具预习词汇，知道"游泳馆"是"游泳池"的测验变体)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- Whiteboard corner for team scoreboard
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Rapid recall of all position words and measure words from Lessons 1–6, as energetic warm-up for today's competition format.

**How:**

*Part A — Buzzer round (5 min):* Project 6 prompts one at a time (mix of position words and measure words: 旁边, 楼上, 一__教学楼, 对面, 一__铅笔, 中间). Students raise hands to answer; first correct hand called wins a point for a quick informal tally. Keep pace fast — this sets the competitive tone for the lesson.

*Part B — Sentence flash (3 min):* Project one facility sentence with a blank measure word or position word; cold call one student, class echoes.

**Connection:** "今天整节课都是比赛！我们先比部首，再比设计城市地图。准备好了吗？"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson.

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 部首大挑战 Radical Writing Competition — We Do/Game (9 min) | 20–29 min

**(Based on Act. 11, p. 78)**

**What:** Team radical-writing competition — write as many correct radicals as possible from memory within the time limit.

**How:**
- Divide the class into 3–4 teams. Each team designates a "scribe" who will write on a shared page (or each member writes in their own exercise book and the team's best set counts).
- Project the reference grid from I Do for 1 minute of final study.
- Hide the grid. Teams have 3 minutes to write as many radicals as they can remember, each with one example word, in their exercise books.
- Time's up: teams read their list aloud one radical at a time; teacher awards 1 point per correct radical + 1 bonus point if the example word is also correct. Track on the whiteboard corner.

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** Every team member must contribute at least one radical to their team's list — teacher circulates during the 3-minute window to confirm.

- **Scaffold:** Teams may keep the meaning column (English) visible as a memory hook during the writing phase, with only the 汉字/example columns hidden.
- **Extension:** Teams earn a bonus point for any radical beyond the twelve shown in the reference grid (e.g. 讠, 刂, 辶) if correctly identified with an example.

---

#### Activity 2: 文具 + 游泳池小测验 — You Do (5 min) | 29–34 min

**What:** Quick independent check on the stationery preview words and the 游泳池/游泳馆 test variant.

**How:**
- Project 4 stationery words (no English); students write the English meaning in their exercise books.
- Add one true/false item: "游泳馆和游泳池意思一样。" (True.)
- Reveal answers; students self-mark.

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** All students complete the quick check independently.

- **Scaffold:** Keep pinyin visible next to each stationery word during the quiz.
- **Extension:** Students write one additional sentence using 游泳池 in context: 我们学校有一个游泳池。

---

#### Game: 城市设计大挑战 City Map Design Challenge (9 min) | 34–43 min

**(Based on Act. 12, p. 79)**

**How to play:**

1. **Describe (3 min):** Project a simple city-centre map (library, school, hospital, park, shops, train station). In the same teams from Activity 1, students describe the map together using position words, preparing 3–4 sentences: "图书馆在学校的对面。公园在医院的旁边。"
2. **Redesign (4 min):** Each team redesigns the city on a blank grid drawn in their exercise books — they may move buildings anywhere — and writes 3–4 sentences describing their new layout using position words.
3. **Present (2 min):** One team member from 2–3 teams presents their redesigned city to the class in Chinese; class listens and can ask one question each.

**What language it practises:** Free application of all position words (Lessons 4–5) and facility/room vocabulary (Lessons 1–3) in an open-ended, creative task.

**Setup needed:** Projector for the original city map; exercise books for the redesign grid.

- **Scaffold:** Provide a sentence frame: ______在______的______。 and a word bank of the six city locations.
- **Extension:** Teams must use at least five different position words across their description and redesign, and justify one design choice: "我们把公园放在学校旁边，因为……"

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the first; student volunteers read the second and third. "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还不行 — for each criterion. Note any students showing 1 finger on criterion 1 (radicals) for the next lesson's review.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Students write ONE radical (their choice) with an example word, and ONE sentence describing their redesigned city using a position word.

- **Preview:** "下一课是这个单元的总复习课，我们会准备好考3.2测验——设施、位置词、量词，全部复习一遍！"
