# Year 9 — 学校设施 (Seq. 2 of 8): Word-Building + School Layout

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 2 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to build compound facility/room words from base characters and describe our school's layout from a map.

## Success Criteria

- I can combine base characters with 馆/场/池/楼/室 to build correct compound words (能用"馆、场、池、楼、室"组成正确的合成词)
- I can describe a school map using 我们学校有…… with at least six facilities (能用"我们学校有……"描述学校地图)
- I can identify measure word errors (个/幢) in a sentence and correct them (能找出并改正量词"个"和"幢"的错误)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 1 facility vocabulary (11 words) and the 个/幢 measure word rule.

**How:**

*Part A — Vocabulary flash (4 min):* Project each of the 11 Lesson 1 facility words one at a time (汉字 only). Class chorally names each in Chinese, then a cold-call student gives the English.

*Part B — Correct the Teacher (4 min):* Project three sentences with deliberate measure word errors, one at a time:
1. 我们学校有五**个**教学楼。→ class calls out "**幢**！"
2. 学校有一**幢**图书馆。→ class calls out "**个**！"
3. 我们学校有一**个**篮球场和一**场**足球场。→ class calls out "第二个'一场'是错的——'场'已经在词里了，不用量词，一**个**足球场！"

**Connection:** "上节课我们学了'图书馆'、'体育馆'这些词。你们注意到了吗？这些词都是两个部分组成的——'图书'+'馆'，'体育'+'馆'。今天我们要练习自己组新词，还要看地图介绍学校的布局。"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson.

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Word-Building Concept

*This is a consolidation and pattern-recognition activity, not new vocabulary — the goal is to show students how facility words are built from smaller parts.*

Display the five "building block" characters on the projector, each with its meaning:

| 部件 | Meaning | Example already known |
|---|---|---|
| 馆 (guǎn) | building; hall | 图书**馆**, 体育**馆** |
| 场 (chǎng) | open field/court | 篮球**场**, 足球**场**, 停车**场** |
| 池 (chí) | pool | 游泳**池** |
| 楼 (lóu) | multi-storey building | 教学**楼** |
| 室 (shì) | room | *(new pattern today)* |

Teacher models the word-building process aloud: "如果我知道'办公'是'work' 的意思，'室'是'room'，那么'办公'+'室' = '办公室' — office. 我们可以用这个方法猜新词的意思。" Model 2 more: 音乐 + 室 = 音乐室 (music room); 实验 + 室 = 实验室 (laboratory — flag 实验 as a new component meaning "experiment", to be taught formally next lesson).

#### Key Sentence Pattern (Recycled — Lesson 1)

Redisplay: 我来介绍一下我们学校。我们学校有…… Students will use this today to describe a map instead of a free list.

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Choral)** "如果'教学'是'teaching' 的意思，'楼'是'building'，那么'教学楼'是什么意思？" → class calls out "teaching building!"
- **(Cold call)** "Sam, 如果我们把'校医'（school doctor）加上'室'，会组成什么词？意思是什么？" → 校医室 — school clinic (flag as a preview, formally taught next lesson).
- **(Gesture)** Project 一**场**足球场 vs 一**个**足球场. "哪个对？" Every student points.

---

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 猜猜看组词 — We Do (7 min) | 20–27 min

**What:** Guided word-building practice. Teacher displays a base character; students guess which "building block" (馆/场/池/楼/室) completes it, drawing on the concept from I Do.

**How:**
- Project one base word at a time: 图书__, 体育__, 篮球__, 足球__, 停车__, 游泳__, 教学__. Class chorally calls out the missing character.
- After each correct answer, reveal the full compound word and its meaning.
- Final round: teacher gives the meaning in English only ("music room") and class must produce the full Chinese compound from scratch (音乐室).

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** Every base word is answered chorally by the whole class before any individual is cold-called to confirm.

- **Scaffold:** Keep the "building block" table from I Do visible throughout as a reference.
- **Extension:** After each correct compound, fluent speakers must use it in a full sentence: "我们学校有一个游泳池。"

---

#### Activity 2: 看地图介绍学校 — You Do, Writing (8 min) | 27–35 min

**(Based on Act. 3, p. 72)**

**What:** Students describe a school layout map using 我们学校有…… with at least six facilities, then write a short paragraph.

**How:**
- Project a simple school map showing: 游泳池, 礼堂, 操场, 足球场, 图书馆, 篮球场, 餐厅, 体育馆, 停车场, 教学楼 as labelled icons/boxes.
- Teacher models describing 3 items from the map aloud, pointing to each: "我们学校有一个图书馆，还有一个礼堂。"
- Turn-and-talk (2 min): Partner A describes 3 facilities from the map, Partner B describes 3 different ones; then swap and check they haven't repeated each other.
- Individual writing (3 min): students write a short paragraph (4–5 sentences) describing the map in their exercise books, using correct measure words throughout.
- Cold call 2 students to read their paragraph aloud.

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** Turn-and-talk requires every student to speak; all students write independently.

- **Scaffold:** Keep the sentence frame projected: 我们学校有__幢教学楼、__个__、__个__和__个__。我们学校还有一个__。
- **Extension:** Students must describe all ten facilities on the map without repeating a sentence structure twice, varying between 我们学校有…… and 我们学校还有……

---

#### Game: 组词接力赛 Word-Building Relay (8 min) | 35–43 min

**(Based on Act. 2, p. 72)**

**How to play:**

1. **Setup:** Divide the class into 3–4 teams. Project a list of base words one at a time (a representative set drawn from Act. 2's larger 21-item bank): 图书, 体育, 篮球, 足球, 停车, 游泳, 教学, 办公, 实验, 音乐, 校医, 校长.
2. For each base word, the first student in each team's line runs (mentally/verbally — no physical movement needed if space is tight) to be first to call out the completed compound word with its correct "building block" character.
3. First correct answer for that round scores a point for their team; teacher reveals the correct compound to confirm.
4. Rotate through all 12 base words, keeping score on the whiteboard corner or projector.

**What language it practises:** Recognition of compound-word structure; rapid retrieval of facility/room vocabulary (including previewed Lesson 3 items: 办公室, 实验室, 音乐室, 校医室, 校长室).

**Setup needed:** Projector only — one slide listing the 12 base words in order; whiteboard corner or projected scoreboard for tallying points.

- **Scaffold:** If a team is stuck, teacher may reveal the first character of the "building block" as a hint.
- **Extension:** Once a team wins a round, one member of that team must use the new compound word in a full sentence before the point is confirmed.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the first; student volunteers read the second and third. "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还不行 — for each criterion. Note any students showing 1 finger on word-building (criterion 1) for extra support next lesson.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Students write ONE compound word from today's game (their choice) plus its English meaning, and ONE sentence describing the school map using 我们学校有……

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要正式学习教室、实验室这些房间的词汇，还要学怎么问'……在哪儿？'"
