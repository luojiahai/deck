# Year 9 — 学校设施 (Seq. 6 of 8): Listening Practice + Character Memorisation

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — School Facilities 学校设施 (Sequence 6 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to understand spoken descriptions of school layout, revise measure words, and memorise twelve component characters.

## Success Criteria

- I can listen to a description of school locations and tick the correct answer (能听懂关于学校位置的描述并选出正确答案)
- I can match at least six nouns to their correct measure word (能给至少六个名词配对正确的量词)
- I can recognise and write all twelve of today's characters from memory (能默写今天学的十二个汉字)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- CD player — Track 30 (Act. 4, p. 73) and Track 32 (Act. 10, p. 78)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** All twelve position words from Lessons 4–5, plus the 个/幢/层/楼 measure words from Lessons 1–3.

**How:**

*Part A — Position word flash (4 min):* Project all twelve position words (旁边, 隔壁, 中间, 左边, 右边, 上面, 下面, 前面, 后面, 对面, 楼上, 楼下) as a grid. Cold call students at random to point and give the English meaning.

*Part B — Measure word quick fire (4 min):* Project three nouns (教学楼, 图书馆, 三______教室 — wait, blank for 层). Class chorally completes each with the correct measure word.

**Connection:** "今天我们要做两个听力练习，还要复习更多量词——不只是学校的，还有其他你们学过的词。最后我们要玩一个记汉字的游戏。"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson.

#### Measure Word Refresher

*This is a review of general measure words from across the year, not new teaching.* Display a quick reference table of measure words used so far: 个 (default), 幢 (buildings), 层/楼 (floors), 本 (books), 支 (pens), 张 (flat objects), 双 (pairs), 件 (clothing), 位 (people, polite), 只 (animals). "今天的游戏会用到这些量词，还有一些新的名词。"

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Choral — character recall)** Teacher points to 米, 田, 鸟, and 天 on the projector (no pinyin) one at a time. Class calls out the pinyin together.
- **(Cold call)** "Sam, 一______书？" → "本！" → "Priya, 一______铅笔？" → "支！"
- **(Gesture)** Project 一**个**猫 vs 一**只**猫. "哪个对？" Every student points.

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 听力理解一 — We Do (8 min) | 20–28 min

**(Act. 4, p. 73, CD Track 30)**

**What:** Guided listening comprehension — tick the correct answer for 6 items about school layout.

**How:**
- Students draw a 6-row answer grid in their exercise books (label rows 1–6, with the answer options as given in the textbook).
- Teacher plays the first item, pauses, and models the process aloud: "我听到了……所以我选……" ticking the answer on the projected grid.
- Play the remaining 5 items one at a time, pausing briefly after each for students to tick their own grid.
- Play the full track once more without pausing for students to check their answers.
- Reveal answers on the projector; students self-mark.

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** Every student ticks all 6 items independently before answers are revealed.

- **Scaffold:** Pause the audio after every item (not just the first) for students who need extra processing time.
- **Extension:** After self-marking, fluent speakers write one full sentence summarising one of the 6 items in their own words.

---

#### Activity 2: 听力理解二 + 量词小测 — You Do (7 min) | 28–35 min

**(Act. 10, p. 78, CD Track 32 + brief measure word quiz)**

**What:** Independent listening comprehension (6 items about building locations), followed by a brief measure-word matching quiz.

**How:**
- Students draw a fresh 6-row answer grid. Play CD Track 32 straight through once with no pausing (more independent than Activity 1).
- Play once more for checking. Reveal answers; students self-mark.
- **Brief measure-word quiz (2 min):** Project 6 nouns from the Act. 5 bank (帽子, 钢琴, 桌子, 汽车, 老师, 项链). Students write the correct measure word for each in their exercise books. Reveal answers (顶, 架, 张, 辆, 位, 条) for self-marking.

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** All students complete both the listening grid and the measure-word quiz independently.

- **Scaffold:** For the measure-word quiz, project a word bank of the 6 correct measure words (in scrambled order) alongside the nouns, so students match rather than recall from scratch.
- **Extension:** Students complete the quiz without the word bank and add one more noun + measure word pair of their own choosing.

---

#### Game: 十二字记忆挑战 Character Memory Challenge (8 min) | 35–43 min

**(p. 77, Act. 8 — projector display/hide; team competition)**

**How to play:**

1. **Phase 1 — Group study (2 min):** Display all 12 characters on the projector (same table from I Do). Students study silently, re-tracing in exercise books if helpful.
2. **Phase 2 — Hide and individual recall (1 min):** Remove all characters (blank slide). Students write as many of the 12 as they can remember, characters only, no talking.
3. **Phase 3 — Team challenge (5 min):** Divide the class into Team Left and Team Right. Project one pinyin at a time with a 5-second countdown; all students write the character. Hold up books — teacher scans and awards 1 point per team for each correct character shown, then reveals the 汉字 to confirm. Play through all 12.

**Setup needed:** Three slides prepared in advance — (1) the full 12-character table, (2) a blank slide, (3) twelve individual pinyin-only slides with a countdown.

- **Scaffold:** In Phase 3, students may refer to their Phase 1 tracing notes in their exercise books.
- **Extension:** After writing the correct character, fluent students must also whisper the English gloss to a partner before the reveal for a bonus point.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria. Teacher reads the first; student volunteers read the second and third. "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还不行 — for each criterion. Note any students showing 1 finger on criterion 3 (characters) for extra practice next lesson.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Teacher calls out two pinyin from today's character set ("guā", "niǎo"); students write the correct characters from memory.

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要玩一个部首比赛，还要看城市地图，学怎么重新设计自己的城市！"
