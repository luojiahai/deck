# Lesson 7.7: 都 (Both/All)

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 7 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to express "both" and "all" preferences using ……，都……

## Success Criteria
- I can place 都 correctly in a sentence — after the subject, before the verb (油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢)
- I can list two or more items and use 都 to say I like/do them all
- I can combine 都 and 正在 in a partner dialogue

## Materials Needed
- Projector (Act. 12 match display, Act. 11 dialogue prompts, Act. 13 sentence completion)
- CD / audio file: Track 35 (Act. 12 — listen & match)
- Mini whiteboards + markers

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** 正在 structure from Lesson 7.6; Text 2 vocabulary
**How:** Quick mime warm-up — teacher mimes 3 actions. Students write the 正在 sentence for each on whiteboards. Then: "You saw 都 in Text 2 — 油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画. Today we learn the rule so you can use it yourselves."
**Connection:** "正在 tells us what's happening right now. 都 tells us about everything in a list. By the end of today you'll combine both in the same dialogue."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read together. Keep visible throughout.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Key Sentence Pattern
**……，都……** = both/all (subject + 都 + verb)

**Rule:** List the items FIRST, then state the subject, then 都, then the verb.

> 油画儿、水彩画儿，我**都**喜欢画。 (Oil paintings and watercolours — I like painting both.)
> 小说、杂志，他**都**喜欢看。 (Novels and magazines — he likes reading both.)
> 弹钢琴、唱歌、听音乐，她**都**喜欢。 (Piano, singing, listening to music — she likes them all.)

Critical word order: **都 goes BEFORE the verb, NEVER at the end.**

Common error — demonstrate the wrong version and correct it:
❌ 小说、杂志，我喜欢都。(WRONG — 都 after verb)
✓ 小说、杂志，我都喜欢。(CORRECT — 都 before verb)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Where does 都 go — before or after the verb?" (before)
- "Write: 'Guitar and piano — I like playing both.'" (吉他、钢琴，我都喜欢弹。)
- "What's wrong? 弹钢琴、唱歌，我喜欢都。" (都 is in the wrong place — should be 我都喜欢)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Correct the Teacher — We Do (5 min)
Teacher says/writes 4 sentences on the board with deliberate 都 placement errors. Students spot the mistake and write the correct version on mini whiteboards.

Sentences (all wrong):
1. 小说、杂志，我喜欢都看。
2. 油画儿、水彩画儿，她画都喜欢。
3. 都弹钢琴、唱歌，他喜欢。
4. 我喜欢都国画儿和油画儿。

- **Scaffold:** Word order reminder on board: items → subject + 都 + verb.
- **Extension:** Students write one deliberate error of their own, pass to a partner to fix.
- **OTR:** Students hold up corrected sentences — teacher scans all boards simultaneously.

#### Activity 2: CD Track 35 — Act. 12, Listen & Match (p. 66) (7 min)
Display the Act. 12 exercise. Students hear descriptions of people's hobbies and match each person to their correct activity. Play CD T35 once — listen only. Play again — students write their answers. Play a third time — check. Go through answers together (students call out each answer).
- **Scaffold:** Preview the name list and activity list before playing.
- **Extension:** Students write a 都 sentence summarising two hobbies they heard for one of the people.
- **OTR:** Students call out each answer as teacher reviews — raise hand if you disagree.

#### Activity 3: Act. 11 — Partner Dialogue (正在 + 都) (8 min)
Pairs use Act. 11 dialogue prompts (p. 66) to create and practise a short conversation that uses BOTH 正在 and 都. Sentence frames on the board:

A: 你正在做什么？
B: 我正在______。
A: 你喜欢______吗？
B: ______、______，我都喜欢。你呢？
A: 我也都喜欢！/ 我只喜欢______。

Each pair practises twice (swap A/B roles). Teacher listens in and takes notes for feedback. 2 pairs perform for the class.
- **Scaffold:** Full sentence frames on board. Students may use the textbook vocabulary list.
- **Extension:** Students ditch the frames and improvise a 6-turn dialogue using at least one 正在 and one 都 sentence; they must also include a 一边……一边…… sentence.
- **OTR:** Both students speak during pair work; whole class listens to 2 performing pairs.

#### Activity 4: Act. 13 — 都 Sentence Completion — You Do (3 min)
Students complete 都 sentence starters in their books (from p. 66):
- 弹钢琴、唱歌，______都喜欢。
- 小说、杂志，他______喜欢看。
- 油画儿、水彩画儿、国画儿，______都喜欢画。

Quick peer-check: swap books with the person next to you.
- **Scaffold:** Keep 都 placement rule on the board.
- **Extension:** Students write 2 additional 都 sentences using their own hobbies.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read each criterion. For criterion 3, teacher asks 2 students to demonstrate a live 2-turn exchange using 都 and 正在.
- **Self-assessment:** Thumbs up / sideways / down for each criterion. Teacher notes which criterion has the most thumbs down — address in Lesson 7.8 review.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one 都 sentence about 2 things you like/do." Collected on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson is our final Lesson 7 class — we'll learn the 4 simple characters 包, 止, 士, 豆, and you'll write a paragraph bringing together everything we've done over all 7 lessons."
