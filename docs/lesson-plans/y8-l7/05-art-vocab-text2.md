# Lesson 7.5: Art Vocabulary + Text 2

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 5 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to talk about reading and art hobbies in Chinese.

## Success Criteria
- I can name 3 types of paintings in Chinese (国画儿, 油画儿, 水彩画儿)
- I can say what I like to read (小说, 杂志)
- I can read, annotate, and answer questions about Text 2 in writing

## Materials Needed
- Projector (vocabulary slides: 汉字 + pinyin + English; Text 2)
- CD / audio file: Track 34 (Text 2)
- Students' exercise books or printed Text 2 worksheet
- Coloured pens for annotation (optional — highlight new words in the text)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** ao/ou pinyin distinction from Lesson 7.4; Text 1 vocabulary and 一边……一边…… structure
**How:** Quick quiz on mini whiteboards — 4 questions:
1. "Write: I like playing piano while singing." (我喜欢一边弹钢琴，一边唱歌。)
2. "She reads for 1 hour every day. Write in Chinese." (她每天读一个小时的书。)
3. "Write a word with ao and a word with ou." (accept any correct answers)
4. "What does 爱好 mean?" (hobby)
**Connection:** "We've been talking about music hobbies — 弹钢琴, 唱歌, 听音乐. Today we add reading and art hobbies to your repertoire."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read together. Keep visible throughout the lesson.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary — Text 2 (p. 65)
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 小说 | xiǎoshuō | novel |
| 杂志 | zázhì | magazine |
| 画儿 | huàr | drawing or painting |
| 国画儿 | guóhuàr | Chinese painting |
| 油画儿 | yóuhuàr | oil painting |
| 水彩画儿 | shuǐcǎihuàr | watercolour (painting) |
| 都 | dōu | all; both |
| 正在 | zhèngzài | in the process of (right now) |

Introduce each word: 汉字 → pinyin → English. Class repeats twice.

Point out the 画儿 pattern: 国画儿 (Chinese painting) / 油画儿 (oil painting) / 水彩画儿 (watercolour). All use 画儿 — students only need to learn the type prefix.

Point out 正在 briefly: "We'll go deep on this in Lesson 7.6 — today just notice it in the text."

#### Text / Dialogue — Text 2 (p. 64, CD Track 34)
> A: 你有什么爱好？
> B: 我喜欢看小说和杂志。
> A: 你喜欢画画儿吗？
> B: 喜欢。油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画。我正在学画国画儿。
> A: 我也在学画国画儿。

1. Teacher plays CD T34 — students listen, books closed.
2. Play again — students follow in the text.
3. Choral read together (twice).

Comprehension questions (answered in writing today):
1. B喜欢看什么？(小说和杂志)
2. B喜欢画什么？(油画儿和水彩画儿)
3. B正在学什么？(学画国画儿)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "What's the difference between 小说 and 杂志?" (novel vs magazine)
- "Can you spot 都 in Text 2? What does that sentence mean?" (油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画 — I like painting both oil paintings and watercolours)
- "What does 正在 tell us?" (action in progress right now)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Annotate Text 2 — We Do (8 min)
Students copy Text 2 into their exercise books (or use the printed worksheet). They:
1. Underline every new word from today's vocabulary list
2. Write the English meaning above each underlined word in pencil
3. Circle 都 and 正在 (preview for Lessons 7.6 and 7.7)

Teacher circulates and checks annotations — address the 1–2 most common errors with the class.
- **Scaffold:** Vocabulary list stays on projector; students can refer to it while annotating.
- **Extension:** Students also annotate any 一边……一边…… or duration structures they notice (carry-forward from earlier lessons).

#### Activity 2: Comprehension Questions — You Do (8 min)
Students answer the 3 comprehension questions from the I Do phase in full Chinese sentences in their books:
1. B喜欢看什么？
2. B喜欢画什么画儿？
3. B正在学什么？

Then 2 additional questions:
4. A也在学什么？(学画国画儿)
5. 你喜欢看小说吗？用中文回答。(open answer)

Teacher circulates — check question 5 for sentence structure.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame for question 5: 我(不)喜欢看______。
- **Extension:** Students write a 3-sentence summary of Text 2 in their own words (not copying), then add one sentence about their own art or reading hobbies.
- **OTR:** Cold call 2 students to share their answer to question 5 with the class.

#### Activity 3: Write About Myself — You Do (7 min)
Students write a short paragraph (4–5 sentences) about their own reading and art hobbies, modelled on Text 2's style. Sentence starters on the board:
- 我喜欢……
- 我不太喜欢……（but you like something else instead）
- ……，我都喜欢。（use 都 — they've seen it in the text even before the grammar lesson）
- 我正在学……

- **Scaffold:** Students may use English for one sentence they can't express yet, with a note to return to it.
- **Extension:** Students remove the sentence starters and write freely; push for 6–7 sentences.
- **OTR:** 2–3 volunteers read their paragraph aloud at the end.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read each aloud together. Thumbs up / sideways / down.
- **Self-assessment:** "Write one word you're confident about and one word you want to practise more."
- **Exit ticket:** "Translate: 'I like painting. I like both Chinese painting and watercolour.'" (我喜欢画画儿。国画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画。) Collected on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we focus on 正在 — how to say something is happening RIGHT NOW. You already saw it in Text 2 today."
