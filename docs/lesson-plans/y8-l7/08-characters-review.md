# Lesson 7.8: Simple Characters + Consolidation

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 8 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to write simple Chinese characters and consolidate all Lesson 7 language in extended writing.

## Success Criteria
- I can write and recognise the 4 simple characters: 包 (bāo), 止 (zhǐ), 士 (shì), 豆 (dòu)
- I can write picture captions using Lesson 7 vocabulary and structures
- I can write a short paragraph about my own hobbies using at least two of the four L7 grammar structures (一边……一边……, duration, 正在, 都)

## Materials Needed
- Projector (character stroke order for 包, 止, 士, 豆; Act. 14 pictures)
- Students' exercise books
- Coloured pens (optional — for annotating their final paragraph)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** All four Lesson 7 grammar structures — 一边……一边……, duration, 正在, 都
**How:** 4 mini whiteboard rounds, one per grammar point:
1. "Write a sentence using 一边……一边……" (any correct sentence)
2. "Write: 'He plays guitar for 45 minutes every day.'" (他每天弹三刻钟的吉他。)
3. "Write a 正在 sentence about someone in this room." (open answer)
4. "Write a 都 sentence about 2 hobbies." (e.g. 弹钢琴、唱歌，我都喜欢。)
Students hold up after each. Teacher addresses the most common error per round.
**Connection:** "You've learned a lot this unit. Today you put it all together in writing — and you'll also add 4 new characters to your toolkit."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read together. Point to the third criterion: "By the end of today, you'll have a complete paragraph you can be proud of."

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Simple Characters — Act. 8 (p. 65)
Teach each character with stroke order on the projector. Students copy each character 3 times in their books as teacher demonstrates.

| 字 | 拼音 | Meaning | Strokes | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 包 | bāo | bag / wrap | 5 | Outer wrap radical 勹 + inner 已 |
| 止 | zhǐ | stop | 4 | Resembles a foot mid-step |
| 士 | shì | scholar / gentleman | 3 | Like 土 (earth) but top stroke longer |
| 豆 | dòu | bean | 7 | Top = lid, middle = container, bottom = feet |

After each character: "Can you see this shape in any words you know?" (e.g. 包 appears in 书包 school bag; 止 appears in 停止 stop)

CFU: "Which character has 4 strokes?" (止) "Which looks like a container with a lid?" (豆)

#### Modelling the Paragraph
Teacher writes a model paragraph on the board (NOT a textbook passage — an original):

> 我有很多爱好。我喜欢一边听音乐，一边画画儿。油画儿、国画儿，我都喜欢画。我每天画半个小时的画儿。我现在正在学画国画儿。我也喜欢读书。我每天读一个小时的书。

Point out: 4 grammar structures all used. "Your paragraph doesn't need to be this long — 4–5 sentences is excellent. The goal is to use at least 2 of the 4 structures."

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Circle the 一边……一边…… sentence in the model. How many can you spot?" (one)
- "Underline the duration structure. How many are there?" (two: 半个小时, 一个小时)
- "What does 正在 tell us in the model paragraph?" (I'm currently learning to paint Chinese painting — ongoing action)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Character Writing — Act. 8 (8 min)
Students practise writing all 4 characters in their books: 包, 止, 士, 豆. Write each character 5 times, following stroke order. Then write each character's pinyin and English meaning next to it.

After 6 minutes: mini whiteboard check — teacher says the meaning in English, students write the Chinese character from memory. 4 rounds.
- **Scaffold:** Stroke order guide stays on projector.
- **Extension:** Students write each character in a short phrase or sentence (e.g. 书包, 停止; or make up a sentence with 豆).
- **OTR:** Mini whiteboard hold-up after each of the 4 memory-check rounds.

#### Activity 2: Act. 14 — Picture Caption Writing (p. 67) (7 min)
Display the Act. 14 pictures (people engaged in hobbies). Students write a caption sentence for each picture in their books — must use correct vocabulary and at least one grammar structure across the 4 captions.

Minimum: one 正在 sentence, one 喜欢 sentence.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frames: ______正在______。/ ______每天______的______。
- **Extension:** Students write 2 sentences per picture — one describing what the person is doing now (正在) and one habitual statement (喜欢 or 每天).
- **OTR:** 2–3 students read a caption aloud; class gives thumbs up if grammar is correct.

#### Activity 3: My Hobbies Paragraph — You Do (8 min)
Students write a paragraph of 4–6 sentences about their own hobbies in their exercise books. Requirements:
- At least 2 of the 4 L7 grammar structures: 一边……一边……, duration, 正在, 都
- At least 4 different hobby vocabulary words from Lesson 7
- No pinyin — characters only (pinyin reference list may be consulted)

Students label the grammar structures they've used in the margin: circle 一边……一边…… in blue, duration in red, 正在 in green, 都 in orange.
- **Scaffold:** Model paragraph stays on projector. Students may use it as a structural guide but must change the content to be true of themselves.
- **Extension:** Students aim for 7–8 sentences; must use all four grammar structures; no model paragraph on the projector (ask teacher to hide it).
- **OTR:** 2–3 volunteers read their paragraph aloud at the end.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read each criterion. Students annotate their paragraph: tick in the margin next to each criterion they've met.
- **Self-assessment:** Students write at the bottom of their paragraph: "I used __ out of 4 grammar structures. Next time I want to improve ______."
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one of the 4 simple characters (your choice) — characters only, no looking." Collected with the paragraph on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next unit we move from music to sports — 运动. Get ready for some very different activity verbs."
