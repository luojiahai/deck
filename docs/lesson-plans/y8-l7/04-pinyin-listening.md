# Lesson 7.4: Pinyin (ao/ou) + Listening

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 4 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to distinguish ao and ou sounds in Chinese and follow spoken Chinese to complete listening tasks.

## Success Criteria
- I can hear the difference between ao (like "aow") and ou (like "oh") in spoken Chinese
- I can correctly identify ao/ou when I hear them in new words
- I can follow spoken Chinese to tick correct answers in a listening grid

## Materials Needed
- CD / audio files: Track 32 (Act. 4 — ao/ou), Track 33 (Act. 6 — listen & tick grid)
- Projector (Act. 4 and Act. 6 exercise sheets)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- Printed Act. 4 and Act. 6 worksheets (or students copy into books)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Duration structure and duration time phrases from Lesson 7.3
**How:** Teacher says a hobby + duration in English — students write the full Chinese sentence on mini whiteboards. 4 rounds:
1. "She sings for 45 minutes every day." → 她每天唱三刻钟的歌。
2. "He plays piano for 1.5 hours every day." → 他每天弹一个半小时的钢琴。
3. "I watch TV for 2 hours every day." → 我每天看两个小时的电视。
4. "She reads for half an hour every day." → 她每天读半个小时的书。
Students hold up whiteboards — teacher corrects the most common error.
**Connection:** "Many of today's listening words contain ao or ou sounds — 爱好, 钢琴, 唱歌, 读书. You'll hear these in the CD exercises today. Let's sharpen your ear first."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read together. Keep visible throughout.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Explicit Phonics: ao vs ou
**ao** (like "ow" in "cow" — mouth opens wide, jaw drops): 爱好 (àihào), 钢琴 (gāngqín has no ao but 好 does), 好 (hǎo)
**ou** (like "oh" in "go" — lips round and close): 读书 (dúshū — no ou), 九 (jiǔ), 走 (zǒu)

Write both on the board. Exaggerate the mouth shapes. Students mirror:
- ao: open wide, drop the jaw, finish with a slight "w" shape
- ou: start open, lips come together and round

Drill 6 minimal pairs (teacher says one, students write ao or ou on whiteboard):
1. 好 hǎo (ao) vs 后 hòu (ou)
2. 告 gào (ao) vs 够 gòu (ou)
3. 包 bāo (ao) vs 不 bù — no ou (neither)
4. 高 gāo (ao) vs 走 zǒu (ou)
5. 草 cǎo (ao) vs 口 kǒu (ou)
6. 到 dào (ao) vs 头 tóu (ou)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Does 爱好 contain ao or ou?" (ao — 好 hǎo)
- "Show me ao mouth shape. Now ou." (teacher checks)
- "I'll say three words. Write A if you hear ao, O if you hear ou." (teacher says: 告, 走, 好)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: CD Track 32 — Act. 4, Listen & Tick (p. 62) — Listening (8 min)
Display the Act. 4 worksheet. Explain the format: students will hear pairs of words and must tick whether they hear ao or ou. Play CD T32 once through — students listen only. Play again — students tick. Play a third time — students check. Go through answers together.
- **Scaffold:** ao and ou written clearly on the board throughout; mouth shape reminder visible.
- **Extension:** Students write one word of their own for each sound at the bottom of the sheet.
- **OTR:** Students hold up 1 finger (ao) or 2 fingers (ou) as teacher goes through answers orally.

#### Activity 2: Teacher-Led Minimal Pairs Drill (5 min)
Teacher says 10 words at natural pace — students write ao or ou on whiteboards:
好, 后, 到, 豆, 高, 头, 包, 口, 草, 走

Students hold up after each — teacher scans, addresses errors. Finish with a speed round (no whiteboards — class shouts ao or ou).
- **Scaffold:** Mouth shape diagram stays on projector.
- **Extension:** Students call out a word of their own for a partner to categorise.
- **OTR:** Every student responds to every word — whiteboard or shout response.

#### Activity 3: CD Track 33 — Act. 6, Listen & Tick Grid (p. 63) — Listening (8 min)
Display or distribute the Act. 6 grid (6 boxes, 3 choices each). Students listen to 6 short recordings and tick the correct picture/answer in each box. Play CD T33 once — listen only. Play again — tick. Play third time — check. Review answers together.
- **Scaffold:** Pre-teach any unfamiliar topic words before playing; write them on the board.
- **Extension:** Students write a sentence in Chinese for the answer they chose in 2 of the boxes.
- **OTR:** Students call out their answer for each box as teacher reviews — instant show of hands to flag disagreements.

#### Activity 4: Chain Game — Act. 7 (p. 64) (5 min)
Teacher starts a category (e.g. hobby words). Students add to the same category one by one around the room: 弹钢琴 → 唱歌 → 听音乐 → 读书 → 看电视 → 弹吉他 → …. If a student can't think of a word in 5 seconds, they're out. Restart with a new category (e.g. duration phrases: 五分钟 → 十分钟 → 一刻钟 → …).
- **Scaffold:** Word bank visible on the board.
- **Extension:** Students must say the word AND a full sentence using it before passing.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read each aloud. Students thumbs up / sideways / down.
- **Self-assessment:** "Do ao and ou feel clearer now? What would help you remember the difference?"
- **Exit ticket:** "Write 2 Chinese words with ao and 2 with ou. No repeats from the lesson." (E.g. 好, 高 / 走, 后)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we start the second half of Lesson 7 — new vocabulary about reading and art hobbies, and a new text."
