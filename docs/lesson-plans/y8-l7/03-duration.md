# Lesson 7.3: Duration of Action

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 3 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to say how long we do activities using the duration structure.

## Success Criteria
- I can say time duration phrases in Chinese (五分钟 → 四个半小时)
- I can use the duration structure: 每天 + verb + number + 小时/分钟 + 的 + noun (他每天弹一个小时的钢琴)
- I can describe how long someone does their hobby from a picture

## Materials Needed
- Projector (duration phrases list, Act. 5 pictures)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- Bingo cards (pre-prepared, one per student — 3×3 grid with duration phrases in Chinese)
- Strips of paper for Writing Relay (one set per team)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** 一边……一边…… structure from Lesson 7.2; hobby vocabulary
**How:** Mini whiteboard quick quiz — teacher shows an image of someone doing two things at once (or describes in English), students write the Chinese sentence. 4 rounds. Then quick choral read of Text 1 from memory.
**Connection:** "At the end of Text 1, 田云 says 我每天读一个小时的书 — she reads for one hour every day. Today we're going to pull apart that structure so you can say the same thing about your own hobbies."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read together. Leave visible for the whole lesson.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Duration Time Phrases (Act. 3, p. 62)
Introduce the full list — choral repetition after each:

| 中文 | English |
|---|---|
| 五分钟 | 5 minutes |
| 十分钟 | 10 minutes |
| 一刻钟 | 15 minutes (a quarter of an hour) |
| 半个小时 | 30 minutes (half an hour) |
| 三刻钟 | 45 minutes |
| 一个小时 | 1 hour |
| 一个半小时 | 1.5 hours |
| 两个小时 | 2 hours |
| 两个小时零五分 | 2 hours and 5 minutes |
| 两个小时十分钟 | 2 hours and 10 minutes |
| 三个小时一刻钟 | 3 hours and 15 minutes |
| 四个半小时 | 4.5 hours |

Point out the pattern: compound times = 小时 amount + 零/minutes.

#### Key Sentence Pattern
**Duration structure:** Subject + 每天 + verb + duration + 的 + noun

> 他每天弹**一个小时**的**钢琴**。 (He plays piano for one hour every day.)
> 她每天唱**半个小时**的**歌**。 (She sings for half an hour every day.)
> 我每天看**两个小时**的**电视**。 (I watch TV for two hours every day.)

Think-aloud: "Notice: verb comes first (弹), then the time (一个小时), then 的, then the object (钢琴). The time goes BETWEEN the verb and the object — this is different from English."

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Translate: 'She reads for 45 minutes every day.' Write on your whiteboard." (她每天读三刻钟的书。)
- "What's wrong? 他每天弹钢琴一个小时。" (time should come between verb and object: 弹一个小时的钢琴)
- "How do you say 'one and a half hours'?" (一个半小时)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Act. 3 Oral Drill — We Do (6 min)
Teacher points to each duration phrase on the projector — class chorally says it. Then teacher says the English, class says the Chinese. Build speed. Finish with a rapid-fire round: teacher says random durations in English, students write the Chinese on whiteboards and hold up.
- **Scaffold:** List stays visible throughout.
- **Extension:** Students close eyes and say the list in order from memory.
- **OTR:** Every student responds chorally or writes on whiteboard every round.

#### Activity 2: Act. 5 — Duration Pictures — We Do→You Do (8 min)
Display the 4 pictures from Act. 5 (p. 63). Each shows a person doing a hobby for a specific duration. Teacher models picture 1: "Picture 1: 田云每天弹一个小时的钢琴。" Students do pictures 2–4 in pairs (spoken). Cold call 2 pairs to share.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame on board: ______每天______的______。
- **Extension:** Students invent a 5th scenario and write the sentence (not from the textbook).
- **OTR:** Cold call — ask first, name second. All students prepare an answer before anyone is named.

#### Activity 3: Bingo — Duration Phrases (8 min)
Pre-prepared Bingo cards: 3×3 grid, each square has a duration phrase written in English. Teacher calls out durations in Chinese — students mark off the English equivalent. Three-in-a-row wins. Play 2–3 rounds (redistribute cards each round).
- **Setup:** Pre-printed Bingo cards (or students write 9 chosen phrases in a grid in the first 2 minutes).
- **Scaffold:** Duration phrase list stays on projector.
- **Extension:** Students call the phrases in Chinese for the final round.

#### Activity 4: Writing Relay — You Do (1 min)
Teams of 4. One strip per team with 4 duration sentences to complete (fill the blank duration/object). First team to correctly complete all 4 wins.

Example sentences on the strip:
1. 他每天打______的球。(两个小时)
2. 她每天唱______的歌。(一刻钟)
3. 我每天读______的书。(半个小时)
4. 他每天听______的音乐。(三刻钟)

- **Scaffold:** Duration phrase list visible.
- **Extension:** Team writes one extra sentence of their own at the bottom.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read aloud together. Students self-assess: thumbs up / sideways / down for each.
- **Self-assessment:** Ask: "Which duration phrase was hardest to remember?"
- **Exit ticket:** "Write this sentence in Chinese: 'She plays guitar for one hour every day.'" (她每天弹一个小时的吉他。)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we'll work on pinyin — specifically how to tell the difference between ao and ou sounds, and we'll do two listening exercises."
