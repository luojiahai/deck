# Lesson 7.1: Hobby Vocabulary — Music Actions

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 1 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to name our hobbies in Chinese using music and leisure vocabulary.

## Success Criteria
- I can say 8+ hobby words in Chinese (弹钢琴, 唱歌, 听音乐, 读书, 看电视, 弹吉他, 玩电脑游戏…)
- I can say what my hobby is using 我喜欢…… (wǒ xǐhuān…)
- I can mime actions when I hear them said in Chinese (TPR)

## Materials Needed
- Projector (for vocabulary slides: 汉字 + pinyin + English)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- Printed or projected vocab list (Text 1 New Words + Extra Words)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Key vocabulary and sentence patterns from Unit 2 — daily activities, time expressions, and verbs (e.g. 吃饭, 上学, 睡觉, 几点)
**How:** Flashcard rapid fire — teacher shows Chinese character on projector, students chorally say pinyin + English; then reverse (teacher says English, students write Chinese on mini whiteboards and hold up)
**Connection:** "Last unit we talked about daily routines — today we start a new topic: 爱好 (hobbies). You'll use the same activity verbs in a new context."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read the learning intention aloud together. Read each success criterion — ask students to give a thumbs up if they already know any of the words listed.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 爱好 | àihào | hobby |
| 弹钢琴 | tán gāngqín | play the piano |
| 唱歌 | chànggē | sing a song |
| 听音乐 | tīng yīnyuè | listen to music |
| 读书 | dúshū | read a book / study |
| 小时 | xiǎoshí | hour |
| 看电视 | kàn diànshì | watch TV |
| 弹吉他 | tán jítā | play guitar |
| 玩电脑游戏 | wán diànnǎo yóuxì | play computer games |

Introduce each word: show 汉字 → say pinyin → give English. Class repeats twice after each word (choral). Keep pinyin visible throughout.

Point out instrument verb pattern: **弹 (tán)** = play a plucked/keyboard instrument → 弹钢琴 (piano), 弹吉他 (guitar). Tell students they'll meet a different verb for bowed strings later.

#### Key Sentence Pattern
> 我喜欢 + hobby.
> 我喜欢弹钢琴。/ 我喜欢唱歌。/ 我喜欢看电视。

Model 3 examples. Use a "silly" one: 我喜欢玩电脑游戏，不喜欢读书！(pause for reactions)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "What does 弹 mean? Write it on your whiteboard." (check: play a string/keyboard instrument)
- "How would I say 'I like listening to music'? Write it." (check: 我喜欢听音乐)
- "What's different about 弹钢琴 and 弹吉他?" (check: same verb, different instrument)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: TPR — Say It and Do It (Act. 1, p. 61) — We Do (8 min)
Teacher calls out a hobby verb (e.g. 弹钢琴, 唱歌, 玩电脑游戏) — students mime the action. Build speed. Then reverse: teacher mimes, students call out the Chinese word.
- **Scaffold:** Vocab list stays visible on projector; students can glance if unsure.
- **Extension:** Students take turns as the caller (say the word for the class to mime).
- **OTR:** Every student responds to every word — whole-class TPR.

#### Activity 2: Vocab Matching — Writing (7 min)
Students draw a quick 3-column table on their mini whiteboards (or paper): 汉字 | 拼音 | English. Teacher calls out 6 words — students write all three columns from memory, then check against the projector.
- **Scaffold:** Pinyin remains on projector; students only need to fill the English column if writing from pinyin is too hard.
- **Extension:** Students hide the projector with a book and complete all three columns from memory.
- **OTR:** Students hold up whiteboards — teacher scans for errors and addresses the 1–2 most common ones.

#### Activity 3: Pair Chain Drill — Speaking (6 min)
A: 你有什么爱好？
B: 我喜欢______。你呢？
A: 我喜欢______。

Students alternate, using a different hobby each time. Goal: use at least 5 different words in the chain.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame on board: 我喜欢______。你有什么爱好？
- **Extension:** Students must add a second hobby: 我喜欢弹钢琴，也喜欢唱歌。
- **OTR:** Turn and talk — every student speaks during this activity.

#### Activity 4: Write My Hobbies — You Do (2 min)
Students write 3 sentences on their whiteboard: one true hobby, one false hobby, one hobby they've never tried. They'll use these in the next lesson.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame provided: 我喜欢______。
- **Extension:** Write a fourth sentence using 不喜欢: 我不喜欢______。

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Teacher reads each criterion aloud; students raise their hand if they feel they've met it.
- **Self-assessment:** Thumbs up / sideways / down for each criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write 3 hobby words in Chinese characters — no pinyin." Collected on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we'll read a text about a student's hobbies and learn how to say you're doing two things at the same time."
