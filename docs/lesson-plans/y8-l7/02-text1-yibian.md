# Lesson 7.2: Text 1 + 一边……一边……

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 2 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to describe doing two things at the same time using 一边……一边……

## Success Criteria
- I can read and understand Text 1 (田云's hobbies)
- I can form 一边……一边…… sentences to describe simultaneous actions (我喜欢一边X一边Y)
- I can put words in the correct order in a 一边……一边…… sentence

## Materials Needed
- Projector (Text 1, sentence jumble strips projected or printed)
- CD / audio file: Track 31 (Text 1)
- Printed or projected sentence strips for Running Dictation (4 sentences from Text 1 posted around room)
- Mini whiteboards + markers

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Lesson 7.1 hobby vocabulary — 弹钢琴, 唱歌, 听音乐, 读书, 看电视, 弹吉他, 玩电脑游戏, 爱好
**How:** Slap the Board — write 8 hobby words on the board in Chinese. Two students at the board, teacher says the English or pinyin, they race to slap the correct character. Rotate 3 rounds.
**Connection:** "You can already say these hobbies. Today's character 田云 has the same hobbies — let's read what she says about them."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Point to each criterion and ask: "By the end of today, you should be able to tick all three of these."

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Key Sentence Pattern
**一边……一边……** = doing X while doing Y (one person, two simultaneous actions)

> 我喜欢**一边**弹钢琴，**一边**唱歌。
> 他**一边**听音乐，**一边**读书。
> 她**一边**吃饭，**一边**看电视。

Model each example. Think-aloud on the third: "First I need my subject (她), then 一边, then the first action (吃饭), comma, 一边, then the second action (看电视)."

Critical point: both actions must be done by the **same person** at the **same time**. It is NOT used for two people doing one thing together (that's 跟……一起, which comes in Lesson 8).

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Is this correct? 我一边弹钢琴，我一边唱歌。" (No — don't repeat the subject)
- "What's wrong? 一边我听音乐，一边读书。" (一边 goes after the subject, not before)
- "Thumbs up if both actions are happening at the same time: 我喜欢一边吃饭一边看电视." (yes ✓)

#### Text / Dialogue
**Text 1 (p. 60, CD Track 31):**
> 我叫田云。我有很多爱好。我喜欢**一边**弹钢琴，**一边**唱歌。我还喜欢听音乐和读书。我每天读一个小时的书。

1. Teacher plays CD T31 — students listen with books closed.
2. Play again — students follow along in the text.
3. Choral read together (twice).
4. Comprehension questions:
   - 田云有什么爱好？(She likes playing piano while singing, listening to music, reading)
   - 她每天读几个小时的书？(一个小时)
   - What two things does she do at the same time? (弹钢琴 and 唱歌)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Choral Text Read + Pair Role-Read — We Do (5 min)
Read Text 1 together twice as a class. Then pairs: A reads the text aloud, B listens and corrects tones/pronunciation. Swap. Both students read the full text once each.
- **Scaffold:** Pinyin stays visible on projector.
- **Extension:** Students try to read from character-only version (teacher hides pinyin).
- **OTR:** Every student reads aloud during pair work.

#### Activity 2: Act. 2 — 6 Pictures, 一边……一边…… Sentences (p. 61) — We Do→You Do (8 min)
Display the 6 pictures from Act. 2. For the first 2 pictures, teacher models: "Picture 1 — what two things is this person doing? So I say: 他喜欢一边______，一边______。" Students then do pictures 3–6 in pairs, speaking aloud. Finally, 2 volunteers share their sentences for the class to check.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame on board: ______喜欢一边______，一边______。
- **Extension:** Students add a time expression: 他每天晚上一边……一边……
- **OTR:** Cold call 2 students for pictures 5 and 6 to share with the class.

#### Activity 3: Sentence Jumble — Game (5 min)
Project 3 jumbled 一边……一边…… sentences (words in wrong order). Students race to write the correct sentence on their mini whiteboard. First team/student to hold up the correct sentence wins the point.

Example jumbles:
- 我 / 一边 / 唱歌 / 一边 / 弹钢琴 / 喜欢 → 我喜欢一边弹钢琴，一边唱歌。
- 他 / 读书 / 听音乐 / 一边 / 一边 → 他一边听音乐，一边读书。
- 妈妈 / 一边 / 做饭 / 看电视 / 一边 / 喜欢 → 妈妈喜欢一边做饭，一边看电视。

- **Scaffold:** Remind students: subject → 一边 → action 1 → 一边 → action 2.
- **Extension:** Students write a fourth jumbled sentence for a partner to solve.

#### Activity 4: Running Dictation — Game (5 min)
Post 4 sentences from Text 1 around the room on separate sheets. In pairs: A runs to read a sentence and memorises it, returns and dictates to B who writes it. Swap roles for next sentence. First pair to correctly write all 4 wins.

Sentences:
1. 我叫田云。我有很多爱好。
2. 我喜欢一边弹钢琴，一边唱歌。
3. 我还喜欢听音乐和读书。
4. 我每天读一个小时的书。

- **Scaffold:** Partners may help each other check characters.
- **Extension:** After finishing, students write a 5th sentence about themselves in the same style.
- **OTR:** Both partners read and write during this activity.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read each criterion. Students show thumbs up/sideways/down for each.
- **Self-assessment:** "Which of the three success criteria are you most confident about? Which do you want more practice on?"
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence using 一边……一边…… about something YOU do." Collected on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we'll learn how to say how long you do something — 我每天读一个小时的书. We'll pull apart that structure and practise it."
