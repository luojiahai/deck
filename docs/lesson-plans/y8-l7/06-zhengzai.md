# Lesson 7.6: 正在 (Present Continuous)

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Hobby (1): Music 音乐 | Lesson 6 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to describe actions that are happening right now using 正在.

## Success Criteria
- I can form 正在 + verb sentences (他正在弹钢琴)
- I can ask and answer 你正在……吗？ / 我正在……
- I can use 正在 in a partner dialogue about what people are doing at this moment

## Materials Needed
- Projector (Act. 9 pictures, Act. 10 stroke count display)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- No other printed materials needed

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Text 2 vocabulary — 小说, 杂志, 画儿/国画儿/油画儿/水彩画儿; also 正在 (preview from last lesson)
**How:** Flashcard rapid fire — teacher says English, students write the Chinese character on mini whiteboards. 6 words. Then: "You saw 正在 in Text 2 last lesson — what do you think it means?" Take 2–3 answers before confirming.
**Connection:** "You already met 正在 in Text 2: 我正在学画国画儿. Today we pull it apart and practise it so you can use it yourselves."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Read together. Keep visible throughout.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### Key Sentence Pattern
**正在 + verb** = action in progress right now (present continuous)

> 他**正在**弹钢琴。 (He is playing the piano right now.)
> 她**正在**唱歌。 (She is singing right now.)
> 你**正在**听什么音乐？ (What music are you listening to right now?)

Contrast with 喜欢 (habitual): 他喜欢弹钢琴 (He likes playing piano — general truth) vs 他正在弹钢琴 (He is playing piano — right now, at this moment).

Model question form: 你正在做什么？→ 我正在______。

Include a silly example: 你们正在学中文。是不是？(Yes — they are, right now.)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Which means 'he plays piano every day' — 他喜欢弹钢琴 or 他正在弹钢琴?" (他喜欢弹钢琴 — habitual)
- "Write: 'She is reading a novel right now.'" (她正在看小说。)
- "What's wrong? 他正在弹了钢琴。" (正在 already signals present tense — don't add 了)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Act. 9 — 正在 Sentences from Pictures — We Do (8 min)
Display the Act. 9 pictures (p. 65–66 — people doing various actions at this moment). Teacher models the first picture: "Picture 1 — 他正在……" Students call out the action. Then pairs: for each remaining picture, students race to say the complete 正在 sentence before their partner does. Share 2–3 sentences with the class.
- **Scaffold:** Verb bank on the board: 弹钢琴, 唱歌, 听音乐, 读书, 画画儿, 看电视, 玩电脑游戏, 弹吉他.
- **Extension:** Students add a second clause: 他正在弹钢琴，一边唱歌。(combining today's grammar with 一边……一边……)
- **OTR:** Rapid-fire — teacher points to pictures at random, cold calls students.

#### Activity 2: Mime & Guess Pair Dialogue — You Do (8 min)
In pairs:
- A mimes an action (silently — no sounds)
- B asks: 你正在______吗？or 你在做什么？
- A answers: 是的，我正在______。or 不是，我正在______。
- Swap. Each student mimes at least 3 times.

After 6 minutes, 2 volunteer pairs perform for the class.
- **Scaffold:** Question frame on board: 你正在______吗？ / 你在做什么？
- **Extension:** Students must use 一边……一边…… in at least one mime (mime two things at once — e.g. air-piano while mouthing singing).
- **OTR:** Every student both mimes and speaks during this activity.

#### Activity 3: Act. 10 — Stroke Count Competition (5 min)
Project the 4 new vocabulary characters from this lesson or from Text 2 one at a time. Students count the strokes silently and write the number on their whiteboard. First student to hold up the correct count wins a point. After 4 characters, teacher reveals stroke counts.

Use characters from Text 2 vocabulary: 画 (8 strokes), 志 (7 strokes), 彩 (11 strokes), 正 (5 strokes).

Note: This is a fun accuracy check, not a writing test — keep it light and fast.
- **Scaffold:** Students may trace the character with their finger while counting.
- **Extension:** Students write the character AND the count.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Teacher reads each criterion. Students demonstrate criterion 2 live: teacher points to a student — student asks the person next to them 你正在做什么？partner answers 我正在……中文课。
- **Self-assessment:** Thumbs up / sideways / down for each criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write 2 正在 sentences — one about yourself right now, one about someone else in the room." (E.g. 我正在学中文。老师正在教我们。)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we cover 都 — 'both/all'. You already saw it in Text 2: 油画儿、水彩画儿，我都喜欢画. We'll learn exactly how to build that structure."
