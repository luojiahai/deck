## Lesson 6: Asking the Time (现在几点？) — Dialogue Practice

**Option: B — Whole-class interaction**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to ask and answer "What time is it?" in natural Chinese conversations.

### Success Criteria
- I can ask the time using 现在几点 (用现在几点问时间)
- I can answer with the most natural time expression (用最自然的表达回答)
- I can have a real conversation about time with classmates (跟同学进行关于时间的对话)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV
- A4 paper
- Pre-assigned "watch cards" for Walk and Talk (small slips, one per student, each with a different time)
- Find Someone Who sheets (printed, one per student)
- Timer visible on projector for Walk and Talk rounds

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** All five time patterns + choosing the most natural expression (from Lesson 5).

**How:** Projector shows 10 clock faces. Students write the MOST NATURAL Chinese expression on paper. Hold up. Quick scan. Times mix all patterns, some deliberately ambiguous: 3:00, 7:55, 12:30, 5:45, 9:10, 2:50, 6:15, 11:45, 8:07, 4:00. After each, choral read the correct answer.

**Connection:** "You can tell any time in Chinese now. But if someone walks up to you and asks the time, can you answer? Today: the question AND the conversation."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display. "Today's goal: by the end, you'll have spoken to at least 10 different classmates in Chinese about time."

#### I Do (10–18 min)

##### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 现在 | xiànzài | now |
| 几 | jǐ | how many; what (number question) |
| 表 | biǎo | watch |

##### Key Question: 现在几点？

Breakdown on projector:
- 现在 (xiànzài) = now
- 几 (jǐ) = what (question word for numbers)
- 点 (diǎn) = o'clock
- → "Now what time?" → "What time is it?"

##### Model Dialogue 1: Basic Q&A
```
A: 现在几点？
B: 两点半。
A: 谢谢！
B: 不客气。
```
Teacher models with a confident student. Then students practise in pairs (30 seconds each role).

##### Model Dialogue 2: Verification
```
A: 现在几点？
B: 差十分九点。
A: 差十分九点？ (surprised — already?!)
B: 对。
```
Adds a natural repetition-for-confirmation pattern.

##### Model Dialogue 3: Extended
```
A: 请问，现在几点？
B: 七点一刻。
A: 谢谢！我要去上课了。
B: 好，再见！
```
Adds 请问 (polite) and a reason for asking (going to class).

##### Natural Answer Guide
Quick recap on projector — choose the best pattern when answering:

| Time | Most natural answer |
|---|---|
| 3:00 | 三点 |
| 3:15 | 三点一刻 |
| 3:30 | 三点半 |
| 3:45 | 三点三刻 |
| 3:10 | 三点十分 |
| 3:50 | 差十分四点 |

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "How do you ask 'What time is it?'" (Choral: 现在几点？)
- "If the time is 7:45, what's the best answer — 七点四十五分, 七点三刻, or 差一刻八点?" (七点三刻 — shortest and most natural)

#### Flexible Practice (18–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Chain Dialogue — We Do (7 min)
Whole class. Teacher starts: turns to Student 1 and asks "现在几点？" Student 1 answers with any time (must use a different pattern from the previous student), then turns to Student 2 and asks "现在几点？" Student 2 answers, asks Student 3. Continue around the entire class. Rules:
- No repeating a time someone already said
- No repeating a pattern twice in a row (if previous student used 半, you must use 分/刻/差/点)
- Answer within 5 seconds or you're out (playful — just skip and move on)

Teacher notes pronunciation issues during the chain. After the full round, give 3 quick targeted corrections: "I heard a few people say 二点半 — remember, it's 两点半."

- **Scaffold:** Pattern summary visible on projector throughout.
- **Extension:** Fluent students must use 差 in their answer.
- **OTR:** Every student speaks at least once. ~25 speaking moments in 7 minutes.

##### Activity 2: Walk and Talk — You Do (10 min)
Each student receives a "watch card" with a time written on it (digits + most natural Chinese). Example cards: "2:30 / 两点半", "7:55 / 差五分八点", "3:15 / 三点一刻".

Students stand up and walk around the room. Teacher sets timer for 3 minutes. In that time, each student must ask at least 5 different classmates "现在几点？" and record their answers (digits on paper).

Round 1 (3 min): Basic. Just ask and record.
Round 2 (7 min): Students must now write down BOTH the digits AND the Chinese expression they heard. After asking, they must also respond with a short follow-up: "谢谢！" or "真的吗？" (Really?) or "好的！"

Teacher walks around, listens in, notes common errors for feedback at end.

- **Scaffold:** Prompt sheet on projector: 现在几点？ / 谢谢！ / 不客气 / 真的吗？ / 好的
- **Extension:** Fluent students act as "time verifiers" — they walk around checking that others' answers match their watch card times, correcting any errors.
- **OTR:** Massive — every student has 10+ speaking interactions.

##### Activity 3: Find Someone Who — Time Edition (6 min)
Each student gets a Find Someone Who sheet with 6 prompts:

1. Find someone whose time is in the morning (早上) → Write their name + the time: _______
2. Find someone whose answer uses 半 → Name + time: _______
3. Find someone whose answer uses 差 → Name + time: _______
4. Find someone whose answer uses 刻 → Name + time: _______
5. Find someone whose time is after 6:00 PM → Name + time: _______
6. Find someone whose time has a 零 in it → Name + time: _______

Students move around asking. First to fill all 6 shouts "好了!" and wins. Winner reads their findings to the class.

- **Setup needed:** Printed Find Someone Who sheets. Watch cards from previous activity reused.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** "Who spoke to at least 5 people? 8? 10? Show of hands." Celebration of the number of interactions.
- **Self-assessment:** "On your paper, rate your conversation skills today: 1 — I mostly pointed at cards, 2 — I said the Chinese but needed help, 3 — I had real conversations in Chinese."
- **Exit ticket:** "Write a short dialogue: A asks the time, B answers, A says thank you." Hand in on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: Time Olympics! Four rounds of competition to see who's the time-telling champion."
