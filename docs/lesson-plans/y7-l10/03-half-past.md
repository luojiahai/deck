## Lesson 3: Half Past (半)

**Option: A — Cumulative mastery**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to say half-past times using 半, and to choose between 点, 分, and 半 when telling time.

### Success Criteria
- I can say half-past times using 半 (用半说半点时间)
- I can tell the difference between X点半 and X点三十分 (区分半和三十分)
- I can see a clock and pick the right pattern — 点, 分, or 半 (选择合适的表达：点、分或半)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV
- A4 paper
- Pre-printed cumulative Bingo cards (9 squares: mix of o'clock, minutes, and half-past times in digits)
- Board space for Writing Relay

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–10 min) — Added word-building from textbook p87

**Part A: Speed Correct (5 min)**
Projector shows Chinese time expression. Students write ✓ or ✗ on paper, hold it up. Quick pace — 8 items, 30 seconds each:

1. 两点 ✓
2. 四点半 ✓ (preview!)
3. 二点十分 ✗ (两!)
4. 三点五分 ✗ (missing 零)
5. 六点零五分 ✓
6. 十二点 ✓
7. 七点零二分 ✓
8. 二点半 ✗ (两点半!)

**Part B: Word Building (Textbook p87) (5 min)**
The textbook p87 has an "Add missing words to form phrases" exercise. Project these half-words. Students write the COMPLETE two-character word on paper:

| Clue | Complete word | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 明__ | 名字 | name |
| 什__ | 什么 | what |
| 现__ | 现在 | now |
| 多__ | 多少 | how many |
| 秘__ | 秘书 | secretary (from Unit 3) |
| 好__ | 好看 | good-looking |
| 年__ | 年级 | year/grade |
| 老__ | 老师 | teacher |

Fast — 30 seconds each. "These are words you already know. Building them from memory keeps them fresh."

**Connection:** "You spotted 半 in the speed correct. Today: half past. It's the easiest one yet."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (10–12 min)
Display. "Today's goal: when you see a clock showing X:30, the answer comes out automatically."

#### I Do (10–18 min)

##### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 半 | bàn | half |

##### Key Pattern: X点半 = X:30
Clock images on projector:
- 两点半 (2:30)
- 六点半 (6:30) 
- 十一点半 (11:30)
- 十二点半 (12:30)

**Note:** X点半 = X点三十分. Both correct. 半 is the shortcut — more common in speech.

##### Cumulative Comparison Chart
Projector shows this table — ask students to identify which pattern to use:

| Clock | Pattern | Chinese |
|---|---|---|
| 3:00 | 点 | 三点 |
| 7:20 | 分 | 七点二十分 |
| 6:30 | 半 | 六点半 |
| 9:05 | 分 + 零 | 九点零五分 |
| 2:30 | 半 + 两! | 两点半 |
| 11:11 | 分 (no 零 — 11 is two digits) | 十一点十一分 |
| 12:00 | 点 | 十二点 |
| 5:07 | 分 + 零 | 五点零七分 |

Walk through each: "What's the pattern here? 3:00 — which one? 点! 7:20 — which one? 分!"

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "2:30 — fingers: 1 for 两点半, 2 for 二点半." (1 — 两点半!)
- "Is 四点半 and 四点三十分 the same?" (Yes — thumbs up)
- "Write 8:30 on paper. Hold it up." (八点半 — or 八点三十分, both fine)

#### Flexible Practice (18–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Cumulative Clock Flash — We Do (8 min)
Projector shows 20 clock faces in rapid sequence — a mix of o'clock, minutes (with and without 零), and half-past. Whole class chorally says each time. Start slow (5 seconds per clock), accelerate (3 seconds, then 2). Goal: automaticity. Sequence builds from easy (3:00, 6:30, 9:00) to hard (2:30, 5:07, 12:30, 3:05).

- **Scaffold:** Pattern chart visible on projector for first 10 clocks. Then hidden for last 10.
- **Extension:** Fluent students add 现在 before every answer. (现在三点半)
- **OTR:** Choral response — 20 OTR moments in 8 minutes.

##### Activity 2: Cumulative Bingo — You Do (9 min)
Pre-printed Bingo cards with 9 digital times in a 3×3 grid (mix of o'clock, minutes, half-past). Teacher calls times in Chinese. Students circle the matching digit. First to complete a row wins. Winner must read their row back in Chinese to claim the prize.

- **Scaffold:** Pattern reference on projector during game.
- **Extension:** Winners must also say whether each time uses 分 or 半.

##### Game: Writing Relay — Cumulative (6 min)
4 teams. Each team lines up. Teacher calls a time (e.g. "Half past two!"). First student in each line runs to the board, writes the Chinese (两点半), runs back. Teacher calls next time. Mix o'clock (三点), minutes (五点二十分), half-past (七点半), and trap times (2:30 — must write 两点半, not 二点半). Fastest correct team wins the point.

Times: Round 1 — 三点 (warm-up). Round 2 — 八点半. Round 3 — 五点零五分 (零 trap!). Round 4 — 两点半 (两 trap!). Round 5 — 十二点半. Round 6 — 九点十一分.

- **Setup needed:** Board space for 4 teams to write simultaneously.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read. "Show me with fingers: 1 — mastered all of them, 2 — mostly there, fist — still confused."
- **Self-assessment:** "On your paper, draw a clock showing a time you find easy to say in Chinese. Now draw a clock showing a time you find hard." Teacher scans — this reveals which patterns need more work.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write 2:30 in Chinese." (两点半 — the double-trap: 两 + 半. This single item tests both rules.)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: quarters — 刻. You'll be able to say :15 and :45."
