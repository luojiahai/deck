## Lesson 5: Minutes To (差)

**Option: A — Scaffolded, step by step**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to say "minutes to" times using 差.

### Success Criteria
- I can calculate how many minutes are left until the next hour (心算差几分)
- I can use the correct word order: 差 + minutes + 分 + hour + 点 (正确使用差的结构)
- I can choose the most natural expression for any given time (选择最自然的表达)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV
- A4 paper
- Pre-cut Sentence Jumble sets — one envelope per pair (word cards: 差, 十分, 七点, etc.)
- "Minutes-to" calculation warm-up sheet (optional — can do on projector)

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** All time patterns so far — especially 三刻, which is conceptually closest to 差 (both express time relative to the next hour).

**How:** Mini whiteboard dig.. actually, paper. "I'll show you 8 clocks. Write the NATURAL Chinese expression. Ready?" Projector shows: 3:00, 7:15, 12:30, 5:45, 9:05, 2:00, 6:15, 8:30. Students write, then choral read the answers together.

Emphasise 5:45 → 五点三刻. "Notice: 三刻 means we're looking TOWARD the next hour in a way. Today we learn a pattern that does the same thing for EVERY minute, not just quarters."

**Connection:** "三刻 = 45 minutes PAST the hour. But what if it's 6:50? We could say 六点五十分... but there's a faster, more natural way. Today: 差."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display. Explain 差 (chà) = "fall short of" — imagine you're climbing toward the next hour and you've still got X minutes to go.

#### I Do (10–22 min) — Extended: three scaffolded layers

##### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 差 | chà | fall short of; to |
| 现在 | xiànzài | now |
| 表 | biǎo | watch |

##### Radical Spotlight (Textbook p85): 彡 and 衤

The final 2 of the textbook's 6 radicals. These connect to the new vocabulary:

| 部首 | Name | Meaning | Role in this lesson |
|---|---|---|---|
| 彡 | sān (三撇旁) | ornament; decoration | Seen in 形 (form), 影 (shadow), 彩 (colour). Related to visual/time concepts. |
| 衤 | yī (衣字旁) | clothing | **表** (watch/express) — the bottom component comes from 衣 (clothing). A watch is something you "wear"! |

Projector: break down 表 = top component + bottom (衤/衣). "Why does a watch contain the clothing radical? Because traditionally, a watch was something you wore — like clothing. This kind of logic helps you remember characters."

Students copy 彡 and 衤 into notebooks. "That's all 6 radicals from Lesson 10. You now know: 土, 寸, 刂, 米, 彡, 衤."

---

##### Layer 1: The Math (4 min)

Before they can say 差, they need to do the mental calculation. Projector shows digital times one at a time. Students calculate "how many minutes until the next hour?" and write JUST THE NUMBER on paper.

| Digital Time | Next Hour | Minutes To | Answer |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6:50 | 7:00 | ? | 10 |
| 7:55 | 8:00 | ? | 5 |
| 3:40 | 4:00 | ? | 20 |
| 9:49 | 10:00 | ? | 11 |
| 11:35 | 12:00 | ? | 25 |
| 2:58 | 3:00 | ? | 2 |

Students hold up paper after each. Fast pace — this is just the math warm-up. Cold call: "How did you figure it out?" (60 − minutes = minutes to next hour)

---

##### Layer 2: The Structure (4 min)

Now add the Chinese formula. Projector shows:

```
差 + MINUTES + 分 + HOUR + 点
```

Walk through one example step by step:
- "Ten to seven" → 10 minutes short of 7 o'clock
- Minutes: 十 → HOUR: 七点
- Put together: 差 + 十分 + 七点 = **差十分七点** (6:50)

More examples, students choral repeat:
- 差五分八点 (5 to 8 / 7:55)
- 差一刻十二点 (quarter to 12 / 11:45 — 一刻 works here too!)
- 差二十分四点 (20 to 4 / 3:40)
- 差一分六点 (1 to 6 / 5:59)

**Word order emphasis:** 差 always comes FIRST. English puts "to" in the middle ("ten TO seven"); Chinese puts 差 at the very beginning. "If you're writing and 差 is not the first character, something is wrong."

---

##### Layer 3: Choosing the Best Expression (4 min)

Now put it all together. For each time, there are TWO correct Chinese expressions. Which is more natural?

| Time | "Past" style | 差 style | More natural? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6:50 | 六点五十分 | 差十分七点 | 差 — shorter |
| 7:55 | 七点五十五分 | 差五分八点 | 差 — much shorter |
| 3:40 | 三点四十分 | 差二十分四点 | 差 — shorter |
| 10:45 | 十点四十五分 / 十点三刻 | 差一刻十一点 | 三刻 — even shorter than 差! |
| 9:10 | 九点十分 | — | 分 — 差 would be 差五十分十点, too long |

**Rule of thumb on projector:** If minutes > 30, consider 差. If minutes > 45, definitely use 差 (or 三刻 for :45). If minutes ≤ 30, use the "past" style.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "6:50 — which is more natural? 1 finger for 六点五十分, 2 fingers for 差十分七点." (2 — 差)
- "Write 7:55 in Chinese using 差." (差五分八点 — hold up paper)
- "What's the trap with 2:50?" (两点 — 差十分三点, must use 两!)

#### Flexible Practice (22–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Sentence Jumble — We Do (8 min)
Each pair gets an envelope with word cards. Teacher calls a time in English (or shows a clock). Pairs arrange the cards to form the correct 差 expression and hold it up. First correct pair gets a point.

Progression:
1. 差十分七点 (6:50) — straightforward
2. 差五分八点 (7:55) — different minutes and hour
3. 差一刻十二点 (11:45) — 一刻 in the middle position
4. 差二十分四点 (3:40) — larger minutes number
5. 差一分六点 (5:59) — almost the next hour
6. **差五分两点** (1:55) — TRAP: must use 两 not 二!
7. 差十一分十点 (9:49) — odd number
8. 差十分十二点 (11:50) — tricky: 十二 is two characters

- **Scaffold:** Formula 差 + X分 + Y点 on projector for rounds 1–4. Removed for 5–8.
- **Extension:** Winners must also say the "past" version for bonus point.
- **OTR:** Every pair physically manipulates cards and holds up answer — active OTR.

##### Activity 2: Clock → 差 + Past — You Do (7 min)
Worksheet: 8 clock faces showing times that favour 差 (6:50, 7:55, 3:40, 10:50, 2:55, 8:49, 12:45, 5:35). Students write BOTH the "past" version AND the 差 version for each. Then circle the one that's more natural.

- **Scaffold:** First 2 done as whole class. Formula visible.
- **Extension:** For each clock, write a full Q&A dialogue: A: 现在几点？ B: 差... A: 谢谢！

##### Game: Sentence Jumble Relay (6 min)
Same Sentence Jumble concept, but now as a relay. 4 teams. Each team's envelope is at the front. Teacher calls a time. First student in each team runs to the front, opens their envelope, arranges the cards on the floor/desk, runs back. Teacher checks. Fastest correct arrangement wins the point.

Times (prep 6 envelopes, identical sets for each team): 
R1: 差十分七点
R2: 差五分八点
R3: 差一刻三点
R4: 差二分六点

- **Setup needed:** 4 identical sets of pre-cut word cards. Space at front for arranging.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** "Three layers today: the math, the structure, the choice. How far did you get? Show fingers: 1 — just the math, 2 — got the structure too, 3 — can choose the best expression."
- **Self-assessment:** "On your paper, write the one 差 time you still find tricky." Teacher collects as exit data.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write 4:50 in Chinese using 差." (差十分五点)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: you've learned ALL FIVE patterns. Now we put them together and learn to ask 'What time is it?' — 现在几点？"
