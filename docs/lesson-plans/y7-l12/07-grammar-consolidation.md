## Lesson 7: Grammar Consolidation

**Option: C — Mixed/balanced**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to match questions to answers and use negation correctly for Unit 4 grammar.

### Success Criteria
- I can match a question to its correct answer (匹配问题和答案)
- I can form the question that matches a given answer (根据答案写问题)
- I can use 不 correctly to say someone doesn't work (用"不"造否定句)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards
- Grammar worksheet (Ex11, p.94 + Ex13, p.95), printed
- Listening exercise (Ex10, p.93)

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 6's interview questions and answers.

**How:** Chain response — each student adds one Q&A pair from memory around the class (student 1: 你几点起床？我七点起床。→ student 2: 你怎么上学？我坐校车上学。→ ...).

**Connection:** "You've practised asking and answering. Today we look closely at the GRAMMAR underneath — matching questions to answers precisely, and how to say 'not'."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project, choral read, keep visible.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Negation Pattern
我妈妈不工作。(My mum doesn't work.) — model 不 placement directly before the verb: 不 + 工作/开车/走路. Clarify: 不 goes right before the verb being negated, not before the time or transport word (我妈妈不坐公共汽车上班，她走路。).

##### Matching Sentence Halves (Ex11, p.94)
Model 2 example pairs, thinking aloud about how the question word signals the kind of answer to look for (怎么 → method; 几点 → time).

##### Forming the Question (Ex13, p.95)
Given an answer, students must produce the matching question. Model: Answer "坐地铁。" → Question: 你怎么上学？(or 上班？) — point out more than one valid question can be fine if the grammar is correct.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "我妈妈不工作。 — what does this mean?" (my mum doesn't work)
- Error correction: "我不妈妈工作了解。" (nonsense — reinforce 不 directly before the verb)
- "If the answer is '开车。', what question could it answer?" (你/你爸爸怎么上班/上学？)

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Match Sentence Halves — We Do (8 min, Writing)
Match sentence halves (Ex11) as a class first (projected), then independently on the worksheet. Check together.

- **Scaffold:** Question-word key displayed (怎么 = method, 几点 = time, 谁 = who) to support matching logic.
- **Extension:** Write one additional sentence pair of their own (question + answer) not from the worksheet.
- **OTR:** Whiteboards — write the matched letter, hold up each round.

##### Activity 2: Listening + Forming Questions — You Do (8 min)
Ex10 listening (p.93, CD T59) — tick correct answers about commute and work (6 items). Then the form-the-question worksheet (Ex13) independently.

- **Scaffold:** Word bank of question words visible.
- **Extension:** For Ex13, write TWO possible questions per answer where plausible.
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk to compare Ex13 answers before teacher reveals.

##### Game: Correct the Teacher (5 min)
Teacher presents 4 sentences with negation/word-order errors (我爸爸工作不。/ 妈妈不上班每天。); students find and fix on whiteboards, fastest correct answer wins a point for their table.

- **Setup needed:** None — whole-class.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On whiteboards: "Show me — does 不 go before or after the verb?" (before)
- **Exit ticket:** "Write: 'My dad doesn't drive.'" (我爸爸不开车。)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: everything comes together — a full paragraph about someone's daily routine, time, and transport, ready for Test 4.3."
