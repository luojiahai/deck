## Lesson 6: Partner Interviews

**Option: B — Scaffolded step-by-step**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to ask and answer a full set of interview questions about routine and transport.

### Success Criteria
- I can ask at least 6 interview questions about a classmate's routine and commute (用中文访问同学)
- I can answer interview questions about my own family (回答关于自己家庭的问题)
- I can report one fact about my partner to the class (向全班报告同伴的信息)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards
- Interview question sheet (Ex8, p.92 + Ex9, p.93), printed, with sentence frames
- Word bank handout

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 5's full sentence pattern (time + transport + activity).

**How:** Correct the Teacher — teacher writes/says 3 sentences with deliberate word-order errors (我开车七点上班。/ 他坐地铁每天上学。/ 你怎么上学几点？), students find and fix each on whiteboards.

**Connection:** "You can build these sentences confidently now. Today you'll use them to interview a partner — asking real questions and reporting back."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project, choral read, keep visible.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Interview Question Set (from Ex8, p.92 and Ex9, p.93 — core subset)
```
你几点起床？
你怎么上学？
你几点上课？
你爸爸/妈妈怎么上班？
你爸爸/妈妈几点下班？
你几点睡觉？
```
Model the full Q&A once with a confident volunteer in front of the class (teacher asks, student answers), thinking aloud about turning a one-word answer into a full sentence.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "How do you ask 'what time do you get up'?" — on whiteboards.
- Thumbs up/down: "起床 means 'get up'."
- "If your partner says 坐地铁, what's the English?" (subway)

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — fading scaffold across the phase

##### Activity 1: Guided Interview Round 1 — We Do (8 min)
In pairs, students ask each other 3 of the core questions using the printed question sheet with sentence frames and word bank fully visible. Teacher circulates, prompting.

- **Scaffold:** Full sentence frames + word bank on the handout; weaker pairs may answer in short phrases rather than full sentences.
- **Extension:** Fluent pairs must answer in full sentences, not single words.

##### Activity 2: Interview Round 2, Reduced Scaffold — You Do (8 min)
Students swap partners and ask 3 different questions (now including the father's commute, Ex9), with the word bank still visible but sentence frames removed.

- **Scaffold:** Word bank remains visible; teacher gives a sentence-starter card to pairs who need it.
- **Extension:** Add ONE follow-up question of their own choosing, and note the partner's answer to report back.
- **OTR:** Cold call 4 students to report one fact about their partner to the class (e.g. "我的同伴每天坐公共汽车上学。").

##### Closer: Find Someone Who (3 min, if time remains)
One question (你怎么上学？), mingling to find 3 classmates with different answers.

- **Setup needed:** Printed question sheets, no devices required.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On whiteboards: "Did you need the word bank by the end? 👍 no / 😐 sometimes / 👎 yes."
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence reporting a fact about your partner's commute."
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: we tighten up the grammar — matching sentence halves, forming questions from answers, and spotting negation."
