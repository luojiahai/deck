## Lesson 5: Synthesis — Time + Transport

**Option: C — Mixed/balanced**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to combine time expressions with transport to describe a full commute.

### Success Criteria
- I can say a sentence with time + transport + activity (说出时间+交通工具+活动的句子)
- I can listen and match transport + activity pairs (听力配对交通工具和活动)
- I can build a sentence in the correct word order every time (正确的词序造句)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards
- Scene cards (Ex6, p.91), printed/projected
- Listening tick-sheet (Ex7, p.92), printed
- Sentence-jumble word cards, one set per pair

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 10 time patterns (X点Y分/半/一刻/三刻, 差Y分X点) — brought back for today's synthesis.

**How:** Quick quiz on mini whiteboards — teacher shows 4 clock faces, students write the time in Chinese. Cold call 2 to read aloud.

**Connection:** "Remember how to tell time? Today we combine it with everything you know about transport — full sentences like 'At 7:30, my dad drives to work.'"

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project, choral read, keep visible.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Key Sentence Pattern
Subject + (每天) + Time + 坐/走路/开车 + Activity. Model 3 examples building complexity:
```
我爸爸七点半开车上班。
我妈妈每天八点坐地铁上班。
我哥哥七点一刻坐校车上学。
```
Silly example to grab attention: 我奶奶晚上十二点坐飞机去买菜 (wait for reactions).

##### Text/Scenes (Ex6, p.91)
5 scenes, each showing a person + transport icon + clock time. Think-aloud for scene 1, building the sentence element by element: subject → time → transport → activity.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "What comes first in the sentence — the time or the transport?" (time, then transport, then activity)
- Error correction: "我开车七点半上班。 — fix the word order." (我七点半开车上班。)
- "Make a sentence: Mum, 8:15, subway, work." — write on whiteboards.

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Scene Description, Choral → Pairs — We Do (8 min, Speaking)
Project each of the 5 Ex6 scenes one at a time; class chorally builds the sentence together first, then in pairs students take turns describing 2 scenes each aloud.

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame displayed: ___ + 点 + (坐+vehicle / 走路 / 开车) + 上班/上学。
- **Extension:** Add 每天 and a simple follow-up comment if time allows.
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk; cold call 3 pairs after.

##### Activity 2: Listening + Sentence Writing — You Do (8 min)
Ex7 listening (p.92, CD T58) — tick the correct transport/activity picture pairs (6 items). Then write 1 full sentence for 2 of the items heard.

- **Scaffold:** Picture answer options labelled with pinyin.
- **Extension:** Write sentences for all 6 items instead of 2.
- **OTR:** Whiteboards — hold up tick-sheet/sentences for a quick teacher scan.

##### Game: Sentence Jumble (5 min)
Word cards for one full sentence (我 / 每天 / 七点半 / 坐 / 校车 / 上学) scrambled; pairs race to arrange them in the correct order on their desk.

- **Setup needed:** Printed word cards, one set per pair.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On whiteboards: "Rate yourself — can you build a time+transport+activity sentence with no help?"
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence about your own commute, including a time."
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: you'll interview a partner about their family's routine and commute — get ready to ask lots of questions!"
