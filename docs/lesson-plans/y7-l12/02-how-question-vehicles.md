## Lesson 2: 怎么 + Vehicle Vocabulary

**Option: B — Scaffolded step-by-step**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to ask and answer how someone travels, using 怎么.

### Success Criteria
- I can ask "how do you get to school/work?" (你怎么上学/上班？)
- I can say the words for train, taxi, public bus and subway (火车, 出租车, 公共汽车, 地铁)
- I can answer using 坐 + the correct vehicle (用坐+交通工具回答)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards
- Word-bank cards, printed (for the compound-building demonstration)
- Sentence-frame handout

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 1 transport vocabulary and 坐/走路/开车 sentences.

**How:** Flashcard rapid fire — teacher shows a transport picture, class chorally says pinyin + English. Then cold call 2 students to make a sentence about their own family's commute.

**Connection:** "You can already say how your family travels. Today we learn to ASK the question — 怎么 — and add 4 more vehicle words."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read aloud. Students repeat chorally. Keep visible all lesson.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### New Vocabulary (p.90, Text 2) — built up character by character
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 怎 | zěn | how |
| 怎么 | zěn me | how |
| 火 | huǒ | fire |
| 火车 | huǒ chē | train |
| 租 | zū | rent |
| 出租 | chū zū | rent out |
| 出租车 | chū zū chē | taxi |
| 公 | gōng | public |
| 共 | gòng | public; common |
| 公共 | gōng gòng | public |
| 汽 | qì | gas; steam |
| 公共汽车 | gōng gòng qì chē | public bus |
| 地 | dì | ground |
| 铁(鐵) | tiě | iron |
| 地铁 | dì tiě | subway |

**Scaffolded build (core technique for this lesson):** introduce each compound one layer at a time on the projector — show 火 alone → add 车 → 火车. Show 出 + 租 → 出租 → + 车 → 出租车. Show 公 → 公共 → 公共汽车. Show 地 → + 铁 → 地铁. Point out the metal radical 钅 in 铁 — "iron/metal underground" is why 地铁 means subway.

##### Key Text 2 (p.90, CD T57)
```
你爸爸怎么上班？ / 坐火车。
你妈妈呢？ / 坐出租车。
你哥哥怎么上学？ / 坐公共汽车。
你怎么上学？ / 坐地铁。
```
Read aloud, choral read, then teacher think-alouds 2–3 more examples swapping in different family members and vehicles.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "怎么 asks about what?" (method/how) — thumbs up if correct.
- "How do you say 'How do you get to school?'" — write on whiteboards.
- Error correction: "你上学怎么？ — what's wrong?" (word order: 怎么 comes before the verb phrase → 你怎么上学？)

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Guided Chain Build — We Do (8 min)
Teacher models 你爸爸怎么上班？坐火车。 on the board, physically building the sentence with word cards one piece at a time. Students copy the process on mini whiteboards with a different family member + vehicle, closely guided.

- **Scaffold:** Full word bank + sentence frame projected throughout: 你___怎么___？坐___。
- **Extension:** Answer with 每天 added: 我每天坐地铁上学。
- **OTR:** Mini whiteboards, held up each round.

##### Activity 2: Pair Dialogue with Fading Scaffold — You Do (8 min)
Pairs practice the 4-line Text 2 dialogue, then swap in their own family members/vehicles. Round 1: word bank visible. Round 2: word bank removed, pairs work from memory.

- **Scaffold:** Keep pinyin visible for weaker pairs throughout both rounds.
- **Extension:** Fluent pairs add a follow-up question, e.g. A: 你怎么上学？B: 坐地铁。A: 你每天几点坐地铁？(recycling the Lesson 10 time question).
- **OTR:** Cold call 3 pairs to perform.

##### Game: Telephone (传声筒) (5 min)
Whisper-chain the sentence 你爸爸怎么上班？坐火车。 down a row of students; the last student says it aloud or writes it on the board. Compare to the original.

- **Setup needed:** None — whole-class activity.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On whiteboards: write 怎么 and rate your confidence 👍/😐/👎.
- **Exit ticket:** "Translate: 'How does your mum get to work?'" (你妈妈怎么上班？)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: the tricky 上/下 pairs — like 上车 and 下车 — plus pinyin practice."
