## Lesson 3: 上/下 Phrase Pairs + Pinyin

**Option: C — Mixed/balanced**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to use the 上/下 phrase pairs correctly and read pinyin for Lesson 12 vocabulary.

### Success Criteria
- I can match 上/下 phrase pairs to their meanings (匹配上/下词组)
- I can explain the difference between 上车 and 下车 (说出上车/下车的区别)
- I can circle the correct pinyin for Lesson 12 words (选出正确的拼音)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards
- Phrase-pair matching worksheet (Ex2, p.88), printed
- Pinyin discrimination worksheet (Ex3, p.88), printed

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 2's 怎么 question and the 4 vehicle words (火车, 出租车, 公共汽车, 地铁).

**How:** Pair dialogue — students redo the Text 2 dialogue from memory with a partner, then swap roles using different vehicles. Cold call 2 pairs.

**Connection:** "You can say HOW you travel. Today we look at related action pairs — going up, going down — 上班/下班, 上车/下车 — and tighten up your pinyin reading."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project, choral read, keep visible all lesson.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Phrase Pairs (p.88, Exercise 2)
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 上课 | shàng kè | attend class |
| 下课 | xià kè | finish class |
| 上学 | shàng xué | go to school |
| 放学 | fàng xué | classes are over |
| 上班 | shàng bān | go to work |
| 下班 | xià bān | finish work |
| 上车 | shàng chē | get on the vehicle |
| 下车 | xià chē | get off the vehicle |

Model each pair together, contrasting 上 (start/go up) with 下 (finish/go down). Point out 上学/放学 is the odd one out — it uses 放 rather than 下, because school "releases" you rather than you "descending" from it.

**Common error note:** word order is 坐 + vehicle + activity, NOT activity + 坐 + vehicle (我上学坐校车 ✗ → 我坐校车上学 ✓). Model the correction on the board.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- Error correction: "我上学坐校车。 — what's wrong?" (word order)
- "What's the odd one out: 上班/下班, 上车/下车, 上学/放学?" (上学/放学 — doesn't use 下)
- Quick translate: "get off the vehicle" → 下车

##### Pinyin Focus (p.88, Exercise 3)
6 discrimination pairs: 作zuò/zòu, 分fēn/fān, 谁suí/shuí, 点dǎn/diǎn, 住zhù/jù, 年nián/nán. Model 2 examples on the projector, thinking aloud about the tone/final difference.

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Phrase-Pair Matching — We Do (8 min, Writing)
Students match 8 pictures to the phrase box (Ex2). Check answers as a class, cold calling for each.

- **Scaffold:** Pinyin printed next to each phrase option.
- **Extension:** Write one full sentence using each of 2 chosen pairs (我们八点上课，三点半下课。).
- **OTR:** Mini whiteboards — hold up the matched letter/number as teacher reveals each picture.

##### Activity 2: Pair Drill of Phrase Pairs — You Do (8 min, Speaking)
Partner A says a phrase (e.g. 上车), Partner B must say its pair (下车) and give the English. Switch roles halfway.

- **Scaffold:** Word bank visible on the projector throughout.
- **Extension:** Partners build a mini dialogue using 2 pairs in context (我们几点上课？八点。几点下课？三点半。).
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk, every student responds.

##### Game: Pinyin Discrimination Quickfire (5 min)
Project one pinyin pair at a time from Ex3's 6 pairs; students write which one is correct on mini whiteboards, hold up. Fast-paced.

- **Setup needed:** Projector only.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On whiteboards: write the 上/下 pair you find trickiest.
- **Exit ticket:** "Correct this sentence: 我上学坐地铁。" (→ 我坐地铁上学。)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: 6 new radicals, a pinyin listening exercise, and a radical puzzle game."
