## Lesson 8: Full Synthesis / Test 4.3 Prep

**Option: A — Writing-focused**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to write a full paragraph describing someone's daily routine, including time and transport.

### Success Criteria
- I can write at least 4 connected sentences about someone's day (写出连贯的四句话描述一天)
- I can correctly sequence time, transport, and routine verbs in each sentence (正确排列时间、交通工具和动词)
- I can review and self-correct using the Unit 4 vocabulary list (核对第四单元词汇表)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards
- Unit 4 vocabulary revision sheet (compiled from the Test 4.3 coverage list)
- Paragraph-writing template/worksheet
- Model paragraph example, teacher-prepared (workbook-style, e.g. 王小天)

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 7's grammar — question/answer matching and negation.

**How:** Mini whiteboards quiz — teacher gives 4 answers, students write the matching question (e.g. "坐火车。" → 你怎么上班？); one negation sentence to translate.

**Connection:** "You've got the grammar solid. Today we put EVERYTHING from this unit together — time, routine, and transport — into one written paragraph, just like Test 4.3."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project, choral read, keep visible.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Unit 4 Vocabulary Revision (Test 4.3 coverage)
Project the combined revision table and choral read through it:

点, 一刻, 上班, 上学, 上车, 上课, 放学, 下车, 下课, 下班, 起床, 睡觉, 工作, 每天, 开车, 走路, 汽车, 地铁, 飞机, 出租车

**New items for today only (appear in Test 4.3 but not in earlier New Words tables):**
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 分钟 | fēn zhōng | minute (duration) | Contrast with 点/分 (a point in time, e.g. 三点) — 分钟 measures how long, e.g. 三十分钟 |
| 公车 | gōng chē | public bus (short form) | Abbreviation of 公共汽车 |
| 坐车 | zuò chē | travel by vehicle (general) | General form when the specific vehicle isn't named |
| 怎么去 | zěn me qù | how to get (there) | General form of 怎么上学/上班, used for any destination |
| 船 | chuán | boat | Recall 渡船 (ferry) from Lesson 1 |

##### Speaking Warm-Up (Ex14, p.95)
Before drafting, do a rapid speaking round: project the 5 compound-sentence scenes (subject + time + transport icon + activity) and have pairs produce each sentence aloud (2–3 min). This bridges straight from oral sentence production into today's written paragraph.

##### Model Paragraph (think-aloud, built step by step on the projector)
```
王小天七点起床。他七点一刻吃早饭。他每天坐公共汽车上学。
他们八点上课，下午三点半放学。他晚上九点半睡觉。
```
Point out the sequence every sentence follows: routine verb → time → transport (where relevant) → next routine verb.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "What are the 3 ingredients every good sentence needs here?" (time, activity, transport where relevant)
- Quick translate: "He goes to school by subway." (他坐地铁上学。)
- Error correction: teacher presents a jumbled model sentence; students fix the order on whiteboards.

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Guided Paragraph Drafting — We Do (8 min)
Using a template with 5 numbered blanks (matching the model's structure: wake up / breakfast / go to school + transport / class times / bedtime), students draft their OWN paragraph about themselves, supported by the vocabulary sheet.

- **Scaffold:** Word bank + sentence frames for each of the 5 slots; shorter, simpler sentences are fine.
- **Extension:** Add a 6th sentence about a family member's commute (我爸爸每天开车上班，五点半下班。), using 不 correctly if applicable.
- **OTR:** Mini whiteboards for the trickiest slot (transport + time) — check before moving on.

##### Activity 2: Independent Writing + Peer Proofreading — You Do (8 min)
Students complete their full paragraph independently, then swap with a partner to check word order and vocabulary using a checklist: (1) time before transport before activity, (2) 不 before the verb, (3) correct radicals in key characters.

- **Scaffold:** Checklist provided with the 3 key rules highlighted.
- **Extension:** Write a paragraph about someone else (a family member or friend) instead of themselves, requiring third-person adjustment.
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk peer check — every paragraph gets a second pair of eyes.

##### Review Activity: Whiteboard Quiz Relay (5 min)
Quickfire round through the full Unit 4 vocabulary revision list; teacher gives English, students write Chinese (or vice versa) on whiteboards, hold up immediately. As an extension layer, ask fluent students to also sort call-outs into categories (transport / routine / time) as they go.

- **Setup needed:** Projector for the vocabulary list, no devices required.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read against all three criteria; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** Students highlight on their own paragraph: one sentence they're proud of, one they're unsure about.
- **Exit ticket:** "Read your paragraph once more and circle the transport word you used."
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: Test 4.3 — covering time, daily routine, and transport."
