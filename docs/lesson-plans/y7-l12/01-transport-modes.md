## Lesson 1: Transport Modes + 坐/走路/开车

**Option: B — Writing-focused**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to name common modes of transport in Chinese and describe how family members get to school or work.

### Success Criteria
- I can name at least 6 modes of transport in Chinese (说出交通工具的名称)
- I can write sentences using 坐 + vehicle, and 走路/开车 (用坐/走路/开车造句)
- I can describe how someone in my family commutes (描述家人怎么上班/上学)

### Materials Needed
- Projector
- Mini whiteboards (one per student)
- Transport vocabulary picture cards, printed — one set per pair (校车, 火车, 电车, 飞机, 渡船, 出租车, 公共汽车, 走路 icon)
- Bingo grids (9-square, pre-made) — one per student
- Sentence-building worksheet, printed

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Lesson 11 daily routine verbs and time expressions (起床, 吃早饭, 上学, 上课, 放学, 睡觉).

**How:** Quick quiz on mini whiteboards — teacher shows 5 clock-face + activity-icon prompts (e.g. clock at 7:00 + school building), students write the matching sentence (我七点去上学). Cold call 2–3 students to read theirs aloud.

**Connection:** "You can now say WHEN you go to school. Today we learn HOW you get there — by bus, on foot, driving..."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read aloud. Students repeat each criterion once chorally. Keep visible all lesson.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Extra Words (p.87, orange box) — for recognition
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 火车 | huǒ chē | train |
| 电车 | diàn chē | tram |
| 飞机 | fēi jī | plane |
| 渡船 | dù chuán | ferry |
| 出租车 | chū zū chē | taxi |
| 公共汽车 | gōng gòng qì chē | public bus |

Model pronunciation twice per word, class repeats twice (choral). Between 走路 and 校车, remind students 走 also appears as a radical (走字旁) — recall from Lesson 11's radical group (走 → 起).

**Grammar note (generalise 每):** 每 + time word = "every" — 每天 (every day), and it extends to 每年 (every year), 每个星期 (every week). Write all three on the board even though only 每天 is used in this unit's sentences.

##### Key Text 1 (p.86, CD T55)
```
我爸爸开车上班。
我妈妈走路上班。
我每天坐校车上学。
```
Read aloud, students follow, then read chorally.

##### Key Sentence Pattern
- 坐 + vehicle (+ activity): 坐校车上学, 坐飞机上班 — silly example to grab attention: "我每天坐飞机上学" (wait for reactions)
- 走路 = walk — no 坐 needed: 走路上班/上学
- 开车 = drive: 开车上班

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "What does 走路 mean? Thumbs up if you know."
- "Do we say 坐走路, or just 走路?" (just 走路 — no 坐)
- "How would you say 'I drive to work'?" (我开车上班) — write on whiteboards.

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Whiteboard Vocab Matching — We Do (8 min)
Teacher projects each transport picture card one at a time (校车, 火车, 电车, 飞机, 渡船, 出租车, 公共汽车, 走路). Students write the pinyin + English on mini whiteboards, hold up. Teacher reveals and corrects.

- **Scaffold:** Word bank with pinyin + English printed at the bottom of the desk card for reference.
- **Extension:** Students also write the character (not just pinyin) for at least 4 of the 8 items.
- **OTR:** Mini whiteboards — every student responds each round.

##### Activity 2: Sentence-Building Worksheet — We Do → You Do (8 min)
Using the Text 1 pattern, students complete a worksheet: given a family member + vehicle icon, write the full sentence (我爸爸/妈妈/哥哥 + 坐/走路/开车 + 上班/上学). Then write 2 original sentences about their own family's commute. Fold in the Q&A (p.87) here: cold call pairs to ask/answer one of the 3 yes/no questions about family commute (e.g. 你爸爸走路上班吗？).

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame 我___(family member)___(坐+vehicle / 走路 / 开车)___(上班/上学)。 with word bank.
- **Extension:** Add 每天 + a time expression recycled from Lesson 10/11 (我爸爸每天七点半开车上班。).
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk — pairs check each other's sentences before teacher reveals model answers.

##### Game: Transport Bingo (5 min)
Printed 9-square bingo grids with transport vocabulary (pinyin or picture). Teacher calls the Chinese word; students cross off the matching square. First "Bingo!" reads their line aloud to confirm.

- **Setup needed:** Printed bingo cards, no devices required.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Teacher reads each criterion; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On whiteboards: "Write one transport word you're confident with, and one you're still learning."
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence about how you get to school, using 坐 or 走路."
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: we learn how to ASK someone how they get to school or work — 怎么."
