# Time (v2) — Lesson 5: Minutes-to (差) + 你的表几点了?

**Year Level:** Year 7
**Unit:** Unit 4 — Time, Daily Routine, Transport (轻松学中文 1)
**Lesson:** Lesson 10 — Time (时间) · v2 sequence (communicative-first)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to say "minutes to" the hour using 差, and to ask the time off a watch.

## Success Criteria
- I can say minutes-to-the-hour with 差Y分X点 (用「差Y分X点」)
- I can understand and answer 你的表几点了? (听懂并回答「你的表几点了?」)
- I can draw clock hands for a time I hear (听时间画指针)

## Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (clocks, 差 sentence frames, blank clock faces for drawing)
- A4 paper + pen, one per student (some clocks pre-drawn as empty circles, or students draw their own)
- Textbook 轻松学中文 1 (pp.71, 74, 76)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** 刻/半 and full minute reading from L3–L4.
**How:** Choral read of 5 projected quarter/half clocks; then a 30-second pair Q&A: 现在几点? using any form.
**Connection:** "So far we count *up* from the hour. Today we count *down* to the next hour — that's 差."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project; read; class echoes 差 and 你的表几点了? Keep visible.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 差 | chà | fall short of; "to" |
| 表 | biǎo | watch |
| 现在 | xiàn zài | now (recycled — reinforce) |

#### Key Sentence Pattern
**差Y分X点** = Y minutes to X o'clock → 差十分七点 (6:50)
- Think-aloud: "6:50 is ten minutes *before* seven. In Chinese we say it the other way round: 差十分七点 — short by ten minutes, [then] seven o'clock."
- Model 3: 差五分三点 (2:55), 差一刻九点 (8:45 — note 刻 works here too), 差两分十二点 (11:58).
- Watch question (textbook p.74 Text 2, Dialogue 2): A: 你的表几点了? B: 差十分七点。

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Say 4:55 with 差." → 差五分五点 (turn and talk).
- Project 8:45 — "差 form?" (差一刻九点).
- Thumbs: does 差十分七点 mean 6:50 or 7:10? (6:50).

#### Text / Dialogue
Drill Dialogue 2 chorally (A/B), then swap roles: 你的表几点了? → 差___分___点。

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — Listening-heavy
#### Activity 1: Pinyin listen-and-write — We Do (6 min) — textbook p.71 Exercise 2 (CD T46)
Warm the ears. Teacher reads syllables; students write the vowel + tone, focus on **ie, üe, er** (e.g. 写, 学, 二 / 儿). Reveal and self-mark.
- **Scaffold:** Tone-mark reference (ˉ ˊ ˇ ˋ) projected; say each twice.
- **Extension:** Write the full pinyin syllable, not just the vowel.
- **OTR:** Everyone writes every item.

#### Activity 2: Listen and draw clock hands — You Do (10 min) — textbook p.76 Exercise 10 (CD T49)
Teacher says 8 times in Chinese — **including several 差 forms** (差十分七点, 差一刻三点) and a couple of 刻/半. Students draw the hands on 8 blank clock circles on their paper.
- **Scaffold:** Repeat each time twice; first one done together on the projector.
- **Extension:** Also write the time in characters beside each clock.
- **CFU:** Reveal answers; students self-check; show fingers for how many 差 items they got right.

#### Game: 差 Dictation Sprint (6 min)
Teacher rapid-fires 6 "minutes-to" times in Chinese; students race to write the *digital* time (e.g. 差五分八点 → 7:55). First correct table reads it back. Tests the trickiest conversion under light pressure.
- **Setup needed:** None — projector to reveal answers.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Volunteer reads; thumbs each.
- **Self-assessment:** 👍 / 😐 / 👎.
- **Exit ticket:** Answer 你的表几点了? for a projected 9:50 clock (差十分十点). Collected.
- **Preview:** "Next: we put it all into the textbook's Text 2 dialogues and write our own."
