# Time (v2) — Lesson 2: Minutes (分) and 零

**Year Level:** Year 7
**Unit:** Unit 4 — Time, Daily Routine, Transport (轻松学中文 1)
**Lesson:** Lesson 10 — Time (时间) · v2 sequence (communicative-first)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to say times with minutes in Chinese.

## Success Criteria
- I can say times in the form X点Y分 (用「X点Y分」说时间)
- I can use 零 before single-digit minutes (单数分钟前加「零」)
- I can write minute-times in Chinese characters (写出带分钟的时间)

## Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (clocks, sentence frames, number sprint prompts)
- A4 paper (folded into 8 boxes) + pen, one per student
- Textbook 轻松学中文 1 (pp.70, 75)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** O'clock from L1 (现在几点? → 现在 X 点) and the 两点 rule.
**How:** Flashcard rapid fire — project 6 o'clock clocks; class chorally answers 现在 X 点。Slip 2:00 in to check 两点. Then pair Q&A for 30 seconds (现在几点?).
**Connection:** "We can say the hour — now we add the minutes, so we can say *exactly* what time it is."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project criteria; read aloud, class echoes the chunks X点Y分 and 零. Keep visible all lesson.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 分 | fēn | minute |
| 五分 | wǔ fēn | five minutes |
| 零 | líng | zero |

#### Key Sentence Pattern
**X点Y分** — 六点二十分 (6:20) · 九点四十五分 (9:45)
- Think-aloud for 6:05: "Five past six is NOT 六点五分. Single-digit minutes need 零 — so 六点零五分."
- Model 3 examples: 七点十分, 三点二十五分, 八点零三分.

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "How do I say 5:05?" → write in box 1 (target 五点零五分).
- Error catch: project 六点五分 — "what's missing?" (零).
- Thumbs up if 分 means "minute."

#### Text / Dialogue
Choral build from **textbook p.70 Text 1** (the precise-minute clocks): 六点零五分 / 七点十分. Class reads each twice.

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — Writing-focused
#### Activity 1: Guided time-writing — We Do (8 min)
Project clocks one at a time; teacher writes the first as a model (X点Y分), then students write each in their paper boxes. Deliberately include 8:04, 10:09, 12:00-then-12:05 to drill 零.
- **Scaffold:** Number-character bank on screen; 零 highlighted in red as a reminder.
- **Extension:** No frame; write 现在 + full sentence; include one time you choose yourself.
- **OTR:** All write every item; hold up boxes 2 and 4 for a 零-check scan.

#### Activity 2: Time dictation strip — You Do (8 min)
Teacher *says* 6 times in Chinese (mix single- and double-digit minutes, e.g. 四点零八分, 十一点三十分). Students write each in characters down their paper. Reveal answers on screen; students self-mark.
- **Scaffold:** Repeat each time twice; allow pinyin.
- **Extension:** Characters only; then convert two of them back to digits in the margin.
- **CFU:** Count of how many got the 零 items right (show fingers 0–6).

#### Game: Multiplication / Number Sprint (6 min) — textbook p.75 Exercise 9
Keeps number fluency sharp for fast minute-reading. Teacher says two numbers in Chinese (七乘九); students write the product *in Chinese characters* and hold up paper. Fast rounds, last 30s as a "lightning" finish.
- **Setup needed:** None — projector optional to display the two numbers.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; ask "did we nail the 零 rule?"
- **Self-assessment:** 👍 / 😐 / 👎 per criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** Translate to Chinese characters: "It's 3:05 now." (现在三点零五分) — collected at door.
- **Preview:** "Next: the shortcuts — 刻 (quarter) and 半 (half)."
