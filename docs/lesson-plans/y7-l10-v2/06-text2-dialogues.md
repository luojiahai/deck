# Time (v2) — Lesson 6: Text 2 Dialogues (写 & 演)

**Year Level:** Year 7
**Unit:** Unit 4 — Time, Daily Routine, Transport (轻松学中文 1)
**Lesson:** Lesson 10 — Time (时间) · v2 sequence (communicative-first)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to write and perform the two Text 2 time dialogues.

## Success Criteria
- I can write a complete 现在几点? / 你的表几点了? dialogue (写出完整时间对话)
- I can fill in missing lines of a time dialogue correctly (补全对话)
- I can read my dialogue aloud with a partner (和同伴朗读对话)

## Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (Text 2, gap-fill dialogue, clock images for Exercise 12)
- A4 paper + pen, one per student
- Textbook 轻松学中文 1 (pp.74, 77)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** 差 form and 你的表几点了? from L5.
**How:** Project 4 "minutes-to" clocks; class chorally answers with 差…. Then quick pair: A asks 你的表几点了?, B answers from a projected clock.
**Connection:** "You can ask and answer every kind of time — today we capture it in writing, in the textbook's Text 2 dialogues, then perform them."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project; read aloud; class echoes the two questions. Keep visible.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### Text / Dialogue — Text 2 (textbook p.74, CD T48)
Two dialogues:
> Dialogue 1 — A: 现在几点? B: 两点三刻。
> Dialogue 2 — A: 你的表几点了? B: 差十分七点。

- Teacher reads each aloud; students follow. Choral read; then A/B split.
- Comprehension: "What time is it in Dialogue 1?" (2:45) "In Dialogue 2?" (6:50).
- Highlight spelling/structure: the question stays fixed, only the time-answer changes.

#### Key Writing Pattern (modelled on the board/projector)
Show how to lay out a written dialogue with A: / B: lines and correct characters:
> A: 现在几点?
> B: ___点___。

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Which question asks about a watch?" (你的表几点了?)
- "Write the B-line for 3:15." (三点一刻 / 三点十五分) in box 1.
- Error catch: project A: 现在几点? B: 差十分。 — "what's missing?" (the hour, e.g. 差十分八点).

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — Writing-focused
#### Activity 1: Gap-fill the dialogues — We Do (8 min)
Project both Text 2 dialogues with words blanked (现在__? / 你的__几点了? / 差__分七点). Students copy and complete in their paper boxes; teacher does the first blank as a model.
- **Scaffold:** Word bank (几点 / 表 / 十 / 差) on screen; pinyin allowed.
- **Extension:** Reproduce both full dialogues from memory, characters only.
- **OTR:** All write; cold-call to read completed lines.

#### Activity 2: Write your own dialogue from a clock — You Do (9 min) — textbook p.77 Exercise 12
Project 4 clock images. Students choose 2 and write a full A/B dialogue for each (A: 现在几点? B: …), using a different time-form per dialogue (one 刻/半, one 差).
- **Scaffold:** Provide the A-line; students write only B; one form allowed throughout.
- **Extension:** Add a third turn (e.g. 谢谢! / 不客气) and use both 现在几点? and 你的表几点了?.
- **CFU:** Partners swap papers, read each other's dialogue aloud, check the time matches the clock.

#### Game: Dialogue Read-Off (6 min)
Pairs perform one of their written dialogues to a neighbouring pair (or front of class for volunteers). Listeners hold up the matching digital time on their fingers / write it. Quick, low-prep performance with a listening check baked in.
- **Setup needed:** None.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Volunteer reads; thumbs each.
- **Self-assessment:** 👍 / 😐 / 👎.
- **Exit ticket:** Write a 2-line dialogue (A: 你的表几点了? B: …) for a projected 11:50 clock (差十分十二点). Collected.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson is all games and free talk — show off everything you can do with time."
