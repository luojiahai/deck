# Time (v2) — Lesson 8: Review + Test 4.1 Prep

**Year Level:** Year 7
**Unit:** Unit 4 — Time, Daily Routine, Transport (轻松学中文 1)
**Lesson:** Lesson 10 — Time (时间) · v2 sequence (communicative-first)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to write all time-forms accurately and to be ready for Test 4.1.

## Success Criteria
- I can write any time in Chinese characters, including 差 forms (写出任何时间含「差」)
- I can choose the correct pinyin in discrimination pairs (选出正确拼音)
- I can self-check my own time-writing for the common errors (自查常见错误)

## Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (review game, Test 4.1 items, pinyin Exercise 13)
- A4 paper + pen, one per student
- Textbook 轻松学中文 1 (p.77); Test 4.1 item list (from reference)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** The four common errors named in the unit: 二点 vs 两点, missing 零, 差 word order, 刻/半 vs 分.
**How:** "Correct the Teacher" rapid round — project 5 wrong times (二点半 / 六点五分 / 十分差七点 / 三点三刻分 / 现在几点。); class calls each fix aloud and one student writes the correction.
**Connection:** "Today we polish every form and rehearse exactly what Test 4.1 asks for."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project; read; class echoes. Keep visible — students will self-rate against these at the end.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### What's on Test 4.1 (walk-through, 5 min)
Project the test shape (from the unit reference):
1. **Vocabulary table** (中文/拼音/英文): 几点, 现在, 点, 一刻, 三刻, 半, 差, 表, 零, 两, 分.
2. **Write times in characters:** 9:30, 12:00, 03:15, 07:45.
3. **Write 差 times:** 差十三分四点, 差一分十二点, 差两分两点, 差一刻零点.
Model ONE of each type aloud (e.g. 03:15 → 三点一刻; and decode 差一刻零点 → 11:45).

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Write 07:45 two ways." (七点三刻 / 七点四十五分).
- "What is 差两分两点 in digits?" (1:58).
- Thumbs: is 二点 ever correct for 2:00? (no — 两点).

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — Mixed
#### Activity 1: Review game — Odd One Out + Bingo (10 min)
**Odd One Out (4 min):** project sets of 4 (e.g. 一刻 / 三刻 / 半 / 表) — class names the odd one and why. **Bingo (6 min):** students draw a 3×3 grid and fill it with any 9 times in characters; teacher calls times (mixing forms incl. 差); students cross off matches. First line / full grid wins.
- **Scaffold:** Provide a word bank of times to fill the grid.
- **Extension:** Grid must include at least two 差 times and two 刻/半 times.
- **OTR:** Whole class responds every call.

#### Activity 2: Test 4.1 written practice — You Do (8 min)
Students complete the real Test 4.1 items on their paper: the four standard times (9:30 / 12:00 / 03:15 / 07:45) and the four 差 items, plus fill the vocab table中文→拼音→英文 for 6 of the 11 words.
- **Scaffold:** Allow the form-reminder strip on screen; pinyin permitted in the vocab table.
- **Extension:** Characters only; write each standard time in *both* forms; cover the reminder strip.
- **CFU:** Reveal answers; students self-mark and tally; teacher notes any 差 / 零 errors to reteach.

#### Pinyin discrimination — textbook p.77 Exercise 13 (5 min)
Project the 9 pinyin pairs (什 shén/sén · 你 nǐ/nín · 妹 méi/mèi · 期 qī/chī · 哥 gē/zhé · 再 zài/zhài · 号 hào/hòu · 半 bàn/bàng · 国 guó/gǒu). Students circle/write the correct one for each; choral check.
- **Scaffold:** Teacher says each correct syllable aloud once before students choose.
- **Extension:** No audio support — choose from sight, then explain the difference for two pairs.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read all three.
- **Self-assessment:** 👍 / 😐 / 👎 per criterion — teacher uses the show of hands to decide what (if anything) to revisit before the test.
- **Exit ticket:** Write 差一刻零点 in digits (11:45) AND write 9:30 in characters (九点半). Collected — a final two-way check.
- **Preview:** "Test 4.1 next — then we move into Lesson 11: your daily routine, using all this time language."
