# Time (v2) — Lesson 7: Fluency, Games + Loose Ends (米 彡 衤)

**Year Level:** Year 7
**Unit:** Unit 4 — Time, Daily Routine, Transport (轻松学中文 1)
**Lesson:** Lesson 10 — Time (时间) · v2 sequence (communicative-first)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to use time language fluently in conversation, and to recognise three more radicals.

## Success Criteria
- I can ask and answer the time quickly without a frame (流利问答时间)
- I can match spoken/written times across forms (匹配不同说法的时间)
- I can recognise the radicals 米, 彡, 衤 (认识部首米/彡/衤)

## Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (mingle prompts, radical display, Exercise 8 leftovers)
- A4 paper + pen (each student writes one "time card" slip; teacher tears or students fold)
- Textbook 轻松学中文 1 (pp.73, 75, 76)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Whole-lesson recall — all time-forms + 你的表几点了?.
**How:** Lightning choral round: project 6 mixed clocks (o'clock, 零, 刻, 半, 差, free); class reads each in under 5 seconds. Then "say it another way" — teacher reads 八点三刻, class responds 差一刻九点.
**Connection:** "Today is your fluency workout — fast talk and games — plus we close out the last radicals and a writing exercise."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project; read; class echoes 米/彡/衤. Keep visible.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Radicals (textbook p.73 Exercise 5 — final three)
| 部首 | Meaning | Example character |
|---|---|---|
| 米 | rice | 粉 |
| 彡 | ornament | 影 |
| 衤 | clothing | 衬 |

Show each with meaning + example; point out the radical inside. (土/又/刀 were covered in L4 — together this completes Exercise 5's six.)

#### Model the fluency tasks
- Demonstrate the mingle question chain: 现在几点? / 你的表几点了? with a student.
- Model "Find Someone Who" prompt: 找一个人,他的睡觉时间是九点半 ("find someone whose bedtime is 9:30").

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "What does 衤 mean?" (clothing) "彡?" (ornament).
- Quick pair: ask your partner the time off an imaginary watch (你的表几点了?).

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — Mixed review
#### Activity 1: Time Card Matching Mingle — Game (9 min) — textbook p.76 Exercise 11
Each student writes ONE time on a paper slip: half the class writes it as **digits** (e.g. 7:45), half as **Chinese characters** (七点三刻). Students mingle, reading their time aloud, to find the person holding the matching form. Re-deal and run twice.
- **Scaffold:** Pair weaker students; allow them to show the slip while saying it.
- **Extension:** Must find the match by *listening only* (no showing the slip).
- **OTR:** Constant speaking — everyone reads their time to many partners.

#### Activity 2: Find Someone Who — Speaking (7 min)
Projected grid of 5 prompts (find someone who gets up at 7:00 / whose watch says quarter past / etc., adapted to time). Students circulate asking 现在几点?-style and routine-lite questions, writing a classmate's name per prompt.
- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame for each prompt on screen.
- **Extension:** Ask a follow-up and report one finding to the class in Chinese.
- **CFU:** Cold-call 3 students to report a name + time they found.

#### Loose-ends writing (5 min) — textbook p.75 Exercise 8 (remaining items) + radicals
Finish any unfinished "add the missing character" items; then write the meaning beside 米/彡/衤 in a paper box. Quick, settles the class before plenary.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Volunteer reads; thumbs each.
- **Self-assessment:** 👍 / 😐 / 👎.
- **Exit ticket:** Write the meaning of 米, 彡, 衤 AND one time of your choice in characters. Collected.
- **Preview:** "Last lesson: review and a Test 4.1 warm-up so you're ready."
