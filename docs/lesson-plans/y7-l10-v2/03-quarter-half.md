# Time (v2) — Lesson 3: Quarters (刻) and Half (半)

**Year Level:** Year 7
**Unit:** Unit 4 — Time, Daily Routine, Transport (轻松学中文 1)
**Lesson:** Lesson 10 — Time (时间) · v2 sequence (communicative-first)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to use 刻 and 半 to say quarter-past, quarter-to and half-past times.

## Success Criteria
- I can say quarter-past with 一刻 and quarter-to-ish with 三刻 (用「一刻/三刻」)
- I can say half-past with 半 (用「半」说半点)
- I can choose between 分-form and 刻/半-form for the same time (同一时间两种说法)

## Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (clocks, sentence frames)
- A4 paper (folded) + pen, one per student
- Textbook 轻松学中文 1 (pp.70, 72, 75)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** X点Y分 and the 零 rule from L2 — via a short listening check.
**How:** Listen-and-tick using **textbook p.72 Exercise 4** style: teacher says 5 times in Chinese; students tick the matching clock from a projected set of A/B options (or write the time in a paper box). Quick reveal.
**Connection:** "We can do any minute now — today, the two shortcuts Chinese speakers actually use: 刻 and 半."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read; class echoes 一刻 / 三刻 / 半. Keep visible.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 刻 | kè | quarter (of an hour) |
| 半 | bàn | half |

#### Key Sentence Pattern
- **X点一刻** = quarter past (X:15) → 九点一刻
- **X点三刻** = three-quarters past (X:45) → 十一点三刻
- **X点半** = half past (X:30) → 两点半
- Think-aloud: "一刻 is 15 minutes, so 三刻 is 45 minutes — three quarters. 半 is exactly half, 30 minutes." Show each clock beside both forms (九点一刻 = 九点十五分).

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Say 4:30 two ways." → 四点半 / 四点三十分 (turn and talk).
- Project 7:45 — "一刻 or 三刻?" (三刻).
- Thumbs: is 两点半 correct for 2:30? (yes — also checks 两).

#### Text / Dialogue
From **textbook p.70 Text 1**: 九点一刻 / 两点半 / 十一点三刻 — choral read twice.

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min) — Mixed
#### Activity 1: Read 8 clocks aloud — We Do (7 min) — textbook p.75 Exercise 7
Project the 8 coloured clock faces (or equivalents). Class reads each chorally using 刻/半 where it fits; teacher cold-calls individuals for 2–3.
- **Scaffold:** Both forms (刻/半 and 分) shown under the first two clocks as models.
- **Extension:** Give the alternative form too ("九点一刻 — 也可以说九点十五分").
- **OTR:** Choral first pass, then cold call.

#### Activity 2: Two-ways conversion write — You Do (8 min)
Project 6 quarter/half times. Students write each **both ways** (刻/半 form and 分 form) in their paper boxes.
- **Scaffold:** Provide the 分-form, students supply only the 刻/半-form.
- **Extension:** Characters only; add one self-chosen time written both ways.
- **CFU:** Hold up box 3 — scan that 三刻 (not 三刻分) is used correctly.

#### Game: Time-conversion relay (6 min)
Two teams. Teacher projects a time; first team to chorally call it correctly in the *named* form (teacher says "用刻!" or "用半!" or "用分!") wins the point. Keeps everyone converting on the fly.
- **Setup needed:** None — projector for the clock + score.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Volunteer reads; thumbs on each.
- **Self-assessment:** 👍 / 😐 / 👎 per criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** Write 6:45 and 8:30 in Chinese characters (六点三刻 / 八点半). Collected.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we read it all together in Text 1 — and meet some new radicals."
