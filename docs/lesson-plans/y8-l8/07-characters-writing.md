# Lesson 8.7: Simple Characters (夕心目方) + Word-Building

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — Hobby (2): Sports 运动 | Lesson 7 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to write four new simple characters and build sport words by combining characters around a shared base.

## Success Criteria
- I can write 夕, 心, 目, 方 with correct stroke order
- I can recognise these 4 characters when I see them inside other words
- I can build a sport word by adding a character to a shared base (e.g. ___球)

## Materials Needed
- Projector (stroke-order animations/slides for 夕心目方)
- Grid paper (米字格 if available) for character writing
- Printed word-building worksheet (Act. 12)
- Printed 11-item hobby survey grid (Act. 11)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Lesson 6's frequency adverbs and 但是 experience sentences
**How:** Correct the Teacher — teacher writes 2 sentences with adverb-placement or 但是 errors, students find and fix on mini whiteboards.
**Connection:** "You've done a lot of speaking and grammar work this unit. Today we slow down and focus on writing — four brand-new characters, and how characters combine to build the sport words you already know."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Show the 4 characters without meanings first and ask "have you seen any of these inside a word before?" to build curiosity before revealing today's focus.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Simple Characters (Act. 9, p. 75)
| 字 | 拼音 | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 夕 | xī | sunset |
| 心 | xīn | heart |
| 目 | mù | eye |
| 方 | fāng | square |

Demonstrate stroke order for each character on the projector, class traces in the air with a finger, then writes each one 3 times on grid paper. These four are simple, standalone characters — like 包止士豆 and 夕心目方 from earlier lessons, they're taught for character literacy and often reappear as building blocks in other words later in the course.

#### Key Pattern — Word-Building
> shared character + new character = new word
> 篮 + 球 = 篮球　　网 + 球 = 网球　　羽毛 + 球 = 羽毛球
> 电 + 视 = 电视　　电 + 影 = 电影

Think-aloud: "球 (ball) is the shared piece in almost every ball sport you know — if I add 篮 (basket) in front, I get basketball. If I add 网 (net), I get tennis." Model with the 球 family, then briefly with the 电 family from Lesson 5.

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Write 心 with correct stroke order — one stroke at a time as I count." (check: whole class writes in the air, then on paper)
- "What does 目 mean? Thumbs up if you remember: eye." (check: eye)
- "Fill the gap: 羽毛___ = badminton." (check: 球)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Character Tracing & Writing Drill — We Do (6 min)
Students trace each of the 4 characters on grid paper following teacher-modelled stroke order, then write each character independently 3 times, saying the pinyin aloud as they write.
- **Scaffold:** Numbered stroke-order diagrams stay projected throughout.
- **Extension:** Students write a 5th repetition from memory with the diagram covered.
- **OTR:** Every student writes every character — visual check as teacher circulates.

#### Activity 2: Word-Building Worksheet — Act. 12 — You Do (6 min)
Students are given base characters (球, 电, 打) and must add the correct character to complete each sport/TV word, writing the full word on the worksheet (e.g. 篮___ → 篮球; ___视 → 电视).
- **Scaffold:** Word bank of possible missing characters provided at the top of the worksheet.
- **Extension:** Students find one more word from this unit that also contains 球 or 电, without it being given as a prompt.
- **OTR:** Students swap worksheets with a neighbour to peer-check answers before the class review.

#### Game: Writing Relay (5 min)
Teams line up at the board. Teacher calls a character (夕/心/目/方); the first student in each team runs up, writes it, and tags the next student, who writes the next called character. Fastest correct team wins.
- **Setup needed:** Board space divided into team columns, markers ready.

#### Activity 3: Hobby Survey Interview — Act. 11 — You Do (6 min)
In pairs, students interview each other across an 11-item hobby grid (covering sports, TV, movies from this unit), marking each item 很喜欢/喜欢/不太喜欢/不喜欢 for their partner, writing the answers directly into the grid.
- **Scaffold:** All 4 response options and today's vocabulary list stay projected as reference.
- **Extension:** Students write one full sentence summarising their partner's overall preferences using 但是 (e.g. 他很喜欢运动，但是不太喜欢看电视).
- **OTR:** Every student completes a full 11-item grid for their partner.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Student volunteer writes one of the 4 characters on the board from memory while the class checks stroke order.
- **Self-assessment:** Mini whiteboards — 👍/😐/👎 against each criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write 心 and one word that contains 球, from memory."
- **Preview:** "Next lesson is our final Sports lesson — we'll pull everything together with a partner dialogue, a listening exercise, and get ready for Test 3.2."
