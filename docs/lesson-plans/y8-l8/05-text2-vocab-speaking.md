# Lesson 8.5: TV, Movies & Weekends + 不太喜欢

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — Hobby (2): Sports 运动 | Lesson 5 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to talk about TV, movies, and weekends, and to use 不太 to say we don't really like something.

## Success Criteria
- I can say 电视, 电影, 节目, 周末 in Chinese (拼音说出电视/电影相关词汇)
- I can use 不太喜欢 to say I don't really like something
- I can use 但是 to contrast two ideas in one sentence

## Materials Needed
- Projector (Text 2 vocab slides + dialogue)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- Role-play cue cards (sport/movie preference prompts, for pair substitution drill)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Lessons 1–4 sports vocabulary and duration questions — 你每天/每个星期打几个小时+sport？
**How:** Pair dialogue — students recreate yesterday's duration Q&A from memory, then swap roles with a different sport.
**Connection:** "So far every sentence has been about liking something. Today you'll learn to say you *don't* really like something — and talk about what you do instead, like watching TV or movies."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Ask: "Who does NOT like sport very much?" — a few honest hands will likely go up. Tell them today gives them the exact words to say that in Chinese.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 太 | tài | too |
| 但 | dàn | but |
| 但是 | dànshì | but; however |
| 视(視) | shì | look; view |
| 电视 | diànshì | TV |
| 影 | yǐng | shadow; film |
| 电影 | diànyǐng | movie |
| 节(節) | jié | festival; measure word |
| 节目 | jiémù | programme; show |
| 差不多 | chàbuduō | almost; nearly |
| 周 | zhōu | week |
| 末 | mò | end |
| 周末 | zhōumò | weekend |

Show each word, model pronunciation twice, class repeats twice (choral). Point out 电 (electric) appears in both 电视 and 电影 — "electric-view" = TV, "electric-shadow" = movie, a nice literal-translation hook.

#### Key Sentence Pattern
> 我不太喜欢 + activity，但是我喜欢 + activity。
> 我差不多每个周末都 + verb。
> 我不太喜欢运动，但是我喜欢游泳。/ 我差不多每个周末都看电影。

Model 3 examples, think-aloud: "不太 softens 喜欢 — it's not a full 'I don't like', it's 'I don't *really* like'." Silly example: 我不太喜欢做作业，但是我很喜欢玩电脑游戏！(pause for reactions — very relatable).

#### Text 2 (p. 74, CD Track 39)
> A: 我不太喜欢运动，但是我喜欢游泳。你喜欢运动吗？
> B: 我很喜欢运动。我还喜欢看电视、看电影。
> A: 你喜欢看什么电视节目？
> B: 我喜欢看体育节目。
> A: 你经常看电影吗？
> B: 我差不多每个周末都看。

Read aloud once, students follow. Read together chorally, then in pairs with roles A/B. Comprehension questions:
1. Does speaker A like sport? What's the one exception? (不太喜欢运动，但是喜欢游泳)
2. What kind of TV programme does B like? (体育节目 — sports programmes)
3. How often does B watch movies? (差不多每个周末都看)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Quick translate: 'I don't really like basketball.' Write it." (check: 我不太喜欢打篮球)
- "Thumbs up if 但是 means 'and'." (check: no — it means 'but')
- "Error correction: 我喜欢不太运动。What's wrong?" (check: word order — should be 我不太喜欢运动)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Choral Vocab Drill — We Do (6 min)
Teacher shows each Text 2 word on the projector in random order; class chorally says pinyin + English. Second pass: teacher says English, students call out the Chinese aloud (no writing).
- **Scaffold:** Pinyin stays visible for the whole activity.
- **Extension:** For 电视 and 电影, students volunteer one more 电-word they can guess the meaning of by pattern (e.g. 电脑 — computer, if they recall it).
- **OTR:** Whole-class choral response, every word, both directions.

#### Activity 2: Pair Role-Play with Substitution — We Do → You Do (7 min)
In pairs, students perform the Text 2 dialogue, then repeat it swapping in their own real preferences (a different sport they don't love, a different thing they like instead, a different TV programme type).
- **Scaffold:** Cue cards with sentence frames and a word bank (体育节目, 电视剧, 卡通片 if recalled; otherwise stick to 体育节目) support students who need it.
- **Extension:** Students extend B's final answer with a duration or frequency detail (我差不多每个周末都看两个小时电影).
- **OTR:** Every student performs both roles (swap A/B halfway); 2–3 pairs perform for the class.

#### Activity 3: 不太喜欢 Chain Drill — You Do (5 min)
Going around the room, each student says one thing they 不太喜欢 and one thing they like instead, using 但是: "我不太喜欢____，但是我喜欢____。" No repeats allowed — students must listen to what's already been said.
- **Scaffold:** Word bank of all sports/hobbies taught so far (Lessons 1–5) stays projected.
- **Extension:** Students who repeat quickly must add a reason or duration on the spot.
- **OTR:** Every student in the room produces one full sentence.

#### Game: Two Truths, One Lie (5 min)
In small groups, each student says three sentences about their own likes/dislikes using 喜欢/不太喜欢 — two true, one false. The group guesses which is the lie by asking follow-up questions in Chinese where possible.
- **Setup needed:** None — purely oral; groups of 3–4.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Student volunteer reads each criterion; class responds with a matching example sentence.
- **Self-assessment:** Show of hands — 👍/😐/👎 against each criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence using 不太喜欢……但是……"
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we'll drill the difference between 差不多, 经常 and 常常, and practise turning 常常 sentences into 差不多每天都 sentences."
