# Lesson 8.6: Frequency Adverbs + 但是 Experience Sentences

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — Hobby (2): Sports 运动 | Lesson 6 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to correctly place frequency adverbs (经常/常常/差不多) before the verb, and use 但是 to contrast experiences.

## Success Criteria
- I can say 经常 and 常常 and place them correctly before the verb, not after
- I can rewrite a 常常 sentence as a 差不多每天都 sentence
- I can make a 但是 sentence about places I have and haven't been (去过……但是没去过……)

## Materials Needed
- Projector (error sentences, Act. 16/15 prompts)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- Printed 11-question interview sheet (Act. 8)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Lesson 5's 不太喜欢……但是…… pattern and Text 2 vocabulary
**How:** Quick quiz on mini whiteboards — teacher gives an English prompt ("I don't really like football, but I like swimming"), students write the full Chinese sentence, hold up.
**Connection:** "You already know 但是 for contrasting likes. Today you'll use it for a new purpose — contrasting places you have and haven't been — and nail down exactly where frequency words like 经常 and 常常 go in a sentence."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Write one sentence with the adverb in the wrong place (我打网球经常。) and ask "does this look right?" before revealing the answer is no — that's exactly the error today's lesson fixes.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 经常 | jīngcháng | often |
| 常常 | chángcháng | often |

Show each word, model pronunciation twice, class repeats twice (choral). Tell students 经常 and 常常 are near-synonyms — both mean "often" and are interchangeable in almost every sentence.

#### Key Sentence Pattern
> Subject + 经常/常常/差不多 + verb + object.
> 我经常运动。/ 我常常看电影。/ 我差不多每天都游泳。
>
> 我去过____，但是没去过____。
> 我去过英国和美国，但是没去过日本。

Model 3 examples for adverb placement, emphasising the adverb sits directly before the verb — never at the end of the sentence. Then model the 去过……但是没去过…… pattern once, reminding students 去过 is the "have been to" form they met earlier — today's focus is joining two experiences with 但是.

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Error check: 我看电影经常。What's wrong?" (check: adverb must come before the verb — 我经常看电影)
- "Quick translate: 'I often play tennis.' Write it." (check: 我经常/常常打网球)
- "Thumbs up if 差不多每天都 means the same thing as 常常, just stronger." (check: yes — 差不多每天都 = almost every day, i.e. very often)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Game: Correct the Teacher — Adverb Placement (5 min)
Teacher shows/says 3 sentences with a deliberate adverb-placement error (我运动经常。/ 我看电影常常。/ 我游泳差不多每天都。). Students find the error and write the corrected sentence on their whiteboards, holding up.
- **Setup needed:** 3 error sentences prepared on slides.

#### Activity 1: Act. 16 Rewrite — 常常 → 差不多每天都 — We Do → You Do (6 min)
Teacher gives 3–4 sentences using 常常; in pairs, students rewrite each as a 差不多每天都 sentence on mini whiteboards (e.g. 他常常打篮球 → 他差不多每天都打篮球).
- **Scaffold:** First example done together as a class (We Do) before pairs continue independently.
- **Extension:** Students write one additional original 常常 sentence of their own, then swap with a partner to rewrite each other's.
- **OTR:** Whole-class whiteboard hold-up after each rewrite.

#### Activity 2: Act. 15 但是 Experience Sentences — You Do (6 min)
Students are given 3 experience checklists (places visited, ✓/✗ format like the textbook example) and write a 去过……但是没去过…… sentence for each on paper or whiteboards.
- **Scaffold:** The worked textbook example (我去过英国和美国，但是没去过日本) stays projected as a model to adapt.
- **Extension:** Students write one checklist and sentence about their own real travel experience.
- **OTR:** Cold call 4–5 students to read one sentence aloud.

#### Activity 3 (Capstone): Cumulative Interview — Act. 8 — You Do (6 min)
In pairs, students work through an 11-question interview covering school and hobbies from across Lessons 1–6 (sports liked, duration, who with, TV/movie preferences, frequency), writing their partner's answers on the printed sheet.
- **Scaffold:** Questions are pre-printed in full Chinese with English glosses underneath for the first few.
- **Extension:** Students ask one bonus follow-up question in Chinese beyond the 11 listed.
- **OTR:** Every student both asks and answers all 11 questions; 2–3 pairs report one interesting answer to the class.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Choral read; class self-checks one 经常/常常 sentence and one 但是 experience sentence against the criteria.
- **Self-assessment:** Thumbs up / sideways / down against each criterion.
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence using 经常 or 常常 correctly placed before the verb."
- **Preview:** "Next lesson we learn four new simple characters and build new words by combining characters."
