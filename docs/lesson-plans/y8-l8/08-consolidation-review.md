# Lesson 8.8: Consolidation + Test 3.2 Prep

**Year Level:** Year 8
**Unit:** Unit 3 — 爱好 (Hobbies)
**Lesson:** Lesson 8 — Hobby (2): Sports 运动 | Lesson 8 of 8
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to combine everything from this unit — sports, TV/movies, frequency, and 但是 — in conversation, listening, and writing, ready for Test 3.2.

## Success Criteria
- I can hold a 6-question hobby conversation using 经常/差不多/但是/不太喜欢/会/不太会
- I can understand spoken descriptions of people's sport habits (Act. 14 listening)
- I can write the key Test 3.2 sentences correctly using this unit's grammar patterns

## Materials Needed
- Projector (Act. 14 listening slides, Test 3.2 recap slides)
- Mini whiteboards + markers
- Audio: CD Track 40 (Act. 14) or teacher-voiced equivalent
- Printed written recap/mini-test sheet

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Rapid recap of all core vocabulary from Lessons 1–7 — sports, TV/movies, frequency adverbs, 但是, characters
**How:** Flashcard rapid fire across the full unit — teacher shows a word from any of the 7 previous lessons, class chorally says pinyin + English at speed.
**Connection:** "This is our last Sports lesson before the test. Today we pull every piece — speaking, listening, and writing — together."

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Display on projector. Tell students this lesson doubles as revision for Test 3.2 — flag that today's success criteria map directly onto what the test will ask them to do.

### I Do (10–20 min)
#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 会 | huì | can; know how to |
| 不太会 | bú tài huì | can't really (do something) |

Show each word, model pronunciation twice, class repeats twice (choral). Explain 会 asks about ability/skill, not preference — different from 喜欢/不太喜欢.

> **Note for the teacher:** Test 3.2's vocabulary list also includes 长跑, 短跑, 走路, 游泳池, 水球, 通常, 大家, and the phrase 打得不太好. These come from the *workbook* dictionary look-up exercises and complement structures, not the core textbook New Words — they aren't covered in this 8-lesson sequence. Worth flagging to students or supplementing directly from the workbook (WB pp. 82–91) before the test, since they fall outside this reference's scope.

#### Key Sentences — Test 3.2 Review
> 我差不多每天都去滑冰。
> 我不太喜欢打篮球。
> Harry常常看电影。
> Tom跟Jerry一起去打乒乓球，但是Tom打得不太好。

Read each aloud, class repeats chorally. For the last sentence, flag 打得不太好 as a new pattern — verb + 得 + description — meaning "doesn't do it very well." Tell students they only need to *recognise* this one for the test, not produce it freely yet.

#### CFU Checkpoints
- "Quick translate: 'I can't really play basketball.' Write it." (check: 我不太会打篮球)
- "Thumbs up if 会 is about liking something." (check: no — it's about ability)
- "Which of the 4 key sentences uses 跟……一起? Point to it." (check: the Tom/Jerry sentence)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Partner Dialogue — Act. 13 (6 Questions) — We Do → You Do (6 min)
In pairs, students ask and answer 6 hobby questions using the unit's reference words (经常/常常, 差不多, 每个周末, 但是, 不太喜欢, 会/不太会), then 2–3 pairs report one interesting answer about their partner to the class.
- **Scaffold:** All 6 reference words and question starters stay projected throughout.
- **Extension:** Students add a follow-up question of their own beyond the 6 given.
- **OTR:** Every student both asks and answers all 6 questions; reporting-back calls on multiple pairs.

#### Activity 2: Listening — Act. 14 (CD Track 40) — You Do (6 min)
Students listen to 6 short items, each with 3 possible answers, and circle the correct choice.
- **Scaffold:** Key vocabulary for all 18 answer options pre-translated at the bottom of the sheet.
- **Extension:** For any 2 items, students also write why the other 2 options are wrong.
- **OTR:** Independent listening task; self-mark as a class immediately after, hands up for any missed.

#### Game: Odd One Out (4 min)
Teacher projects 4 words at a time (3 from one category, 1 from another — e.g. 篮球/网球/乒乓球/电影). Students identify and explain which one doesn't belong and why.
- **Setup needed:** 4–5 rounds of word groups prepared on slides.

#### Activity 3: Written Mini-Test Recap — Test 3.2 Prep — You Do (6 min)
Students complete a short written recap sheet: translate the 4 key Test 3.2 sentences (E→C or C→E), then fill in 6 vocabulary gaps drawn from this unit's core word list.
- **Scaffold:** Word bank provided for the vocabulary-gap section.
- **Extension:** Students write one additional original sentence using 差不多每天都 or 不太喜欢……但是……
- **OTR:** Collected as a formal check — doubles as informal pre-test data for the teacher.

### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read all 3 aloud; class self-checks against the written recap they just completed.
- **Self-assessment:** Traffic-light on mini whiteboards — 👍/😐/👎 against each criterion, plus "how ready do I feel for Test 3.2?"
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence using 会 or 不太会 about a sport."
- **Preview:** "Next up: Test 3.2 — then we move into Lesson 9, Dance 舞蹈."
