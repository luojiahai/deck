# 为什么？因为！— Text 2 Vocabulary & 为什么/因为 Patterns

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 5 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention
We are learning to ask and explain WHY we like or dislike a school subject using 为什么 and 因为.

## Success Criteria
- I can recognise and write the new Text 2 vocabulary: 科目、为什么、意思、有意思、没意思、教 (我能认读并写出今天的生词)
- I can answer a 为什么 question about a school subject using 因为我觉得……很有意思/没意思 (我能用"因为"回答"为什么"问题)
- I can write my own 为什么 question about a school subject (我能写出自己的"为什么"问题)

## Materials Needed
- Projector (vocabulary slides, sentence frames, Act. 8 prompts, game scrambled sentences)
- CD player / audio system (CD Track 27 — Text 2, opening portion only)
- Students: exercise books and pens

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)
**What:** Text 1 vocabulary and patterns from Lesson 4 — subject names (物理, 化学, 生物, 地理, 历史 etc.), opinion structures (觉得, 有用, 难, 容易), the 对……感兴趣 and 觉得 interview patterns, and the 12 flash characters (东南西北木禾石山光土足舌).

**How:**

1. **12-character flash check (2 min):** Display the 12 characters on projector with pinyin hidden. Cold call 3–4 individual students ("这个字怎么读？"), then choral response for all 12.

2. **Pattern cold call (3 min):** Teacher reads 3 subject names aloud one at a time (e.g., 化学——; 历史——; 数学——). After each, a cold-called student responds in a full sentence using 觉得 or 对……感兴趣. Display sentence frames as a safety net:
   > 我觉得 ＿＿ 很 ＿＿。/ 我对 ＿＿ 很感兴趣。

3. **Turn-and-talk (3 min):** "告诉你的同学：你今年学哪些科目？你对哪个科目感兴趣？" 30 seconds each way. Cold call 2 pairs to share one sentence each.

**Connection:** "今天我们继续学 Text 2。我们要学怎么问和回答'为什么'——为什么你喜欢一个科目？" (Today we move to Text 2 — asking and explaining WHY you like a subject.)

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 科目 | kēmù | subject; course |
| 为什么 | wèishénme | why |
| 意 | yì | meaning |
| 思 | sī | think |
| 意思 | yìsi | meaning |
| 有意思 | yǒu yìsi | interesting |
| 没意思 | méi yìsi | boring; not interesting |
| 教 | jiāo | teach |

**Teacher notes on delivery:**
- Present 意 and 思 separately first, then show how they combine to form 意思 (meaning), then how 有 / 没 prefix the compound to form 有意思 / 没意思. This builds the internal logic of the vocabulary.
- 没意思 is NOT in the Text 2 New Words list but appears on Test 3.1 and is the direct negative of 有意思 — teach as a pair from the start.
- For 为什么: drill the three-syllable sequence. Mark the tones clearly on the projector slide.
- Model pronunciation of each word twice; class repeats twice (choral). After 有意思/没意思, use a thumbs-up gesture for 有意思 and thumbs-down for 没意思 to lock in the contrast.

#### Key Sentence Patterns

**Pattern 1 — Superlative 最:**
- 我最喜欢化学。
- 你最喜欢哪个科目？
- *(Silly example to engage students:)* 老师最喜欢数学，因为每天给学生出很多作业！ — pause for reaction; confirm the 因为 structure.

**Pattern 2 — 为什么 question + 因为 answer:**
- 你为什么喜欢化学？—— 因为我觉得化学很有意思。
- 你为什么不喜欢物理？—— 因为我觉得物理很难学，没意思。
- 你最喜欢哪个科目？为什么？—— 我最喜欢历史。因为我对历史很感兴趣。

Model the 为什么……因为…… structure by writing the question on the projector in one colour and the answer in another — students see the two halves as a pair.

**Pattern 3 — Light exposure to 教得也好:**
- Point to the Text 2 line 老师教得也好 and tell students: "这个意思是'老师也教得好'——the teacher also teaches well."
- Explicitly flag: "这个语法 (verb+得+complement) 你们以后会学——今天只要认识这个句子，不需要自己用。" (This grammar you'll learn properly later — for now just recognise it in context.)

#### CFU Checkpoints
- After 为什么/因为 modelling: "我说一句话，你们用手势告诉我：这是'为什么'问句还是'因为'回答？" Teacher alternates between question and answer sentences; students gesture (question: hand cupped to ear; answer: hand to chest). Do 4 rapid examples.
- "用中文怎么说'boring'?" Cold call. Expected: 没意思。
- "给我一个例子：用'有意思'造一个句子。" Turn-and-talk 15 seconds, then cold call 2 students.

#### Text / Dialogue (CD Track 27 — opening portion only)

Display on projector with pinyin below each line:

> — 你喜欢学哪个科目？
> *(Nǐ xǐhuān xué nǎge kēmù?)*
>
> — 我最喜欢化学。
> *(Wǒ zuì xǐhuān huàxué.)*
>
> — 为什么？
> *(Wèishénme?)*
>
> — 因为我觉得化学很有意思，老师教得也好。
> *(Yīnwèi wǒ juéde huàxué hěn yǒu yìsi, lǎoshī jiāo de yě hǎo.)*

**Stop the CD before 你每天功课多吗** — that section (homework/tests/duration) is Lesson 6.

1. Play CD Track 27 opening. Students listen only.
2. Play again; class reads along chorally with the projector display.
3. Comprehension questions — students answer in Chinese (short-form accepted):
   - 他最喜欢哪个科目？(Expected: 化学。)
   - 他为什么喜欢化学？(Expected: 因为他觉得化学很有意思。)
   - 化学老师教得怎么样？(Expected: 教得很好。/ 也教得好。— accept reasonable approximations; this line is light-touch only.)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 生词本 — Vocabulary in Exercise Books (We Do, 7 min — 20–27)

Teacher displays each new word one at a time on projector (large characters, pinyin below). For each of the 6 words (科目, 为什么, 意思, 有意思, 没意思, 教):
1. Teacher pronounces × 2 → class chorals × 2
2. Students write a three-column entry in their exercise books: 汉字 | 拼音 | 英文
3. After writing, teacher points to the character without pinyin and cold calls: "怎么读？"

After all 6 entries are written: teacher hides pinyin on all words and does a rapid quiz — points to each character, class gives choral response. Then one cold call per word for individual check.

- **Scaffold:** Pinyin stays visible on projector while students are writing — only hide for the quiz portion at the end. Students who fall behind may write English only for now and add pinyin later; teacher will not penalise.
- **Extension:** After each entry, students add one sample sentence in Chinese in a fourth column (e.g., 我觉得历史很有意思。/ 我对数学没意思。). Extension students also note the antonym pair 有意思 ↔ 没意思 as a vocabulary note.
- **OTR:** Choral pronunciation after every word (× 6 cycles); cold call rapid quiz at end.

---

#### Activity 2: Act. 9 Adapted — 有意思/没意思 Sentence Writing (We Do → You Do, 6 min — 27–33)

Adapted from Act. 9 p. 66. Original activity: Student A picks a phrase card; Student B makes a sentence with 对……感兴趣. Adaptation for projector-only: teacher displays the phrase on projector; students write their sentences.

Leave sentence frames on projector throughout:
> 我觉得 ＿＿ 很（有意思 / 没意思）。
> 我对 ＿＿ 很感兴趣。 / 我对 ＿＿ 不感兴趣。

Teacher displays one subject word at a time: 数学 → 历史 → 音乐 → 物理.

For each subject, students write TWO sentences in exercise books:
- Sentence 1: using 有意思 or 没意思
- Sentence 2: using 对……感兴趣 or 对……不感兴趣

After each subject: 10-second turn-and-talk to compare sentences with a partner, then cold call 1–2 students to read a sentence aloud. Cycle through 3 subjects (approximately 2 minutes each including writing and share time).

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frames remain on projector for the entire activity. Students who cannot manage both sentences write Sentence 1 only. Pinyin for 有意思/没意思/感兴趣 stays visible.
- **Extension:** Students must write both sentences AND add a 因为 clause: e.g., 我觉得物理没意思，因为物理很难学。They may also choose their own subject rather than the one on the projector, and may use 最喜欢 to combine with the structure.
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk after each subject (× 3 turns); cold call shares after each cycle.

#### Activity 3: Act. 8 Adapted — Written 为什么 Q&A (You Do, 5 min — 33–38)

Adapted from Act. 8 p. 66 (give 为什么 answers to 6 questions; then write 4 为什么 questions for other groups — WRITTEN).

Display sentence frame on projector:
> 为什么你喜欢/不喜欢 ＿＿？
> 因为我觉得 ＿＿ 很 ＿＿。

**Part A (3 min):** Teacher displays 6 subject-based question prompts on projector (per textbook Act. 8, p. 66 — use the textbook page as displayed content). For each, students write a complete 因为 answer sentence in their exercise books independently. Teacher circulates and scans written work for accuracy; note common errors for plenary.

**Part B (2 min):** Students write 2 of their own 为什么 questions in their exercise books (directed at "other groups" as the textbook specifies). They should choose subjects not already covered in Part A.

CFU: After Part A, teacher cold calls 2–3 students to read one written answer aloud. If errors appear (e.g., missing 因为, wrong 有意思 placement), write the error on projector and ask class: "哪里不对？" — class corrects chorally.

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame stays on projector throughout. Accept one-clause answers (因为我觉得化学很有意思 — no need to add 所以 clause). Students may refer back to vocabulary entries in their exercise books.
- **Extension:** Students write 4 questions for Part B (not 2), and for at least 2 of their Part A answers include both conjunctions 因为……所以…… and a 对……感兴趣 clause: 因为我觉得化学很有意思，所以我对化学很感兴趣。

#### Game: 理顺句子 — Sentence Scramble (Projector, Writing) (5 min — 38–43)

Projector-based, writing-oriented group game consolidating 为什么/因为, 有意思/没意思, 最喜欢, and 对……感兴趣 patterns from the lesson.

**Setup:** Divide class into 4 teams. All writing in exercise books (no verbal-only answers).

**How to play:**
1. Teacher displays a scrambled sentence on projector — words are out of order, separated by slashes. New vocabulary items include pinyin.
2. Teams write the correct sentence (unscrambled) in exercise books as quickly as possible.
3. Teacher counts down 30 seconds, then: "笔放下！"
4. Teacher cold calls one student per team to read their written answer aloud.
5. Teams with a correct sentence score 1 point. Teacher reveals the answer on projector.
6. Repeat for all 4 sentences.

**Scrambled sentences (display one at a time):**

| # | Scrambled (display on projector) | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 觉得 / 我 / 很 / 化学 / 有意思 | 我觉得化学很有意思。 |
| 2 | 你 / 历史 / 为什么 / 喜欢 | 你为什么喜欢历史？ |
| 3 | 因为 / 觉得 / 我 / 数学 / 没意思 / 很 | 因为我觉得数学很没意思。 |
| 4 | 我 / 对 / 很 / 音乐 / 感兴趣 | 我对音乐很感兴趣。 |

Winner = team with most points. Optional forfeit for last-place team: each member says one sentence in Chinese using 有意思 or 没意思 before the lesson ends.

- **Setup needed:** Projector only. Prepare the 4 scrambled sentences as slides before class (or write them on the board during the Plenary transition).
- **Scaffold:** Pinyin included on projector for new vocabulary items (有意思, 没意思, 感兴趣). Students may use their exercise book vocabulary entries as reference during the game.
- **Extension:** After each answer is revealed, extension students write one additional related sentence unprompted (e.g., after Sentence 1: 所以我最喜欢化学。; after Sentence 3: 所以我最不喜欢数学。).

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Teacher points to the 3 criteria still displayed on projector. Student volunteer reads each one aloud. Teacher: "我们今天达到了这些目标吗？看看你们的练习本。"

- **Self-assessment:** Students look at their written exercise book work and silently rate each criterion: 👍 达到了 / 😐 差不多 / 👎 还需要练习。Quick show of hands for each criterion ("Criterion 1 — 词汇，谁觉得自己达到了？举手。") — gives teacher informal data on where to re-teach in Lesson 6.

- **Exit ticket (2 min):** Students write ONE 为什么/因为 exchange in their exercise books before packing up:
  > 写一个问题和一个回答，用"为什么"和"因为"。
  > (Write one Q + one A using 为什么 and 因为 about any school subject.)
  >
  > Example: 为什么你喜欢化学？—— 因为我觉得化学很有意思。
  
  Teacher scans books as students write. No collection — no homework.

- **Preview:** "下节课（Lesson 6）我们继续学 Text 2 的后半部分——你们会学'功课、测验、考试、学期'，还有怎么问'你每天要做多长时间作业？'。" (Next lesson we finish Text 2 — homework, quizzes, exams, school terms, and asking how long things take.)
