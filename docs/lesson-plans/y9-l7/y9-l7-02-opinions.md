# 我觉得——Giving Opinions on Subjects

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 2 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

WALT: express opinions about school subjects using 觉得 and difficulty/ease verb compounds (难学, 容易写, etc.).

## Success Criteria

- I can say what I think about a subject using 我觉得……很……。（我能用"觉得"说出对科目的看法。）
- I can use 难学 / 容易学 / 难写 / 容易写 correctly in a sentence. （我能正确运用"难学"、"容易学"、"难写"、"容易写"。）
- I can write at least four opinion sentences about different subjects. （我能写出至少四句关于不同科目的意见句。）

## Materials Needed

- Projector (slides prepared in advance: subject characters + images; sentence frames; compound bank; Correct-the-Teacher error slides; SC display; exit-ticket prompt)
- Student exercise books and pens

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Rapid recall of Lesson 1 content — ten subject names and the measure word 门.

**How:**

1. Projector displays the ten subject characters one by one (英语、汉语、音乐、数学、电脑、物理、化学、生物、地理、历史). Class reads each aloud chorally as it appears. (OTR — whole-class choral response, ~2 min.)
2. Teacher switches to a blank slide and cold-calls five individual students: "这个科目的中文怎么说？" — pointing to a subject image. Students answer in full Chinese.
3. Projector displays: 我今年学___门课。 Teacher asks: "你们今年学几门课？" — three or four students answer in a full sentence, e.g., 我今年学十门课。
4. Teacher cold-calls one student to list three subjects using 门: 我学英语、数学和汉语，一共三门课。

**Connection:** 好！我们已经认识了这些科目的名称。今天我们来给这些科目"打分"——你觉得它们有用吗？难学吗？还是容易学？ (Bridge to expressing opinions.)

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Projector displays the WALT and three SC in both English and Chinese. Teacher reads through each criterion once, then asks: "有没有问题？" Students acknowledge with a thumbs-up gesture. Teacher previews the shape of the lesson in Chinese: "今天我们先学新词，然后用句型练习说话，最后写句子。"

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Projector displays each entry in turn (character + pinyin + English + brief image or colour-coding). Teacher models pronunciation twice; class repeats chorally after each word. Pinyin is shown alongside the character for all five items as they are brand new.

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 觉得 | juéde | feel; think (that) |
| 有用 | yǒuyòng | useful |
| 难 | nán | difficult |
| 容易 | róngyì | easy |
| 写 | xiě | write |

Teacher points out the compound formation rule (English explanation, ~1 min):

> 难 or 容易 placed directly before a verb creates a compound meaning "difficult/easy to [verb]":
> 难学 = difficult to learn · 难写 = difficult to write
> 容易学 = easy to learn · 容易写 = easy to write

#### Key Sentence Pattern

Teacher models all four examples below by reading them aloud and pointing to each word on the projector. After each sentence the class repeats chorally.

| Sentence | Notes |
|---|---|
| 我觉得汉语很有用。 | Subject + 觉得 + topic + 很 + adjective |
| 我觉得数学很难学。 | 难 + verb compound follows the adjective slot |
| 汉字不容易写。 | Negation with 不 before 容易 |
| 我觉得英语不难学。 | 不 before 难 negates the compound |

Teacher draws attention to the position of 觉得 immediately after the subject, and that the opinion clause follows it directly.

#### CFU Checkpoints

- After introducing each new word: teacher asks class to gesture thumbs-up (理解) or flat hand (不太确定). Any flat-hand responses prompt a second modelling pass.
- After completing all four modelled sentences: cold-call two students — "请用'觉得'说一个句子。" Whole class listens and evaluates with thumbs-up/down.

#### Text / Dialogue

Projector displays the relevant lines from Text 1 (p. 60) with pinyin beneath each character line:

> 我觉得汉语很有用。但是汉语很难学，因为汉字不容易写。
> Wǒ juéde Hànyǔ hěn yǒuyòng. Dànshì Hànyǔ hěn nán xué, yīnwèi Hànzì bù róngyì xiě.

Teacher reads the passage aloud once; class repeats chorally. Teacher then cold-calls two students to each read one sentence.

Comprehension questions (teacher asks orally; students respond in Chinese):

1. 他觉得汉语有用吗？(Answer: 有用。/ 他觉得汉语很有用。)
2. 汉语难学吗？(Answer: 难学。/ 汉语很难学。)
3. 为什么汉字不容易学？(Accept: 因为不容易写。)

Note: 因为 is used here only as a light connector to introduce the idea; the full 因为……所以…… grammar structure is taught in Lesson 3.

---

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Subject Spin — We Do (8 min, 20–28)

Projector cycles through the 13 subject pictures from Act. 1 (p. 61) one by one. The sentence frames below remain pinned in a corner of every slide:

> 我觉得___很___。
> ___也不___学。/ ___很___学。

Compound bank strip (also pinned): **难学 · 难写 · 容易学 · 容易写 · 有用**

Teacher models the first subject: 我觉得英语很有用。英语也不难学。 Class repeats. For each subsequent subject, the class generates the sentence together chorally (OTR — this is the first formal OTR moment), then teacher cold-calls two individuals per subject to give an alternative version.

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frames and compound bank remain visible on the projector throughout. Teacher's model sentence for the first subject stays on screen for the first three subjects before fading.
- **Extension:** After the choral response, students who finish early add a contrast sentence using 但是: e.g., 我觉得物理有用，但是物理很难学。Teacher acknowledges these verbally during cold-calls.

**OTR #1:** Whole-class choral response for every subject displayed (~13 rounds).

#### Activity 2: Written Sentence Builder — You Do (10 min, 28–38)

Projector displays six subject characters with small images alongside. Three sentence frames are displayed simultaneously:

> Frame A: 我觉得___很___。
> Frame B: ___（不）容易学。/ ___很难学。
> Frame C (extension): 我觉得___很有用，但是___很难学，因为___不容易写。

Task (teacher explains in Chinese): 现在选你喜欢的五个科目，在本子里写两句话：一句用"觉得"，一句用难学/容易学/难写/容易写。目标：写十句或以上。

Students write in exercise books. Teacher circulates and reads exercise books, providing quiet verbal corrections. If a common error emerges, teacher pauses briefly: "请大家注意——'觉得'的位置在主语后面，例如：我觉得……" and writes a corrected model back on projector.

- **Scaffold:** Frames A and B always on projector; compound bank visible. Students who need extra support may copy and adapt the model sentence from the I Do section, visible in their own notes.
- **Extension:** Frame C — write a connected 2–3 sentence mini-paragraph per subject using 但是 and 因为 (lightly) to link ideas, e.g., 我觉得汉语很有用，但是汉语很难学，因为汉字不容易写。

**OTR #2 (mid-activity, ~min 33):** Teacher calls: "好，停一下。举起本子！" Students hold up exercise books. Teacher scans for common error patterns, then addresses one pattern to the whole class (e.g., incorrect placement of 不), models the correction on projector, and resumes writing time.

#### Game: Correct the Teacher (5 min, 38–43)

Teacher displays four error sentences on the projector, one at a time. Students have 30 seconds per sentence to write the corrected version in their exercise books. After 30 seconds, teacher reveals the correct answer. Students compare. Teacher asks the class to call out whether their version matches — verbal thumbs-up from the class signals agreement.

Errors displayed (slides prepared in advance):

| Error on projector | Correct answer |
|---|---|
| 我很难学觉得数学。 | 我觉得数学很难学。 |
| 汉字容易不写。 | 汉字不容易写。 |
| 英语很我觉得有用。 | 我觉得英语很有用。 |
| 物理写难不容易。 | 物理很难学，也不容易写。 |

- **Setup needed:** Four error slides in the projector slideshow, each with a 30-second pause prompt, followed by a reveal slide.
- **Scaffold:** Compound bank and sentence frames remain on a split-screen or corner strip during the game.
- **Extension:** After writing the correction, students add one original sentence using the same structure. Teacher spot-checks extension sentences during the reveal moment.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Projector displays the three SC again. Teacher cold-calls one student per criterion: e.g., "用'觉得'说一个句子，请。" / "用'难写'说一个句子，请。" / "给我看你本子上最好的四句话。" Class listens; peers give thumbs-up if the SC is met.

- **Self-assessment:** Students draw a small box next to each SC number in their exercise book and tick those they feel confident about. Teacher briefly scans the room.

- **Exit ticket:** Projector displays:
  > 用"觉得"写两句话：一句说有用，一句说难学或容易学。
  
  Students write their two sentences in exercise books (2 min). Teacher circulates to scan — this provides CFU data for Lesson 3 planning. No collection needed; students keep their books.

- **Preview:** 下节课我们会学"对……感兴趣"，还有"因为……所以……"的完整用法，另外我们会读课文一的完整内容。期待下节课！
