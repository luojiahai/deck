# 科目 — Interest + Reasons + Text 1

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 3 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to write sentences using 对……感兴趣 and 因为……所以…… to express interest in subjects and give reasons.

## Success Criteria

- I can write a correct 对……感兴趣 sentence: Subject + 对 + topic + 很 + 感兴趣（我对历史很感兴趣。）
- I can write a complete 因为……所以…… sentence with both clauses in the correct order（因为……，所以……）
- I can read Text 1 (p. 60) and identify how these two structures appear together in context

## Materials Needed

- Projector (vocabulary slides, grammar model slides, Text 1 display, game slides with error sentences)
- CD audio: Track 25 (Text 1), Track 26 (Act. 6)
- Exercise books and pens

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** 复习第二课——用觉得 + 有用/难/容易来表达对科目的看法。(Recap Lesson 2 — opinions about subjects using 觉得 + 有用/难/容易.)

**How:**
1. Project a table of six subjects: 英语、数学、体育、音乐、汉语、历史. Teacher models two sentences: 我觉得数学很难学。我觉得英语很有用。
2. Choral response (2 rounds): teacher points to a subject + gestures to an adjective (难/容易/有用); class responds together: 我觉得______很______。
3. Cold call (x 4): teacher points to a different subject each time; individual students produce a full opinion sentence with no support.
4. Turn-and-talk (30 sec): 告诉你旁边的同学——你觉得哪个科目最难？哪个科目最有用？用中文说。 Teacher cold-calls 2–3 pairs to report back in Chinese.

**Connection:** 你们已经会说觉得……很难/有用。今天我们要学更多——用"因为……所以……"来解释为什么，用"对……感兴趣"来说对什么有兴趣。我们还要读课文一，里面把这些句型放在一起用。

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Display on projector:

**今天的学习目标：**
我们学习用对……感兴趣和因为……所以……来写句子。

**今天你要能做到：**
1. 我能写一个正确的对……感兴趣句子（主语 + 对 + 话题 + 很 + 感兴趣）。
2. 我能写一个完整的因为……所以……句子（两个分句都要写出来）。
3. 我能读懂课文一，找出这两个句型。

Teacher reads each criterion aloud in Chinese; class repeats chorally. Teacher explains Criterion 2 briefly in English: "In written Chinese, always pair 因为 with 所以 — we'll see exactly why when we read the text together."

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Project each row with character + pinyin. Pinyin is shown because all items are brand-new. Students copy the table into their exercise books.

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 对……好 | duì…hǎo | nice/good to (someone) |
| 感 | gǎn | feel; sense (character component) |
| 兴（興） | xīng | mood; interest (character component) |
| 趣 | qù | interest (character component) |
| 兴趣 | xìngqù | interest (noun) |
| 对……感兴趣 | duì…gǎn xìngqù | be interested in |

Teacher notes: 兴趣 is the noun ("interest"); 对……感兴趣 is the full verb phrase ("to be interested in"). Draw attention to the traditional form 興 alongside simplified 兴 — students note both.

#### Key Sentence Patterns

Project and model each pattern; students copy the examples into their exercise books as teacher talks through them.

**Pattern 1：对……感兴趣**

结构：主语 ＋ 对 ＋ 话题 ＋ 很 ＋ 感兴趣

Examples (project one at a time):
> 我对历史很感兴趣。
> 她对音乐很感兴趣。
> 我对数学不太感兴趣。

Project in red — COMMON ERROR:
> ❌ 我**在**历史很感兴趣。
> ✓ 我**对**历史很感兴趣。

Teacher explains in English: "The preposition here is 对, never 在. 对 means 'towards/regarding'. Think: 'towards history, I feel interest.' 在 means 'in/at a place' — history is not a location."

**Pattern 2：因为……所以……**

结构：因为 ＋ 原因，所以 ＋ 结果

Examples (project one at a time):
> 因为老师对我很好，所以我喜欢上历史课。
> 因为我对历史很感兴趣，所以我喜欢上历史课。
> 因为汉字不容易写，所以汉语很难学。

Teacher explains in English: "In writing, always include BOTH 因为 AND 所以. Never start a written sentence with 所以 alone — 所以 without 因为 leaves the reader asking 'because of WHAT?' We will practise catching this mistake in today's game."

#### CFU Checkpoints

**Hold-up-book (CFU 1):** Project the sentence gap: 我______历史很感兴趣。 Students write 对 or 在 in their exercise book, then hold up on the teacher's count: 一、二、三——举起来！ Teacher scans room. Address any errors immediately: 是对，不是在——因为历史不是一个地方……

**Gesture check (CFU 2):** Teacher reads three sentences aloud. Students show thumbs up (✓) or thumbs down (✗):
- 所以我喜欢汉语。 (✗ — 所以 without 因为)
- 我对物理很感兴趣。 (✓)
- 因为我对化学很感兴趣，所以我喜欢学化学。 (✓)

#### Text / Dialogue

**Text 1 (p. 60, CD Track 25)**

Project the full text with pinyin underneath. Play Track 25 once. Students listen with text visible.

> 我今年学十门课：英文、汉语、音乐、数学、电脑、物理、化学、生物、地理和历史。我喜欢上历史课，因为老师对我很好，我对历史很感兴趣。我也喜欢学汉语，因为我觉得汉语很有用。但是汉语很难学，因为汉字不容易写。

> Wǒ jīnnián xué shí mén kè: Yīngwén, Hànyǔ, yīnyuè, shùxué, diànnǎo, wùlǐ, huàxué, shēngwù, dìlǐ hé lìshǐ. Wǒ xǐhuān shàng lìshǐ kè, yīnwèi lǎoshī duì wǒ hěn hǎo, wǒ duì lìshǐ hěn gǎn xìngqù. Wǒ yě xǐhuān xué Hànyǔ, yīnwèi wǒ juéde Hànyǔ hěn yǒuyòng. Dànshì Hànyǔ hěn nán xué, yīnwèi Hànzì bù róngyì xiě.

Choral read (1 round, whole class). Teacher then highlights on projector: the three instances of 因为, the phrase 对我很好, and 我对历史很感兴趣.

**Comprehension questions (oral CFU — teacher asks, students respond verbally; no writing yet):**
1. 他今年学几门课？（举手回答）
2. 他为什么喜欢上历史课？（找两个原因——冷叫一个学生）
3. 汉字为什么不容易写？（用课文里的话——冷叫一个学生）

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Act. 6 — Listen and Write — We Do (8 min)

**What:** 我们听六个学生说他们喜欢什么科目，为什么。Students listen to CD Track 26 (Act. 6, p. 64). Instead of matching, they write a sentence in Chinese for each speaker.

**How:**
1. Project the writing frame:
   > 学生一：因为______，所以他/她喜欢______。
   > 学生二：他/她对______很感兴趣，因为______。
   > 学生三：因为______，所以他/她喜欢______。
   > （以此类推——六个学生）
2. Also project a support word box: 物理、化学、历史、数学、汉语、音乐
3. Play Track 26 once through. Students write.
4. Play Track 26 a second time. Students check and complete.
5. Teacher reveals written answers on projector. Students self-mark in their exercise book.

- **Scaffold:** Frame sentences and subject word box remain on projector throughout both plays.
- **Extension:** For each speaker, write a second sentence using whichever structure was NOT used first. If 因为……所以…… was used for speaker 1, add a 对……感兴趣 sentence about the same person.
- **OTR:** Hold-up-book after self-marking — teacher asks: 谁至少写对了四个句子？举起书来！ Teacher scans for recurring patterns in errors and addresses 1–2 aloud.

---

#### Activity 2: Act. 5 — Written Sentences — You Do (9 min)

**What:** 现在你们自己写句子。Students write their own sentences about subjects and teachers using the frame from Act. 5 (p. 63): 因为我对……感兴趣，……老师对我很好，所以我喜欢……

**How:**
1. Project the sentence frame. Teacher models one sentence aloud: 因为我对历史很感兴趣，历史老师对我很好，所以我喜欢上历史课。
2. Students choose three subjects from their own timetable and write three sentences independently in their exercise books.
3. Teacher circulates, reads over students' shoulders, and gives quiet individual feedback in Chinese only.
4. After 7 minutes: turn-and-talk — 把你写的一个句子读给你旁边的同学听。Check: 你的搭档用了因为和所以吗？用了对……感兴趣吗？

- **Scaffold:** Frame sentence stays on projector throughout: 因为我对______很感兴趣，______老师对我很好，所以我喜欢______。
- **Extension:** Write a contrasting sentence using 但是 + 觉得 + 难/不容易: 但是我觉得______很难学，因为______。 (Recombines Lesson 2 vocab with the new pattern.)
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk peer check x1; teacher cold-calls 2 students to read one sentence aloud to class after turn-and-talk.

---

#### Game: Correct-the-Teacher (6 min)

**What:** 现在来帮老师改错！Projector-based error-correction game. Teacher projects one error sentence at a time. Students write the corrected sentence in their exercise book. After each round, teacher cold-calls one student to read the correction aloud; the rest of the class responds with thumbs up or down before the teacher confirms. Optional scoring: left side vs. right side of class on a verbal honour system.

**Sentences (project one slide at a time; correction revealed on next click):**

| # | Error sentence ❌ | Correction ✓ |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 我在历史很感兴趣。 | 我对历史很感兴趣。 |
| 2 | 我对音乐感兴趣很。 | 我对音乐很感兴趣。 |
| 3 | 所以我喜欢化学。 | 因为我觉得化学很有意思，所以我喜欢化学。 |
| 4 | 因为老师对我很好，我喜欢上历史课。 | 因为老师对我很好，所以我喜欢上历史课。 |
| 5 | 我对数学有很大兴趣。（不是我们学的句型） | 我对数学很感兴趣。 |

After each correction, teacher explains the error in English in no more than 10 seconds (e.g. sentence 1: wrong preposition; sentence 2: 很 must come before 感兴趣; sentence 3: 所以 needs 因为; sentence 4: missing 所以; sentence 5: use 感兴趣, not 有……兴趣).

- **Setup needed:** 5 slides, each showing one error sentence; next click reveals the correction. Error text in red; correction in green or black.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three success criteria again. Teacher asks: 今天你们做到了吗？让我们一起看一下。Teacher briefly reviews each criterion with a show-of-hands or choral confirmation.

- **Self-assessment:** Students write a symbol next to each criterion in their exercise book:
  - ✓ 我做到了
  - ~ 我还不太确定
  - ✗ 我需要再练习

- **Exit ticket (written, 2 min):** Project the prompt:
  > 写一个句子：用对……感兴趣和因为……所以……介绍你喜欢的一个科目和原因。

  Students write one sentence combining both structures. Teacher scans books as students pack up for a quick informal CFU. Target response (display after 90 seconds as a model): 因为我对______很感兴趣，所以我喜欢学______。

- **Preview:** 下节课我们要练习记十二个汉字——比如东、南、西、北……你有三分钟的时间来记住它们，然后我们继续练习课文一，最后做一个搭档采访。做好准备！
