# 功课、测验和考试 — Homework, Tests and Exams

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 6 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to ask and answer questions in Chinese about homework, tests, and how long things take (多长时间).

## Success Criteria

- I can write a duration question and answer using 多长时间 (e.g. 要做多长时间？— 大概要做两个小时。)
- I can use 功课, 测验, 考试, 学期 correctly in written sentences (用本课生词写句子)
- I can write approximate frequency expressions using 每个月……一次 and 每个学期……两三次 (用频率表达写出考试次数)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (display new vocab table, sentence frames, word bank, Act. 11 & 14 questions)
- CD audio — Track 27 (L7 Text 2, second portion only)
- Exercise books and pens

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Key vocab and sentence patterns from Lesson 5 (Sequence 5) — the first portion of Text 2 (p. 65): 科目, 为什么, 有意思, 教; and the 最喜欢 structure.

**How:**

1. Project 5 Chinese sentences on the board (one at a time). Students write the English meaning in their books, then cold-call one student per sentence before revealing the answer. Sentences:
   - 你喜欢学哪个科目？
   - 我最喜欢化学。
   - 为什么？
   - 因为我觉得化学很有意思。
   - 老师教得也好。

2. OTR — Turn-and-talk (30 sec): 用中文告诉你的同伴——你最喜欢哪个科目？为什么？Partners respond in Chinese. Cold-call two pairs to share.

**Connection:** 我们继续学课文二的第二部分。今天我们谈的是功课、测验、考试，还有做功课要多长时间。

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Display the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria on the projector. Read them aloud; students repeat key phrases chorally (重复：多长时间 / 大概要做两个小时 / 每个学期考两三次). Tell students to copy the Success Criteria into their exercise books — these are the targets to measure against at the end of the lesson.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Display the table below on the projector. For each row: teacher says the word twice, class repeats twice. Then show the character only (hide pinyin) and cold-call a student to read it.

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 功 | gōng | achievement; effort |
| 功课 | gōngkè | homework; schoolwork |
| 测（測） | cè | measure; test |
| 验（驗） | yàn | examine; test |
| 测验 | cèyàn | test; quiz |
| 考 | kǎo | sit an exam; examine |
| 考试 | kǎoshì | examination |
| 学期 | xuéqī | semester; term |
| 作业 | zuòyè | homework (also on Test 3.1) |
| 大概 | dàgài | approximately; about |
| 差不多 | chàbuduō | about the same; almost; roughly |

> **Note for teacher:** 功课 and 作业 are near-synonyms. 功课 is the textbook term (Text 2); 作业 appears on Test 3.1 (and in Act. 7 partner interview). Teach both now and contrast them — 都是"homework"的意思，考试都会考。

CFU after vocab: "举手——谁能用中文说'quiz'？" → cold-call. "测验还是考试？哪个更大？" → thumbs up for 考试, thumbs down for 测验.

---

#### Key Sentence Patterns

Project each pattern with two examples. Teacher reads aloud and students copy the frame into their books.

**Pattern 1 — Duration question and answer (多长时间)**

> 你每天功课多吗？要做多长时间？
> 不太多，大概要做两个小时。

Model two more examples:
- 你每天睡觉要多长时间？— 大概要睡八个小时。
- 打电话要多长时间？— 差不多要打半个小时。

Highlight: 大概 and 差不多 both mean "approximately" and are interchangeable here. 大概 is slightly more common in speech.

**Pattern 2 — Approximate frequency**

> 每个月测验一次，每个学期考两三次。

Model:
- 我每个月游泳两次。
- 我们每个学期考三次。

> **Scope note:** 两三次 appears verbatim in the textbook text — students see it in context. Do **not** expand to the full approximate-number grammar (五六个, 一两百) today — that is the focus of the next lesson.

---

#### CFU Checkpoints

- 翻译练习 (cold call): "How do you say 'I do homework for about two hours'?" — expect: 我大概要做两个小时功课。
- "What's wrong with this sentence? 大概我要做功课两个小时。" — elicit: 大概 should come before the verb phrase; 两个小时 comes before 功课: 大概要做两个小时功课。
- Gesture: "If I say 测验, put one finger up. If I say 考试, put two fingers up." Teacher reads a mix of words aloud; scan for accuracy.

---

#### Text / Dialogue

Play CD Track 27 (second portion only, starting from 你每天功课多吗…). Students follow along in their books.

Project the text with pinyin on new words only:

> — 你每天**功课**（gōngkè）多吗？要做**多长时间**（duō cháng shíjiān）？
> — 不太多，**大概**（dàgài）要做两个小时。
> — 你们常常有**测验**（cèyàn）、**考试**（kǎoshì）吗？
> — 每个月测验一次，每个**学期**（xuéqī）考两三次。

Read the dialogue chorally once (teacher leads); then students read in pairs — one reads A lines, one reads B lines (30 sec), then swap.

**Comprehension questions** (students write answers in books):
1. 他/她每天功课多不多？
2. 他/她做功课要多长时间？（用中文写）
3. 他/她每个学期考几次？

Cold-call three students to share answers before moving on.

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: Act. 11 — Written Opinion Interview — We Do (8 min)

Project 5 selected questions from Act. 11 (p. 67) — adapted here for written responses. Students write one complete Chinese sentence answering each question in their exercise books.

Project both the question **and** a sentence frame beneath it:

1. 你最喜欢哪个科目？
   _Frame: 我最喜欢____，因为我觉得____。_
2. 你最不喜欢哪个科目？为什么？
   _Frame: 我最不喜欢____，因为____。_
3. 你每天功课多吗？要做多长时间？
   _Frame: 不太多，大概要做____个小时。/ 差不多____。_
4. 你们每个月有几次测验？
   _Frame: 我们每个月大概有____次测验。_
5. 你每个学期考几次试？
   _Frame: 我们每个学期考____次。_

After 5 minutes of writing, OTR — Turn-and-talk: 把你写的第三句话读给同伴听。Partner says 对 or 不对. Cold-call two students to share their sentence 3 with the class.

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frames remain on projector throughout. Pinyin for this lesson's new vocab also stays visible.
- **Extension:** After writing the 5 answers, students write one additional question of their own (from the remaining Act. 11 questions not projected) and answer it. Push for 因为……所以…… in the answer.
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk after writing; cold-call two students.

---

#### Activity 2: Act. 14 — Duration Questions Writing — You Do (10 min)

Project 5 prompts from Act. 14 (p. 68). For each prompt, students write **both the question and the answer** in their books.

Project the target format and model the first item together:

> **Model (teacher):**
> Prompt: 打电话 (phone call)
> Question: 你每天打电话要多长时间？
> Answer: 大概要打____分钟/小时。

Then project the remaining 4 prompts. Students write questions and answers independently:

1. 游泳（swimming）
2. 做功课（homework）
3. 睡觉（sleep）
4. 上学（getting to school）

Students use their own real information in the answers, or invent realistic details. After 8 minutes, teacher cold-calls one student per item for the question, another student for the answer.

- **Scaffold:** Keep the model on screen throughout. First prompt (打电话) is the shared model — scaffold students can use a second sentence on the same pattern. Allow 差不多 or 大概 interchangeably.
- **Extension:** Students add a second sentence explaining why using 因为……所以……, and can substitute 作业 for 功课 where appropriate. Challenge: write an answer that uses both 大概 and a duration, e.g., 我每天大概要游泳四十五分钟，因为我对游泳很感兴趣。
- **OTR:** Cold-call on questions and answers (5 cold-calls total, one per item).

---

#### Game: Vocab Bingo 宾果 (5 min)

**Setup:** Project a word bank of 12 words on the screen (characters + pinyin):

| 功课 gōngkè | 作业 zuòyè | 测验 cèyàn |
|---|---|---|
| 考试 kǎoshì | 学期 xuéqī | 大概 dàgài |
| 差不多 chàbuduō | 多长时间 duō cháng shíjiān | 小时 xiǎoshí |
| 每个月 měi gè yuè | 每个学期 měi gè xuéqī | 一次 yī cì |

**How to play:**
1. Students draw a 3×3 grid in their exercise books and choose **9** of the 12 words to write in (characters only, no pinyin — word bank stays on projector).
2. Teacher calls out English definitions in random order (e.g., "approximately", "examination", "one semester", "quiz / short test", "how long", "one time"). Students cross off the matching Chinese character in their grid.
3. First student to cross off a full row, column, or diagonal calls 宾果！ and reads back the three characters aloud to verify.
4. Play a second round if time allows (students swap grids with a partner or redraw).

- **Scaffold:** Word bank with both characters and pinyin remains on the projector throughout — students are not expected to have memorised spellings yet.
- **Extension:** After the game, students write 3 sentences in their books using any 3 words they crossed off. Each sentence must use the duration or frequency pattern from today.
- **Setup needed:** Projector only. No printed materials. Students draw their own grid.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three criteria. Read them chorally as a class. Teacher says: 现在看一看——我们做到了吗？

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up 1, 2, or 3 fingers against each criterion (1 = 我还不太会, 2 = 差不多会了, 3 = 我会了！). Teacher scans for patterns — if many students show 1 finger on criterion 3 (approximate frequency writing), note this for the next lesson's warm-up.

- **Exit ticket:** Students write **one sentence** in their books before packing up:

  > 用大概或差不多，写一句话告诉我你每天做多长时间作业。
  > (Use 大概 or 差不多 to write one sentence about how long you spend on homework each day.)

  Example acceptable answers: 我每天大概要做两个小时作业。/ 我每天差不多做一个小时功课。Teacher scans books as students exit or pack up — look for correct word order and use of approximate adverbs.

- **Preview:** 下节课我们要学一个新的语法——用"得"来描述怎么做。比如：老师**教得**好，我**写得**快。我们也会学更多的约数：五六个、一两百。Stay tuned!
