# Year 9 — 科目 (Seq. 4 of 8): Characters, Comprehension & Interview

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 4 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

WALT: recall twelve component characters from memory, deepen our understanding of Text 1, and interview a partner about school life using 对……感兴趣, 觉得, and 因为……所以……

## Success Criteria

- 我能默写十二个汉字：东、南、西、北、木、禾、石、山、光、土、足、舌。(I can write all twelve characters from memory.)
- 我能用中文回答关于课文一的深层理解问题。(I can answer deeper comprehension questions about Text 1 in Chinese.)
- 我能用对……感兴趣、觉得和因为……所以……采访同学，谈学校生活。(I can interview a partner about school life using the target patterns.)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals projected only; no printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices)
- Students' exercise books and pens

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Text 1 (p. 60) choral re-read, then rapid-fire drill of 对……感兴趣 and 因为……所以…… from Lesson 3.

**How:**

*Part A — Text 1 choral re-read (3 min):* Project Text 1 in characters only (no pinyin). Teacher reads the text aloud once at normal pace; class follows silently. Whole-class choral read × 2. Then cold call three students to each read one sentence aloud.

*Part B — Pattern drill (5 min):* Project four sentence starters one at a time:

1. 我对______很感兴趣。
2. ❌ 我不对______感兴趣。— teacher corrects immediately: ✅ 我对______不感兴趣。
3. 因为老师对我很好，所以我喜欢______课。
4. 我觉得______很______，因为……

Turn-and-talk (30 sec): partners give one true answer each for prompts 1 and 3. Cold call four students — one per pattern. Correct any missing 对 in 对……感兴趣 on the spot.

**Connection:** "今天我们要做三件事：先用十二个汉字比赛，再深入理解课文一，然后用这些句型采访同学。"

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the LI and all three SCs. Teacher reads the LI aloud; three volunteers each read one SC in Chinese. Teacher points to each criterion in turn: "到今天结束，这三件事你们都能做到。" Leave SCs visible in a corner of the screen for the rest of the lesson.

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

These twelve characters are the subject of today's memory game (p. 62, Act. 2). They are structural/component characters, not subject-area vocabulary — the goal is recognition and recall for the game, not production in sentences. Display the full table on the projector; teacher reads each character aloud twice, class repeats twice. Students trace each character once in their exercise books as it is read — this is the study window before the game.

| 汉字 | 拼音 | Brief gloss |
|---|---|---|
| 东 | dōng | east |
| 南 | nán | south |
| 西 | xī | west |
| 北 | běi | north |
| 木 | mù | wood; tree |
| 禾 | hé | grain; cereal plant |
| 石 | shí | stone; rock |
| 山 | shān | mountain |
| 光 | guāng | light; brightness |
| 土 | tǔ | earth; soil |
| 足 | zú | foot; sufficient |
| 舌 | shé | tongue |

*Teacher note:* Keep the explanation brief — these are not vocabulary items to be taught in depth. One or two word connections are fine (e.g. "你们在'感兴趣'里看到过'足'吗？'足'在'趣'里。'木'出现在很多字里，比如'学'旁边。") Do NOT teach a separate "Learn the Radicals" segment — Unit 3 has no such teaching section. This is a memory-and-recall activity.

#### Key Sentence Patterns (Recycled — Lessons 1–3)

These are the patterns students will use in today's Text 1 comprehension and the partner interview. Display each with a model example.

| Pattern | Example from Text 1 |
|---|---|
| 对……感兴趣 | 我对历史很感兴趣。|
| 觉得 + adj / clause | 我觉得汉语很有用。/ 我觉得数学很难学。|
| 因为……所以…… | 因为老师对我很好，所以我喜欢历史课。|
| 难 / 容易 + verb | 汉字不容易写。/ 数学很难学。|
| 有用 | 汉语很有用。|

Highlight the most common error from Lesson 3: project side by side — ❌ 我**在**历史很感兴趣 → ✅ 我**对**历史很感兴趣。"对 不能省略！"

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Choral — character recall)** After the tracing step, teacher points to 东, 木, 足, and 舌 on the projector (without pinyin) one at a time. Class calls out the pinyin together. If any are missed, model once more and class repeats.
- **(Cold call — pattern accuracy)** Teacher: "Sam, 用'觉得'说一个关于汉语的句子。" → "Priya, 用'因为……所以……'解释你为什么喜欢某一门课。" Accept only answers that include both 因为 and 所以 — prompt any student who uses only one.
- **(Gesture — error detection)** Project: 我**在**历史很感兴趣。"这个句子有错误。找到了吗？举手。" Cold call one student to correct it.

#### Text / Dialogue

**Text 1 (p. 60, CD Track 25) — displayed on projector with pinyin:**

> 我今年学十门课：英文、汉语、音乐、数学、电脑、物理、化学、生物、地理和历史。我喜欢上历史课，因为老师对我很好，我对历史很感兴趣。我也喜欢学汉语，因为我觉得汉语很有用。但是汉语很难学，因为汉字不容易写。

> Wǒ jīnnián xué shí mén kè: Yīngwén, Hànyǔ, yīnyuè, shùxué, diànnǎo, wùlǐ, huàxué, shēngwù, dìlǐ hé lìshǐ. Wǒ xǐhuān shàng lìshǐ kè, yīnwèi lǎoshī duì wǒ hěn hǎo, wǒ duì lìshǐ hěn gǎn xìngqù. Wǒ yě xǐhuān xué Hànyǔ, yīnwèi wǒ juéde Hànyǔ hěn yǒuyòng. Dànshì Hànyǔ hěn nán xué, yīnwèi Hànzì bù róngyì xiě.

Teacher reads aloud once more (slower, expressive); then removes pinyin and displays characters only. Model the following three comprehension questions with a brief think-aloud before the We Do activity — show students how to locate the answer in the text:

1. 这个同学今年学几门课？他/她学哪些课？
   (Think-aloud: "我在课文里找……'十门课'——对！然后列出了十个科目：英文、汉语……")
2. 他/她为什么喜欢历史课？说出两个原因。
   (两个原因: ① 老师对他/她很好 ② 他/她对历史很感兴趣)
3. 他/她觉得汉语怎么样？为什么汉语难学？
   (觉得很有用；但是汉字不容易写)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 课文一深层提问 — We Do (7 min) | 20–27 min

**What:** Whole-class spoken comprehension of Text 1 using the three questions modelled in I Do. Students answer in full Chinese sentences without looking at pinyin.

**How:**

- Project Text 1 in characters only (no pinyin). The three questions are projected below the text.
- **Question 1 — choral answer:** "大家一起说！这个同学今年学几门课？" → class responds "十门课！" → follow-up cold call: "哪些课？说三个。" Three students each name a different subject.
- **Question 2 — turn-and-talk (1 min):** "他/她为什么喜欢历史课？告诉同桌，用因为……说两个原因。" Cold call two pairs to share.
- **Question 3 — cold call then choral echo:** "Alex, 他/她觉得汉语怎么样？" → student answers → class echoes the answer chorally.
- **Extension question (cold call, fluent students only):** "你同意吗？你觉得汉字不容易写吗？为什么？" — pushes students to personalise with 我觉得 + 因为.

**OTR 1:** The turn-and-talk on Question 2 requires every student to produce at least one 因为 sentence before pairs are cold-called. Teacher circulates during the 1 minute and listens in on pairs.

- **Scaffold:** Text 1 stays on screen in full throughout. All three questions remain visible. Students may point at the text when forming their answer.
- **Extension:** Students must answer every question with the complete 因为……所以…… structure (both conjunctions). For Question 3, they add a personal reaction: 我也觉得…… — applying the text's opinion to themselves.

#### Game: 十二字记忆赛！Character Memory Challenge (9 min) | 27–36 min

**(p. 62, Act. 2 — projector display/hide; team competition)**

**How to play:**

*Phase 1 — Group study (3 min):*
Display all 12 characters on the projector (the same table from I Do: 汉字 + pinyin). Students study silently — they may re-trace characters in their exercise books. Teacher sets a visible 3-minute countdown on the projector or calls "三分钟，尽量记！"

*Phase 2 — Hide and individual recall (1 min):*
Remove all characters (blank slide). Students write as many of the 12 characters as they can remember in their exercise books — characters only, any order, no talking. After 1 minute, call stop but do not reveal answers yet.

*Phase 3 — Team challenge (5 min):*
Divide class into Team Left (left half of room) and Team Right. Project one pinyin at a time (e.g. "dōng") with a 5-second countdown. All students write the character in their exercise books. When time is up, students hold up their books. Teacher scans: every student with a correct character earns 1 point for their team. Teacher then reveals the 汉字 on the projector to confirm. Play through all 12 pinyin clues in sequence. Keep a running team tally on the projector.

**Setup needed:** Three slides: (1) the full 12-character table for Phases 1–2; (2) a blank slide for the hide phase; (3) twelve individual slides each showing one pinyin with a 5-second visual countdown (one per character). Prepare in advance.

- **Scaffold:** In Phase 1, students who need more support may annotate their traced characters from I Do — they are allowed to refer to those exercise book notes during Phase 2. In Phase 3, all students still write and hold up independently; seeing a neighbour's book is acceptable since the goal is recall under time pressure, not a formal test.
- **Extension:** After writing the correct character for team points in Phase 3, fluent students must also whisper the English gloss to their partner before the reveal. If their gloss is accurate, their team earns a bonus point for that round.

---

#### Activity 2: 学校生活采访 — You Do (7 min) | 36–43 min

**(p. 64, Act. 7 — 9-question partner interview)**

**What:** Students interview their partner using the nine school-life questions from Act. 7 (year level, subjects studied, favourite subject, favourite teacher, reasons using 因为……, homework load, frequency of tests, and one free-choice question). Answers are noted in exercise books.

**How:**

- Project the nine question prompts from Act. 7 in Chinese on the projector (no English translations).
- Teacher models the first exchange with a student volunteer: teacher asks "你今年上几年级？" → student answers → teacher writes a brief note on the projector ("上九年级") as a model note-taking format.
- Students work in pairs: Partner A interviews Partner B through questions 1–5 while B answers and A notes; then swap, Partner B interviews Partner A through questions 6–9.
- After 6 minutes, cold call two pairs to share one exchange each — question and their partner's answer — in Chinese in front of the class.

**OTR:** The interview itself requires every student to speak as both interviewer and interviewee. Teacher circulates during the 6 minutes and listens in on at least four pairs, listening specifically for correct use of 对……感兴趣 and 因为……所以……

- **Scaffold:** Project a response bank alongside the nine questions — key phrases students may draw on: 我今年上九年级 / 我学_门课 / 我最喜欢_ / 因为老师对我很好 / 因为我对_感兴趣 / 因为我觉得_很有用 / 功课不多 / 每个月有一次测验. Students choose from this bank if they cannot produce freely.
- **Extension:** Students must answer every question with a minimum two-sentence response: the direct answer plus an elaboration using 因为……所以…… or 觉得. They may not use the response bank.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project all three SCs. Teacher reads each aloud in turn. Students hold up fingers: 3 = 完全可以, 2 = 差不多, 1 = 还需要练习. Teacher scans — note who shows 1 on SC 1 (character recall) or SC 3 (interview patterns) for the start of the next lesson.

- **Self-assessment (quick):** Call on one student to write one of the 12 characters on a piece of paper held up to the projector camera, or simply call out: "谁能告诉我'guāng'是哪个字？写在书上，举起来。" Class confirms chorally.

- **Exit ticket (exercise books, 2 min):** Students write two things:
  1. One sentence reporting a classmate's interview answer: 他/她对___很感兴趣，因为……
  2. One of the 12 memorisation characters written from memory (teacher calls out the pinyin: "写'hé'"). Teacher circulates and scans — gently tap any student whose character is wrong and gesture toward their exercise book notes from the study phase.

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要学课文二的新词——'科目'、'为什么'、'有意思'和'教'。我们还要学怎么用'为什么'来问和回答喜欢某个科目的原因。"
