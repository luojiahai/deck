# Year 9 — Verb + 得 + Complement & Approximate Numbers

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 7 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to describe how well people do things using verb + 得 + complement, and to express approximate quantities using consecutive digits.

## Success Criteria

- I can make a verb + 得 + complement sentence about an activity (e.g. 他滑冰滑得很好, 她跑步跑得很快) — 我能用"动词+得+补语"造句。
- I can translate sentences containing approximate consecutive-number patterns (两三次, 五六个, 一两百) — 我能翻译含相邻数字的句子。
- I can use 经常 and 常常 to describe frequency — 我能用"经常"和"常常"表示频率。

## Materials Needed

- Projector (vocabulary slides, grammar diagrams, sentence frames, game rounds)
- Exercise books and pens (all students)

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Rapid recall of Lesson 6 vocabulary and patterns — 功课, 测验, 考试, 学期 — plus the duration question 多长时间 and the approximation word 大概.

**How:**
1. Project the four characters one at a time: 功课 / 测验 / 考试 / 学期. Choral read each; cold call 2 students for the English meaning. (2 min)
2. Teacher asks the class: 你每天功课要做多长时间？ Students respond chorally: 大概要做______个小时。Cold call 2 students to give a full sentence answer. (2 min)
3. Project the sentence from Text 2 (p. 65): 每个月测验一次，每个学期考**两三次**。Choral read. Teacher asks: "两三次 — how many times is that exactly?" Take 2–3 cold-call responses to surface the idea of "approximately two or three." (2 min)
4. Teacher bridges in Chinese: 今天我们要学两件事：第一，怎么说某人做某件事做得怎么样（动词+得+补语）；第二，怎么用相邻数字表示大约的数量，比如两三次、五六个。这两个语法点都在第七课里。(2 min)

**Connection:** 大概 (approximately) from Lesson 6 connects directly to today's approximate-number grammar. 老师教得也好 from Text 2 (p. 65) is the anchor example for today's verb + 得 structure — students have already heard it in context.

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Display the WALT and three SC statements on projector. Teacher reads each statement aloud in Chinese; students repeat chorally. Teacher clarifies: 今天我们不学新课文——我们专门练习两个语法点，用练习本来写。

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 经常 | jīngcháng | often; regularly |
| 常常 | chángcháng | often |
| 滑冰 | huá bīng | ice-skate |
| 跑步 | pǎo bù | run; jog |
| 钢琴 | gāngqín | piano |
| 弹 | tán | play (piano or stringed instrument) |

Teacher note on 经常 vs 常常: 两个词都是"often"，但是经常含有"规律性"的意思（regularly/routinely），常常比较口语。两个词都要背——经常在测验3.1里考。Display both in parallel sentences: 我经常做功课。/ 我常常做功课。Choral read × 2. (2 min)

#### Key Sentence Pattern

**Grammar 1 — Verb + 得 + complement**

Project the structure diagram on screen and leave visible for the rest of I Do:

```
Subject + Verb (+Object +Verb) + 得 + Complement
```

| Example sentence | Structure note |
|---|---|
| 老师**教**得**好**。| 教 + 得 + 好 (verb has no explicit object here) |
| 他**滑冰滑**得**很好**。| 滑冰 (V+O) → repeat verb 滑 + 得 + 很好 |
| 她**跑步跑**得**很快**。| 跑步 (V+O) → repeat verb 跑 + 得 + 很快 |
| 他**钢琴弹**得**很好**。| 钢琴 (object fronted) + 弹 + 得 + 很好 |

Teacher models all four sentences aloud; students repeat chorally after each one. Grammar tip in English: "When the verb has an object, you have two options — repeat the verb (他滑冰**滑**得很好) or front the object (他**钢琴**弹得很好). Both are correct." (3 min)

**Grammar 2 — Approximate numbers with consecutive digits**

Project the five examples below and leave visible:

| Chinese | English meaning |
|---|---|
| 两三次 | about two or three times |
| 四五个 | about four or five |
| 五六个 | about five or six |
| 一两百 | about one or two hundred |
| 十五六岁 | about fifteen or sixteen years old |

Rule (display in English for clarity): Place two consecutive numbers directly next to each other — no extra word needed. The pair itself signals "approximately."

Choral read all five phrases × 2. (2 min)

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **After Grammar 1:** Teacher gives a topic + subject + complement prompt (e.g. 跑步 / 她 / 很快) — students produce the full sentence chorally: 她跑步跑得很快。Cold call 2 additional students for different combinations. (1 min)
- **After Grammar 2:** Teacher says a phrase in English ("about five or six things") — students give the Chinese (五六个). Then reverse: teacher says 一两百, students give English ("about one or two hundred"). (1 min)

#### Grammar Anchor Sentences

Project all four verb + 得 sentences together and ask students to copy them into their exercise books. These become a written reference for the practice activities. (1 min copying)

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 句子工厂 (Sentence Factory) — We Do (20–31 min)

**What:** Textbook Act.13 p.68 — students build and write verb + 得 + complement sentences in exercise books using the prompts 滑冰, 跑步, and 钢琴. The activity moves from guided to independent production, with a partner-talk moment in the middle.

**How:**
1. Project a three-column prompt table (Activity | Subject | Complement). First row: 滑冰 / 他 / 很好. Teacher models aloud: 他滑冰滑得很好。Students write it in exercise books. (1 min)
2. Project the next two guided prompts one at a time: 跑步 / 她 / 很快 and 钢琴 / 老师 / 也好. Students write each sentence independently. Teacher circulates and reads over shoulders. (3 min)
3. Turn-and-talk (OTR Moment 1): 你经常滑冰吗？你滑冰滑得好吗？Partners ask and answer using the verb + 得 pattern. (2 min)
4. Cold call 3 students to report a partner's answer in a full sentence (e.g. 她不经常滑冰，但是她滑冰滑得很好). (2 min)
5. Students write 2 more sentences independently — free choice of subject and complement from any of the three activities. Teacher circulates. (3 min)

**Scaffold:** Sentence frame stays on projector throughout: _____ [activity] [V] 得 _____。Word bank for complements: 很好 / 很快 / 很慢 / 不太好 / 非常好。

**Extension:** Add a frequency clause before the verb + 得 sentence using 经常 or 不经常 (e.g. 她不经常跑步，但是跑步跑得很快). Stronger students also try adding a reason: 因为他每天练习，所以钢琴弹得很好。

**OTR:** Turn-and-talk (Step 3); cold call reporting (Step 4).

---

#### Activity 2: 大约翻译 (Approximate Translation) — You Do (31–38 min)

**What:** Textbook Act.10 p.67 — students translate 8 sentences containing approximate consecutive-number patterns (including 两三次, 四五个, 五六个, 一两百, 十五六岁) from Chinese to English in exercise books.

**How:**
1. Teacher projects the instruction in Chinese: 请把Act.10 p.67的句子翻译成英文，写在练习本上。(30 seconds)
2. Display the 8 sentences from Act.10 p.67 on screen (project the textbook page or retype). Students translate independently and silently. (5 min)
3. Teacher reveals answers on projector one by one. Students self-check and correct any errors. (1 min)
4. Hold-up-book (OTR Moment 2): 请把练习本举起来。Teacher scans the room to gauge accuracy on 2–3 key sentences before moving on. Quick verbal affirm: 做得很好！(30 seconds)

**Scaffold:** The approximate-number rule box (from I Do) remains visible on projector throughout the translation task as a reference.

**Extension:** After finishing all 8 translations, students write 2 original Chinese sentences using any two approximate-number patterns in a school-life context (e.g. 我们班每周有两三次测验; 我每天大概学五六个汉字).

---

#### Game: 语法大挑战 — Sentence Jumble (38–43 min)

**What:** Projector-based word-order game targeting both grammar points. Teams race to reconstruct scrambled sentences verbally — no writing, no devices.

**Setup:** Teacher has 5 scrambled-sentence slides prepared. Each slide shows colour-coded word tokens in random order. Class divides into 4 teams by rows or table groups. (30 seconds setup)

**How:**
1. Project Slide 1 — scrambled tokens (e.g. 很快 / 她 / 跑步 / 跑 / 得). Teams have 15 seconds to whisper and agree on the correct order.
2. Teacher calls 举手！First team to raise a hand says the full sentence. If correct: 1 point on the tally on the projector. If wrong: next team attempts.
3. Repeat for all 5 rounds. Winning team is applauded by the class. (4.5 min)

| Round | Jumbled tokens | Target sentence |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 很快 / 她 / 跑步 / 跑 / 得 | 她跑步跑得很快。|
| 2 | 教 / 老师 / 得 / 好 / 也 | 老师教得也好。|
| 3 | 很好 / 他 / 钢琴 / 弹 / 得 | 他钢琴弹得很好。|
| 4 | 两三次 / 每个 / 学期 / 考 | 每个学期考两三次。|
| 5 | 五六个 / 月 / 每个 / 测验一次 / 四 | 每个月测验四五次。(teacher adapts to class level) |

**Scaffold:** The structure diagram (Grammar 1) and approximate-number table (Grammar 2) remain visible on a side panel or as a split-screen reference during the game, so lower-ability students can check the pattern.

**Extension:** After a team gives the correct answer, teacher asks: 你能加一个词让这个句子更有意思吗？(Can you add one word to make the sentence more interesting?) e.g. adding 经常 or a complement of degree.

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

**Revisit Success Criteria (1 min):** Project the three SC statements. Students gesture: thumbs up (I can do this confidently) / sideways (mostly) / down (still unsure). Teacher notes any widespread "sideways" or "down" on SC 2 (approximate numbers) for a brief recap at the start of Lesson 8.

**Self-assessment (1 min):** Turn-and-talk: 今天你学到了什么？用中文说一句话。(e.g. 我学了"verb+得+complement"。/ 我学了两三次这种大约数字的表达。)

**Exit ticket (3 min):** In exercise books, students write:
1. One verb + 得 + complement sentence using any topic of their choice.
2. One sentence containing an approximate consecutive-number pattern.

Teacher circulates and reads over shoulders, verbally affirming correct sentences and prompting self-correction for errors. No collecting — students keep their exercise books.

**Preview (2 min):** 下节课（第八课）是这个单元的最后一课！我们会复习所有第三单元的语法，准备测验3.1。你们还要练习自我介绍——用中文介绍你自己的科目和你的看法。记得复习一下今天学的两个语法点，还有经常这个词——测验里会考到！
