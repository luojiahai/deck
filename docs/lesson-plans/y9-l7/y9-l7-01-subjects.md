# Year 9 — 科目 (Seq. 1 of 8): School Subjects + Measure Word 门

**Year Level:** Year 9
**Unit:** Unit 3 — School Life
**Lesson:** Lesson 7 — Subjects of Study 科目 (Sequence 1 of 8)
**Duration:** 50 minutes

## Learning Intention

We are learning to name school subjects in Chinese and count them correctly using the measure word 门.

## Success Criteria

- I can name at least ten school subjects in Chinese (能说出至少十个科目名称，发音正确)
- I can use the measure word 门 correctly to count subjects — not 个 (能正确使用量词"门"而不用"个")
- I can say which subjects I study this year in a complete sentence (能用完整句子说：我今年学__门课：……)

## Materials Needed

- Projector (all visuals displayed and controlled from projector only)
- Students' exercise books and pens
- No printed cards, no mini whiteboards, no student devices required

---

## Lesson Sequence

### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Subject names students already know from prior study (英文, 数学, 音乐, 历史, 电脑, 汉语, 体育) + numbers 1–10 in Chinese (needed to count subjects with 门 later in the lesson).

**How:**

*Part A — Numbers (2 min):* Project the numerals 1–10 alongside their Chinese characters (一 二 三 四 五 六 七 八 九 十). Whole-class choral count forwards, then backwards. Teacher then cold-calls students at random: point to a numeral, student names it in Chinese. Keep pace quick.

*Part B — Subject flash (6 min):* Project subject names students already know one at a time — show the 汉字 only (no pinyin, no English). Sequence: 英文 → 数学 → 音乐 → 历史 → 电脑 → 汉语 → 体育. For each: class chorally names the subject, then a cold-call student gives the English meaning. After all seven: display all seven at once and ask students to count them in Chinese together (一、二、三、四、五、六、七 — 七门课) — this seeds the measure word naturally without formally teaching it yet.

**Connection:** "今天我们学更多科目，一共十五个科目。我们还要学怎么数科目——用量词'门'，不是'个'。"

---

### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)

Project the Learning Intention and all three Success Criteria on screen. Teacher reads the Learning Intention aloud in Chinese; a student volunteer reads each Success Criterion. Leave these on-screen as a persistent reference strip (minimise to a corner or keep on a split view) for the rest of the lesson. Teacher points to the three criteria and says: "到今天结束，这三件事你们应该都能做到。我们一起加油！"

---

### I Do (10–20 min)

#### New Vocabulary

Display each row on the projector: 汉字 → pinyin → English. Teacher says the word twice; class repeats twice. After every three words, do one quick choral sweep of all words so far.

**Consolidation (already known — confirm and anchor):**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 英文 | Yīngwén | English |
| 汉语 | Hànyǔ | Chinese (Mandarin) |
| 音乐 | yīnyuè | music |
| 数学 | shùxué | maths |
| 电脑 | diànnǎo | computing / IT |
| 历史 | lìshǐ | history |

*Display these briefly as a group and say:* "这些你们都认识了。我们快速复习一下。" Choral read once; move on.

**New — Text 1 (p. 61):**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 物 | wù | thing; matter |
| 物理 | wùlǐ | physics |
| 生物 | shēngwù | biology |
| 化 | huà | change; transform |
| 化学 | huàxué | chemistry |
| 地理 | dìlǐ | geography |

*Pronunciation tip for 物 (wù):* lips round, falling tone — model and drill. Point out: 物理 = "things + reason" → the science of how things work. 生物 = "life + things" → the science of living things. 化学 = "change + study" → the science of how substances transform. 地理 = "earth + reason" → the study of the earth.

**Activity 4 extras (p. 63 flashcards):**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 美术 | měishù | fine art |
| 戏剧 | xìjù | drama |
| 法语 | Fǎyǔ | French |
| 日语 | Rìyǔ | Japanese |

**Measure word:**

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 门 | mén | measure word for subjects / courses |

*Teach 门 explicitly:* "量词'门'专门用来数科目、课。" Write on projector: ❌ 一**个**课 → ✅ 一**门**课。Flag this as the most common error: "很多同学喜欢说'一个课'——这是错的。记住：科目用'门'，不用'个'。"

#### Key Sentence Pattern

**Structure:** 我今年学 + [number] + 门课：[subject 1]、[subject 2]、…… 和 [last subject]。

**Modelled examples (project each one, teacher reads aloud, class repeats):**

1. 我今年学十门课：英文、汉语、音乐、数学、电脑、物理、化学、生物、地理和历史。
2. 我今年学七门课：英文、数学、科学、历史、音乐、法语和体育。
3. (Silly example — wait for reaction:) 我今年学一门课：睡觉。

Point out the list structure: commas between subjects, 和 before the last one, full stop at the end.

#### CFU Checkpoints

- **(Gesture)** Project "一门课" and "一个课" side by side. "哪个对？右手指对的那个。" Every student points. Call attention to anyone who pointed wrong; correct immediately.
- **(Cold call)** "Alex，'geography' 用中文怎么说？" → "Priya，'biology' 呢？" → "Sam，'drama'？"
- **(Choral — error correction)** Project the sentence: 我今年学十**个**课：英文、汉语……. "这个句子有错误。哪里错了？大家一起说正确的！" → Class calls out "十**门**课！"

### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

#### Activity 1: 看图说科目 — We Do (7 min) | 20–27 min

**What:** Display the 13 subject pictures from Act. 1 (p. 61) on the projector, one picture at a time. The picture appears with no text. Students name the subject in Chinese.

**How:**
- Teacher projects each picture for 3 seconds: class chorally names the subject.
- After naming, the 汉字 fades in to confirm.
- After all 13: display all at once. Teacher points to pictures at random. Cold call individual students to name them.
- Final round: teacher says the English name, class chorally says the Chinese — no visual support.

**OTR 1 (every student responds):** After the random-pointing round, teacher says: "现在，我说英文，你们说中文——大声说！" Teacher calls out English names in rapid succession (physics, chemistry, geography, drama, fine art, French, Japanese…). All students respond chorally each time.

- **Scaffold:** Keep pinyin visible on screen during the first pass through all 13 pictures; remove pinyin for the second and third passes.
- **Extension:** After chorally naming each subject, fluent speakers must immediately add the 门 pattern: "物理——我今年学物理。" or "化学——我没有化学。" Prompt them silently by mouthing "用'门'" as they answer.

---

#### Activity 2: 星期一课程表 — You Do (7 min) | 27–34 min

**What:** Display the sample 星期一 timetable from Act. 3 (p. 62) on the projector. Students describe the timetable aloud using the sentence pattern, then briefly describe their own.

**How:**
- Teacher models one period: "星期一，第一节课是数学。" Then models a full count: "星期一有五门课：……"
- Turn-and-talk (2 min): Partner A describes periods 1–3 from the projected timetable; Partner B describes periods 4–5 plus the total count. Then swap.
- Cold call 2–3 pairs to share their sentences with the class (30 sec each).
- Individual (1 min): Students write ONE sentence in their exercise book about their own Monday timetable using the 门 pattern. Hold up books briefly so teacher can scan.

**OTR 2 (every student responds):** The turn-and-talk step requires every student to speak. Teacher circulates and listens for correct use of 门.

- **Scaffold:** Keep this sentence frame projected throughout the activity:
  - 星期一，第__节课是__。
  - 星期一我有__门课：__、__、__和__。
- **Extension:** Students must use the full listing pattern with AND a total count, then add one true detail: "星期一有五门课：…… 我觉得星期一很忙！" (they know 我觉得passively at this point — if they don't use it spontaneously, don't force it; it is taught in Lesson 2).

---

#### Game: 找不见的科目！Projector Memory Game (9 min) | 34–43 min

**Based on:** Act. 4 (p. 63)

**How to play:**

*Setup (projector only — no cards, no printing):*

1. **Display phase (1 min):** Project all 15 subject names as a grid (汉字 only, no pinyin):
   英文 ｜ 汉语 ｜ 音乐 ｜ 数学 ｜ 电脑
   物理 ｜ 化学 ｜ 生物 ｜ 地理 ｜ 历史
   美术 ｜ 戏剧 ｜ 法语 ｜ 日语 ｜ 体育

2. **Write phase (1 min):** Screen goes blank (projector covered / slide hidden). Students write as many subjects as they can remember in exercise books — in Chinese characters.

3. **Remove phase (multiple rounds, ~7 min):** Bring back the grid with ONE subject hidden (blank box in its position). Class looks at the remaining 14. Teacher says: "少了哪个？" Teams (left half of class vs right half) race to call out the missing subject in Chinese — first team to say it correctly earns a point. Teacher reveals the hidden word to confirm. Play 5–6 rounds, hiding a different subject each time. Prioritise hiding the four new subjects (物理, 化学, 生物, 地理) and the Act. 4 extras (美术, 戏剧, 法语, 日语) in the later rounds for spaced retrieval.

**What language it practises:** Rapid retrieval of all 15 subject names; character recognition; motivated repetition.

**Setup needed:** Projector only. Prepare a slide in advance with the 15-word grid; duplicate the slide 6 times, each version with one different word blanked out.

- **Scaffold:** In rounds 1–2, leave pinyin visible in the blank box (only the 汉字 is hidden) — students see the pinyin hint but must produce the character name.
- **Extension:** When their team wins a round, fluent speakers must use the missing subject in a full sentence before the point is awarded: "少了物理！我今年学物理。" or "少了美术！我不学美术。"

---

### Plenary (43–50 min)

- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Project the three Success Criteria again. Teacher reads the first criterion; a student volunteer reads the second and third. Teacher asks: "今天我们做到了吗？"

- **Self-assessment:** Students hold up fingers — 3 fingers = 完全可以 (I've got it), 2 fingers = 差不多 (getting there), 1 finger = 还不行 (need more practice) — for each criterion in turn. Teacher scans quickly; note any students showing 1 finger on criterion 2 (门 vs 个) for the next lesson's review.

- **Exit ticket (in exercise books, 2 min):** Students write ONE complete sentence in their exercise book:
  > 我今年学__门课：……和……。
  They must include the correct count and at least five subjects from today's lesson. Teacher circulates and reads over shoulders; tap any student who has used 个 instead of 门 and gesture them to self-correct.

- **Preview:** "下一课，我们要学怎么说我们对这些科目的看法——比如觉得汉语很难，或者觉得历史很有用。我们还要学'因为……所以……'。"
