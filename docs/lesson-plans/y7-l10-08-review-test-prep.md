## Lesson 8: Review & Test Preparation — "Beat the Test"

**Option: C — Game show format**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to confidently demonstrate our time-telling skills for the Unit 4.1 test.

### Success Criteria
- I can translate any time between English and Chinese (中英时间互译)
- I can identify and correct common time-telling mistakes (发现并纠正常见错误)
- I feel ready for the Unit 4 vocabulary test (准备好单元测试)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV
- A4 paper
- Game show scoreboard on whiteboard (4 team columns)
- 3 rounds of game show questions pre-loaded on projector
- Buzzer system: 4 different sounds (or students raise coloured paper — red, blue, green, yellow per team)
- Practice test paper (matching real Test 4.1 format) — one per student, for final segment

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–6 min) — The Warm-Up Round

**What:** Everything. Low-stakes opener in game show format.

**How:** Already in teams of 4–5 (assigned at start). "Warm-up round — these don't count for points, just to warm up your brains."

5 quick questions on projector. Teams huddle and whisper answer. Teacher counts down "3, 2, 1 — show!" All teams hold up paper with answer. Correct teams get a cheer.

1. "How do you say 2:00?" (两点)
2. "差十分七点 — what time is this?" (6:50)
3. "What's wrong: 三点五分" (missing 零)
4. "9:45 — best expression?" (九点三刻)
5. "Translate: What time is it now?" (现在几点？)

**Connection:** "That was the warm-up. Now the real game begins. Three rounds. Your team's score determines whether you walk out of here test-ready."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (6–8 min)
Display. "Game show rules: work as a team, but everyone must write their own answer on their own paper — I'll check. Teams earn points. But more importantly: every question is the kind of thing that WILL be on your test."

#### Round 1: Vocabulary Lightning (12 min)

**Category:** Time vocabulary (matching Test 4.1 format: 中文 → 拼音 → 英文).

15 questions. Each question shown on projector for 30 seconds. Teams write answer on paper. After each, reveal answer. Self-mark. Team with most correct in each question gets 10 points.

Questions (mix of formats matching the test):

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 点 → 拼音? | diǎn |
| 2 | 点 → English? | o'clock |
| 3 | fēn → 汉字? | 分 |
| 4 | 半 → 拼音 + English? | bàn / half |
| 5 | quarter → 汉字 + 拼音? | 刻 / kè |
| 6 | 现在 → 拼音 + English? | xiànzài / now |
| 7 | chà → 汉字? | 差 |
| 8 | 两 → English? | two (used before 点) |
| 9 | zero → 汉字 + 拼音? | 零 / líng |
| 10 | watch → 汉字 + 拼音? | 表 / biǎo |
| 11 | 几 → 拼音 + English? | jǐ / how many; what |
| 12 | minute → 汉字 + 拼音? | 分 / fēn |
| 13 | 三刻 → what does this mean for time? | :45 / three quarters past |
| 14 | 一刻 → what time is this? (:00, :15, :30, :45?) | :15 |
| 15 | 差 → what does this mean in time expressions? | "to" / minutes short of the next hour |

After all 15: teams tally their correct count. Max round score: 150 (10 per correct answer).

Teacher notes most-missed items for targeted review after the round.

#### Round 2: Translation Showdown (10 min)

**Category:** Translate times between English and Chinese (matching test format).

12 questions. Each on projector for 45 seconds. Teams write answer. Self-mark. 15 points each. Max: 180.

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Write 3:00 in Chinese characters | 三点 |
| 2 | Write 2:30 in Chinese characters | 两点半 |
| 3 | 差十分七点 → digits? | 6:50 |
| 4 | Write 9:15 in Chinese (best expression) | 九点一刻 |
| 5 | Write 6:45 in Chinese (best expression) | 六点三刻 |
| 6 | 五点零五分 → digits? | 5:05 |
| 7 | Write 7:55 in Chinese using 差 | 差五分八点 |
| 8 | 十二点半 → digits? | 12:30 |
| 9 | Write 3:40 in Chinese using 差 | 差二十分四点 |
| 10 | 一点一刻 → digits? | 1:15 |
| 11 | Write 11:50 in Chinese (best expression) | 差十分十二点 |
| 12 | 差五分两点 → digits? | 1:55 |

After each: teacher reveals correct answer. Common errors addressed immediately:
- Q2, Q7, Q12: emphasis on 两 vs 二
- Q6: emphasis on 零
- Q5: emphasis on 三刻 meaning :45

#### Round 3: Error Bounty (8 min)

**Category:** Find and fix errors (hardest test questions).

8 items. Each on projector. Teams must: (a) identify if wrong, (b) write the correction. 20 points per fully correct answer (must identify AND correct). Max: 160.

| # | Item | Wrong? | Correction |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 二点十分 | ✓ wrong | 两点十分 |
| 2 | 四点三十分 | ✓ correct (same as 四点半) | — |
| 3 | 三点五分 | ✓ wrong (missing 零) | 三点零五分 |
| 4 | 七差十分点 | ✓ wrong (word order) | 差十分七点 |
| 5 | 六点十五分 | ✓ correct (same as 六点一刻) | — |
| 6 | 差十分二点 | ✓ wrong (二→两) | 差十分两点 |
| 7 | 九点三刻 | ✓ correct (9:45) | — |
| 8 | 十二点六十 | ✓ wrong (no such time) | 一点 / 十三点 |

After each: team discussion. "Why is this wrong? How do you know?" Teacher clarifies #4 (word order), #8 (conceptual — 60 minutes rolls over).

#### Final Segment: 3-Question Test Sprint (4 min)

Now switch to individual mode. "You've been working as teams. Now show what YOU can do. These 3 questions are exactly the format of your real test."

Distribute practice paper with 3 questions:
1. Write 7:45 in Chinese characters (best expression): _____________
2. 差十分八点 → write the digits: _____________
3. Translate: What time is it now? → Chinese: _____________

2 minutes. Silent. No team help. Teacher collects. This is your diagnostic — mark these quickly to see who needs last-minute support before the real test.

#### Game Show Finale + Plenary (4 min)

**Team scores announced:**
1st place: 🥇 "Test-Ready Champions"
2nd place: 🥈 "Almost There"
3rd place: 🥉 "Solid Foundation"
4th place: "Keep Practising" (encouragement, not shame)

**Revisit Success Criteria:** "Raise your hand if you feel ready for the test. Raise your hand if you know exactly what you need to review tonight."

**Self-assessment:** "On your paper, write: My strongest area: _____ / I still need to review: _____" Hand in with the 3-question sprint.

**Preview:** "Test next lesson. After that we start Lesson 11 — Daily Routine. You'll use ALL your time knowledge to talk about your own life in Chinese."

## Teacher Notes: Lesson 10 — 8-Lesson Overview

```
Lesson 7: Mixed ───────► Time Olympics (Option B)
Lesson 8: Review ──────► Beat the Test game show (Option C)
```

### Key Diagnostics (reinforce in every lesson)
1. **两点 not 二点** — most common error, appears in every lesson
2. **零 insertion** — single-digit minutes after 点 always need 零
3. **差 word order** — 差 FIRST, minutes + 分, then hour + 点
4. **三刻 = :45** — three quarters past the hour, not quarter-to
5. **Pattern choice** — choose the most natural expression, not just the first one you learned
6. **6 Radicals** — 土, 寸, 刂, 米, 彡, 衤 all taught across Lessons 2/4/5. Reference them when characters containing them appear.
