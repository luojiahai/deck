## Lesson 6: Listening (a.m./p.m.) + Sports Vocabulary Integration

**Option: C — Game-dominated**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to listen for a.m./p.m. context and combine our daily-routine sentences with activities we already know.

### Success Criteria
- I can listen and correctly tick whether a time is a.m. or p.m. (听音判断上午还是下午/晚上)
- I can make a sentence combining a time + an activity from earlier units (用时间+活动造句, e.g. 我下午四点打篮球)
- I can spot an error in a combined routine-and-activity sentence (找出句子里的错误)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (with audio for CD T53)
- Listen-and-tick worksheet (Ex8 format, 6 items: a.m. vs p.m. columns) — one per student
- Sentence Jumble word-card envelopes (4 identical sets: routine + sports vocab mixed) — one per team
- A4 paper

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Statement/question sentence patterns from Lesson 5, and a quick recall of sports/activity vocabulary from Units 1–3.

**How:** Rapid pair quiz — Student A asks a routine question (你几点起床？ etc.), Student B answers, then swap. After 90 seconds, cold call 3 pairs to perform one exchange each. Then flash 5 sports words in English (swim / play basketball / play table tennis / do homework / read books) — students say the Chinese chorally as each appears.

**Connection:** "You already know how to talk about sports and hobbies from earlier units. Today we glue that vocabulary onto the time expressions you've been building all week."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read aloud. Students echo each criterion once.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Listening Focus (Ex8, p.83, CD T53)
Explain the task: 6 items, each gives a time — students must tick whether it's a.m. or p.m. based on context clues (activity mentioned, or explicit 上午/下午/晚上). Play the track once through fully first so students hear the natural pace.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "我下午四点半___。 Fill in a sports activity." Write on paper, hold up.
- "Is 我晚上七点游泳 a likely sentence, or should we picture-check it?" — discuss briefly (unusual but not wrong; pools can be indoor).
- Teacher says a sentence with time + activity; students give thumbs up if it makes logical sense, thumbs down if the time/activity combo is odd (not necessarily grammatically wrong).

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Ex8 Listen-and-Tick (CD T53) (8 min)
Students complete the listen-and-tick worksheet: 6 audio items, tick a.m. or p.m. for each. Play the track pausing after each item on the first pass; play once more straight through for self-check.

- **Scaffold:** Students who need more support get a written transcript with the time-of-day word blanked out, to focus listening on just that word.
- **Extension:** Fluent students also write the full sentence they heard, not just tick a box.
- **OTR:** Thumbs up/down check after each item before moving to the next.

##### Activity 2: Sentence Jumble — Routine + Sports (8 min)
In teams of 4, students receive an envelope of word cards (time-of-day word, clock time, subject pronoun, and a mix of routine/sports verbs). Teams race to arrange the cards into 3 different valid sentences, writing each down as they build it.

Example card set: 我 / 下午 / 四点 / 打篮球 / 早上 / 七点 / 起床 / 晚上 / 九点半 / 做作业

- **Scaffold:** Teams struggling may ask for the sentence-frame reminder card (subject + time-of-day + time + verb).
- **Extension:** Fast teams build a 4th sentence using 我们 instead of 我, adjusting if needed.
- **OTR:** Every team member must read one completed sentence aloud to the class.

##### Game: Two Truths, One Lie — Daily Routine + Sports (7 min)
Each student writes 3 sentences about their day (mixing routine and sports vocabulary) — 2 true, 1 false — on paper. In groups of 4, students take turns reading their 3 sentences aloud; the group guesses which is the lie by pointing/voting.

Example: 我早上六点起床。我下午打网球。我晚上十二点睡觉。(spot which doesn't match reality)

- **Setup needed:** None — paper and pen only. Give 3 minutes writing time before groups start sharing.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read aloud; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On paper: "Write one sentence combining a time and a sport/activity that you're proud of."
- **Exit ticket:** "Write: 'I play badminton at 5:00 in the afternoon.'" Collect on the way out. (我下午五点打羽毛球。)
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: you interview a partner about their whole daily routine and write up a report."
