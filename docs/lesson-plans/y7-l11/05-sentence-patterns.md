## Lesson 5: Sentence Patterns — Statements & Questions

**Option: A/B — Mixed/balanced**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to turn a daily-routine statement into a question, and answer it about ourselves or someone else.

### Success Criteria
- I can say a daily-routine statement using 我/我们 + time + verb (用我/我们说日常起居句子)
- I can ask a daily-routine question using 几点 (用几点提问)
- I can describe someone else's routine from a picture using 他/她 (用他/她描述日常起居)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV
- Ex7 picture set (7 daily routine scenes with time shown in corner, p.83)
- Sentence-frame handout (statements + questions side by side)
- A4 paper
- Pre-written "error" sentences for the error-correction game (projected)

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** The 6 routine verbs and Text 2's six sentences from Lesson 4.

**How:** Teacher says a routine verb in English (e.g. "get up"); students write the Chinese on paper, hold up. 6 items: get up / eat breakfast / go to school / attend class / classes are over / go to bed. Then cold call 2 students to recite Text 2's full sentence set from memory (choral fallback if needed).

**Connection:** "You can describe YOUR day. Today you learn to ask someone else about THEIRS — and describe a stranger's day just from a picture."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read aloud. Students repeat each criterion once.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Key Sentence Patterns

**Statements:**
| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| 我早上X点起床。 | I get up at X in the morning. |
| 我X点一刻去上学。 | I go to school at quarter past X. |
| 我们X点上课。 | We attend class at X. |
| 我们下午X点半放学。 | Classes finish at half past X in the afternoon. |
| 我晚上X点半睡觉。 | I go to bed at half past X in the evening. |

**Questions:**
| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| 你早上几点起床？ | What time do you get up in the morning? |
| 你几点吃早饭？ | What time do you eat breakfast? |
| 你们下午几点放学？ | What time do you (pl.) finish school in the afternoon? |
| 你几点睡觉？ | What time do you go to bed? |

Think-aloud: "To turn a statement into a question, I replace the exact time with 几点, and move 你 into the 我 spot. 我早上六点半起床 → 你早上几点起床？"

Model 2–3 Q&A pairs live with the class: teacher asks, answers with own (real or invented) routine.

Introduce 他/她 for describing someone else: "他早上七点起床。" — point out only the pronoun changes; the rest of the sentence stays the same.

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "Turn this into a question: 我们下午三点半放学。" Write on paper, hold up. (你们下午几点放学？)
- "What's wrong with this question: 你几点起床几点？" — cold call (几点 used twice; should only appear once)
- Teacher shows a picture with 她 + a time — "Make a sentence." Write and hold up.

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Guided Sentence-Frame Practice — We Do (8 min)
Using the sentence-frame handout, students convert 4 given statements into questions and vice versa, checking each with a partner before the teacher reveals the answer.

- **Scaffold:** Frame boxes on the handout show exactly where 几点 and the pronoun go.
- **Extension:** Fluent students also answer their own converted question with a different time than the original.
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk on every item before the reveal — every student commits an answer first.

##### Activity 2: Ex7 Picture Description (p.83) — You Do, Oral (8 min)
Project the 7 daily-routine scenes (time shown in corner of each). In pairs, students take turns describing each scene aloud using 他/她 + time + verb, e.g. 他早上七点起床。 Partner listens and gives a thumbs up/down on correctness.

- **Scaffold:** Sentence frame stays projected: 他/她 + [time-of-day] + [time] + [verb]。
- **Extension:** Students add a follow-up question to their partner based on the picture, e.g. 你呢？你几点起床？, and the partner must answer about themselves.
- **OTR:** Cold call 4 pairs to perform one scene description each to the whole class.

##### Game: Correct the Teacher (7 min)
Teacher projects 6 sentences with deliberate errors (wrong pronoun, 几点 in the wrong place, missing verb, wrong time-of-day word). Students work in pairs to spot and fix each one on paper, then a representative from each pair reads out the correction.

Example errors:
1. 你们几点下午放学？(word order — should be 你们下午几点放学？)
2. 我早上六点半起了床。(should be 起床, not 起了床)
3. 他晚上上午睡觉。(two time-of-day words — remove 上午)
4. 你几点几点吃早饭？(几点 duplicated)
5. 我们下午三点半上学。(should be 放学, not 上学 — classes ending, not starting)
6. 她早上七点半上课上学。(two verbs combined incorrectly — should be one or the other)

- **Setup needed:** None beyond the projected sentence list; pairs just need paper and pen.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read aloud; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On paper: "Write one question you can now ask a classmate about their routine."
- **Exit ticket:** "Write a question asking what time someone goes to bed, and answer it for yourself." Collect on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: we listen for a.m. vs p.m. and combine your routine sentences with sports you already know how to say."
