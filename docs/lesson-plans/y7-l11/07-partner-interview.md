## Lesson 7: Partner Interview + Listening

**Option: B — Writing-focused**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to interview a classmate about their daily routine and write up what we find out.

### Success Criteria
- I can ask all 8 interview questions about someone's daily routine (问八个关于日常起居的问题)
- I can write a short report about my partner's routine in Chinese (写一份关于同伴日常起居的报告)
- I can listen and correctly tick the time and activity I hear (听音判断时间和活动)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV (with audio for CD T54)
- Ex9 interview question sheet (8 questions) — one per student
- Report-writing template (sentence frames for the write-up) — one per student
- Ex10 listen-and-tick worksheet (8 items: time + activity columns) — one per student
- A4 paper

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Combined time + activity sentences from Lesson 6.

**How:** Teacher says an English sentence (e.g. "I do homework at 8pm"); students write the Chinese on paper, hold up. 4 items mixing routine verbs and sports/activities. Cold call 2 students per item.

**Connection:** "You can describe your own day and combine it with activities. Today you find out about someone else's day by asking them directly — a real interview — and write it up."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read aloud. Students echo each criterion once.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Extra Vocabulary Needed for the Interview
| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 午饭 | wǔ fàn | lunch |
| 晚饭 | wǎn fàn | dinner |

Point out these follow the same pattern as 早饭 (早+饭, 午+饭, 晚+饭) — students should be able to guess the meaning from the pattern.

##### The 8 Interview Questions (Ex9, p.84)
| # | Question | Covers |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 你早上几点起床？ | 起床 |
| 2 | 你几点吃早饭？ | 早饭 |
| 3 | 你几点去上学？ | 上学 |
| 4 | 你们几点上课？ | 上课 |
| 5 | 你几点吃午饭？ | 午饭 |
| 6 | 你们下午几点放学？ | 放学 |
| 7 | 你几点吃晚饭？ | 晚饭 |
| 8 | 你晚上几点睡觉？ | 睡觉 |

Model the full process with a student volunteer: teacher asks all 8 questions, writes the volunteer's answers on the board in note form (keywords only, not full sentences), then shows how those notes become one written report sentence: "他/她早上七点起床，七点半吃早饭……"

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "Which question asks about lunch?" Cold call. (你几点吃午饭？)
- "Turn question 6 into third person for your report." Write and hold up. (他们/她 下午几点放学 → 他们下午三点半放学。)
- "What's the pattern connecting 早饭/午饭/晚饭?" — cold call (time word + 饭)

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Partner Interview (Ex9) — We Do → You Do (10 min)
In pairs, Student A asks all 8 questions while Student B answers; B writes their own answers in note form on paper as they go (to keep a record). Then swap roles.

- **Scaffold:** Question sheet with pinyin available throughout; students may answer in note form only, not full sentences, during the interview itself.
- **Extension:** After the standard 8 questions, fluent pairs add one follow-up question of their own (e.g. 你几点吃晚饭以后做作业？ — accept English scaffolding if needed).
- **OTR:** Every student must both ask and answer all 8 questions — no observers.

##### Activity 2: Write the Report — You Do (10 min)
Using their partner's note-form answers, students write a short report (5–6 sentences) about their partner's day, using the report-writing template and switching to 他/她.

- **Scaffold:** Sentence-starter template provided: 他/她 + [time-of-day] + [time] + [verb]。 with all 8 slots outlined; struggling students may write in the same order as the interview questions.
- **Extension:** Fluent students combine some sentences using time-of-day words to reduce repetition (e.g. 他早上七点起床，七点半吃早饭 in one flowing sentence) and add one sentence about a sport/activity from Lesson 6.
- **OTR:** Pair-check — partner reads the report about themselves and confirms every detail is correct before handing in.

##### Activity 3: Ex10 Listen-and-Tick (CD T54) — You Do (8 min)
Students complete the listen-and-tick worksheet: 8 items, each requiring a tick for both the correct time AND the correct activity heard. Play the track pausing after each item on the first pass, then once more straight through for self-check.

- **Scaffold:** Students who need more support get a version with the activity already given, only needing to tick the time.
- **Extension:** Fluent students write the full sentence heard for at least 4 of the 8 items.
- **OTR:** Thumbs up/down check after every 2 items.

##### Game: Report-Back Round Robin (5 min)
In groups of 4, each student reads ONE sentence from their written report aloud (not the whole thing) — the group must guess whose classmate is being described if the writer doesn't say the name, based only on the routine details.

- **Setup needed:** None — completed reports from Activity 2 are reused.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read aloud; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On paper: "Which of the 8 questions can you now ask without checking the sheet?"
- **Exit ticket:** "Write one sentence from your partner's report using 他 or 她." Collect on the way out.
- **Preview:** "Next lesson: a full cumulative review — you'll put together everything from this whole unit for one big dialogue, plus get ready for Test 4.2."
