## Lesson 8: Cumulative Review + Test 4.2 Preparation

**Option: B — Writing-focused**

### Learning Intention
We are learning to combine everything from this unit — time, time-of-day, routine, and activities — into one cumulative dialogue, and prepare for Test 4.2.

### Success Criteria
- I can write a full dialogue covering time, routine, and activities (写一段综合对话)
- I can translate daily-routine sentences between English and Chinese (中英日常起居句子互译)
- I can recall vocabulary within a category without hesitation (快速说出同类词语)

### Materials Needed
- Projector/TV
- Ex11 dialogue-building worksheet (prompts covering the full unit) — one per student
- Test 4.2-style translation practice sheet (5 sentences) — one per student
- A4 paper

---

### Lesson Sequence

#### Review (0–8 min)

**What:** Everything from Lessons 1–7 — time-of-day words, routine verbs, questions, sports/activities.

**How:** Rapid written round — teacher gives 6 prompts, students write the Chinese for each on paper, hold up after each:
1. "morning (before noon)" (上午) 2. "get up" (起床) 3. "What time do you go to bed?" (你几点睡觉？) 4. "play basketball" (打篮球) 5. "classes are over" (放学) 6. "I do homework at 8pm" (我晚上八点做作业。)

**Connection:** "This whole unit has been building toward today — one big review, and getting you fully ready for Test 4.2."

#### Learning Intention & Success Criteria (8–10 min)
Project and read aloud. Students echo each criterion once.

#### I Do (10–20 min)

##### Cumulative Dialogue Model (Ex11, p.85)
Model a full dialogue on the projector combining prior-unit greetings/introductions with this unit's time and routine language:

```
A: 早上好！你叫什么名字？
B: 我叫王小明。
A: 你早上几点起床？
B: 我早上六点半起床，六点三刻吃早饭。
A: 你几点去上学？
B: 我七点一刻去上学，坐校车。
A: 你们下午几点放学？
B: 我们下午三点半放学。放学以后，我打篮球。
A: 你晚上几点睡觉？
B: 我晚上九点半睡觉。
```

Point out: this dialogue strings together greeting → routine → activity → bedtime, using almost every pattern from the unit.

##### Test 4.2 Translation Focus
Review the target vocabulary and translation style directly from the test:

| 汉字 | 拼音 | English |
|---|---|---|
| 早 | zǎo | early |
| 晚 | wǎn | late; evening |
| 早上 | zǎoshàng | early morning |
| 晚上 | wǎnshàng | evening |
| 上午 | shàngwǔ | morning (before noon) |
| 中午 | zhōngwǔ | midday |
| 下午 | xiàwǔ | afternoon |
| 看书 | kànshū | read books |
| 睡觉 | shuìjiào | sleep |
| 游泳 | yóuyǒng | swim |
| 打篮球 | dǎ lánqiú | play basketball |
| 做作业 | zuò zuòyè | do homework |

Model one worked translation: "I sleep at 9:30 pm." → think-aloud: "time first or last? In Chinese, time comes before the verb: 我晚上九点半睡觉。"

##### CFU Checkpoints
- "Translate: 'I read books in the evening.'" Write on paper, hold up. (我晚上看书。)
- "What's wrong with 我打篮球下午?" — cold call (word order — time must come before the verb: 我下午打篮球。)
- "Translate: 'I do homework every day.'" (我每天做作业。)

#### Flexible Practice (20–43 min)

##### Activity 1: Guided Dialogue Building — We Do (8 min)
Using the Ex11 worksheet (prompts for each line, some blanked), students complete a dialogue similar to the model, filling in their own real times and one activity of their choice, checking each line with a partner before continuing.

- **Scaffold:** Full sentence frames provided for each line; word bank of routine verbs and sports/activities at the bottom of the sheet.
- **Extension:** Fluent students add 2 extra lines of their own (e.g. asking about lunch, or a second activity).
- **OTR:** Turn-and-talk after each line before moving to the next.

##### Activity 2: Test 4.2 Translation Practice — You Do (10 min)
Students complete the 5 target-style translations independently, then self-mark against a projected answer key:
1. "I read books in the evening." → 我晚上看书。
2. "I play basketball in the afternoon." → 我下午打篮球。
3. "I do homework every day." → 我每天做作业。
4. "I sleep at 9:30 pm." → 我晚上九点半睡觉。
5. "I swim in the morning." → 我早上游泳。

- **Scaffold:** Students who need support get the Chinese word order already laid out as slots (time-of-day / time / verb) to fill rather than a blank line.
- **Extension:** Fluent students write one additional sentence of their own in the same style and translate it.
- **OTR:** Cold call 5 students, one sentence each, before revealing the answer key.

##### Activity 3: Write Your Own Cumulative Dialogue — You Do (5 min)
In pairs, students write a short original dialogue (4–6 lines) combining a greeting, a routine question, and an activity — different from the model, using their own real information.

- **Scaffold:** Reuse the Ex11 worksheet's sentence frames as a backbone.
- **Extension:** Add a question about lunch or dinner (午饭/晚饭) not included in the model dialogue.
- **OTR:** Each pair reads their finished dialogue aloud to a neighbouring pair.

##### Game: Word Category Chain (Ex12, p.85) (5 min)
Whole class in a circle or rows. Teacher names a category from across the whole year so far (e.g. 时间词, 日常活动, 运动, 家庭成员, 颜色). Going student to student, each must add a new word in that category within 5 seconds; a student who repeats a word or freezes is out for that round. Play 3–4 rounds with different categories.

- **Setup needed:** None — verbal game only.

#### Plenary (43–50 min)
- **Revisit Success Criteria:** Read aloud; students show 👍/😐/👎.
- **Self-assessment:** On paper: "Write your strongest area from this unit, and one thing you'll review before the test."
- **Exit ticket:** "Translate: 'I get up at 7am and go to school by school bus.'" Collect on the way out. (我早上七点起床，坐校车上学。) — this is your #1 diagnostic before Test 4.2.
- **Preview:** "Test 4.2 next lesson. After that, we start Lesson 12 — Transport — building on how you get to school."

### Key Diagnostics (reinforce across lessons)
1. **早上 vs 上午** — both mean "morning" in English but cover different ranges (早上 5:00–8:00 only; 上午 up to noon) — flagged in Lesson 1, retested in Lesson 8's exit ticket
2. **Word order** — time-of-day + time + verb, NEVER verb-then-time (我下午打篮球, not 我打篮球下午) — flagged in Lessons 5, 6, 8
3. **上学 vs 上课** — 上学 is attending school generally; 上课 is attending a specific class — flagged in Lesson 4
4. **几点 used once per question** — flagged in Lesson 5's error-correction game
5. **早饭/午饭/晚饭 pattern** — time word + 饭 — flagged in Lesson 7 before the partner interview
6. **6 Radicals** — 穴, 矢, 页, 扌(才), 艹, 走 — taught in Lesson 3; 起 (Lesson 4 vocab) reinforces 走 as a real example

### No Mini Whiteboards / No Devices Adaptation
This class has projector access only — no mini whiteboards, no 1:1 devices. All activities across the sequence use: paper + hold up (in place of whiteboards), choral response, cold call, turn-and-talk, thumbs up/down, printed cards for games (Bingo, relay, Sentence Jumble) instead of Kahoot/Quizlet. Written responses on paper double as informal exit tickets where useful.
